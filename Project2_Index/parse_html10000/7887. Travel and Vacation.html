From international travel to ocean cruises, the UCSB Alumni Association has helped alumni explore travel and recreational interests. Gauchos can explore cultures and gain new perspectives from behind-the-scenes guides on Gaucho Getaways alumni tours, or discover a world-class city from the convenience of Club Quarters properties. Online travel resources can help alumni make plans to see the world. The Family Vacation Center and Summer Inn offer alumni a fun-filled opportunity to spend summer together with their families, where current UC Santa Barbara students serve as camp counselors. .

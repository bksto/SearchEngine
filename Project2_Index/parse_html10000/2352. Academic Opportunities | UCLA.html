From UCLA's Academic Advancement Program to its Information Literacy Program, students can leverage every resource on campus to reach their full potential. The campus has a wide scope of innovative academic opportunities, including internships and research and experiential learning opportunities that help bridge theory and practical application. Study abroad and summer travel programs offer students a global perspective. And concurrent and articulated degree programs provide a streamlined curriculum so that graduate students can earn two degrees in less time than it would take to earn them sequentially. For every interest, academic goal and career aspiration, UCLA students receive the most valuable asset in life — academic opportunity.

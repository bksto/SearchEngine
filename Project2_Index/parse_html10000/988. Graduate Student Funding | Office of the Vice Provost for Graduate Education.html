VPGE 2013 The cost of graduate study at Stanford — and the resources to support graduate students — vary by school, degree, and enrollment status. Students are encouraged to plan ahead and anticipate their financial needs to complete their degree. Campus Offices and Resources Your Department or Program Many funding policies and resources are local, so seek out your student services staff for help or advice first. Financial Aid Office The main Financial Aid Office (FAO) offers a broad overview of graduate student funding and generates a sample graduate student budget (or estimated “cost of attendance”). The FAO also administers loans and manages the Emergency Grant in Aid Process.

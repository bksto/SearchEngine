A Bauer degree has earned national recognition. You'll learn alongside top-notch faculty and motivated students at a school that ranks fifth among public schools for producing CEOs of S&P 500 companies. Bauer is also one of only 412 schools in the nation to be accredited by AACSB International, the leading business school accreditation body. There's more to brag about: Bauer is the only comprehensive business school in Houston that offers BBA, MBA, Ph.D and MS (accountancy and finance) degrees. Bauer is #1 in the Gulf Coast for published research and in the Top 5 percent nationwide. Our graduates consistently exceed the national average on the CPA exam pass rate.

Offices & Directories University of Redlands > Offices & Directories > SLIC (Student Leadership & Involvement Center) SLIC (Student Leadership & Involvement Center) The Student Leadership & Involvement Center (SLIC) strives to promote involvement by providing our students with guidance, resources, and the opportunities necessary to gain hands-on experiences that will be useful long after they have left the University of Redlands. SLIC believes that everyone has the opportunity to discover the leader within and intends to bring out the best in each and every individual student. SLIC is committed to excellence, integrity, professionalism, creativity and, above all else, fun! SLIC houses: Director of Student Leadership and Involvement Director of Greek Life Director of Recreation Assistant Director of Student Activities Offices of ASUR Student Government Club and Organization Work Room Working with Residence Life, Community Service Learning, Athletics, Faculty, the Student Development Center, Student Life, Clubs and Organizations, and numerous other areas on campus, SLIC provides opportunities for students to get involved and learn. SLIC is located on the second floor of the Hunsaker University Center. Contact information: (909)-748-8100 Address: Student Leadership and Involvement Center 1200 E.

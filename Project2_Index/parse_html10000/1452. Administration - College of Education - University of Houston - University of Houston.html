The dean's office provides leadership for all of the programs in the College and for the activities of faculty, staff, and students. The dean and his office staff have general academic responsibility for the degree and certificate programs in the college and for fiscal accountability and resource development. The Office of the Dean is a hub for communications among the college's constituents, and it is responsible for the full realization and rejuvenation of the college's motto - Collaboration for Learning and Leading.

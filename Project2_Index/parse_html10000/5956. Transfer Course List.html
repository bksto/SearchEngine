Completion of all the requirements of the CSUGE (California State University General Education Pattern) will permit students to transfer to a CSU campus without the need to take additional lower-division general education courses. Taking the courses on the Transfer Major Sheets for your major for a university of your choice or following the courses listed at ASSIST (an online student-transfer information system) will permit students to enter the major in the university without the need to repeat lower-division courses in the major.

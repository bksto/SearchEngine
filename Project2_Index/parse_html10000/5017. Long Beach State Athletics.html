Long Beach State Closes Regular Season At UC Riverside 3/6/2015 Long Beach State closes out the regular season with a trip to UC Riverside Saturday at 5 p.m. The 49ers are locked into the (4) seed at the Big West Tournament, and will open play at 2:30 p.m. next Thursday.

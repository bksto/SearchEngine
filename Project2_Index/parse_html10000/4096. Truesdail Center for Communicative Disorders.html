Centers of Distinction University of Redlands > Centers of Distinction > Truesdail Center for Communicative Disorders Truesdail Center for Communicative Disorders Service has been one of the central tenets guiding our curriculum at Redlands since our founders first broke ground on campus in 1907. As part of that commitment, we pride ourselves on the close relationship we share with the region in which we reside. One shining example, our Truesdail Center for Communicative Disorders, is one of the five Centers of Distinction that set us apart in California and nationally as an innovative liberal arts institution. We opened the Truesdail Center to offer our graduate students in the Department of Communicative Disorders a place to work directly with clients while also servicing our community.

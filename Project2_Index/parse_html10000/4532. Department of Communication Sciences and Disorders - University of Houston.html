The Department is in the College of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences, and it is home to approximately 26 faculty and staff. The Department's mission is to advance the understanding of normal and disordered speech, language and hearing through teaching, scholarship and service to the community. We train graduate students to provide clinical services in speech-language pathology, provide service to the community, and conduct research on various areas of communication sciences and disorders.

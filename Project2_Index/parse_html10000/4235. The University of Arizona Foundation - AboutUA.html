Our world-class institution is committed to developing a diverse and motivated community of students, faculty and staff, all of whom are driven toward discovery and innovation. University students may opt to study from a diverse select of more than 150 undergraduate degree programs and 200 graduate degree programs. At the end of the day, the goal of the institution is to create the leaders of tomorrow within each of our program areas. About The University of Arizona Alumni Association: The University of Arizona Alumni Association exists to serve the special interests of current and former UA students through building and maintaining relationships, promoting service, and focusing support for the campus community. The Alumni Association connects alumni from all generations and geographic locations through clubs, councils and other special interest groups. Its purpose is to keep alumni engaged with the University and its unique needs.

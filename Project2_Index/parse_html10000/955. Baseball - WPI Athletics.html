[read more] 49 Spring Student-Athletes Honored As NEWMAC All-Academic Standouts April 29, 2014 Boston, MA --- WPI saw 49 student-athletes across five spring sports collect NEWMAC All-Academic honors, as announced by the conference office. WPI had three out their five teams reach double-digit selections led by Men’s Track and Field with 15, followed by Women’s Track and Field with 13 and Baseball with 12. With the 49 selections, WPI ranks second in the 10-member conference behind only MIT, which compiled 52. [read more] Five From Baseball Selected to Worcester Area All-Star Team May 15, 2014 Worcester, MA --- The fourth annual Worcester Area Baseball All-Star team has been announced after a vote by sports information directors representing all ten Worcester area colleges and universities. WPI had five players named to the team, including sophomore Sean Greene and freshman Dan Bettigole to the first team and senior Sean Kelly, junior Taylor Landry and sophomore Vinny D’Ambrosio to the second team. [read more] Greene and Landry Net NEWMAC Baseball Postseason Honors May 7, 2014 Boston, MA --- Sophomore Sean Greene and junior Taylor Landry have earned all-NEWMAC accolades as voted upon by the league's head coaches at the conclusion of the regular season. Greene was selected to the conference’s first team while Landry was named to second team. The Engineers qualified for the NEWMAC Tournament in 2014 and won its final two non-conference games last week to finish the season with a 18-15 record.

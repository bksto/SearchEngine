Benefits of having an AS Card Every student enrolled at Crafton Hills College who chooses to purchase an AS Card is entitled to the following benefits: Advocacy for students at the local, state, and national level. Automatic entry into Student Senate drawings. 5% discount on all purchases at the Crafton Hills College Bookstore, includes clothing, food, electronics, etc. Discounted movie tickets, and other entertainment items. Discounted admission to campus events. An opportunity to win a free cap and gown rental for commencement. Discounted prices on commencement sashes. Free food and giveaways at Student Senate sponsored Student Appreciation days. Potential to earn an Associated Students Scholarship of over $300. Opportunity to win a free year long parking permit, if you purchase your AS card by August 1st. All these things are just a few examples of your money at work, when you purchase an AS Card for only $9.00 per semester!

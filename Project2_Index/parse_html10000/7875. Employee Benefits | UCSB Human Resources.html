Managers and supervisors play an important role in helping employees to navigate the world of University benefits. Managers and supervisors should be available to assist employees in understanding the benefits programs and identifying when an action needs to be taken in relation to a personal change or life event, e.g., marriage, birth, adoption, etc. Many of life’s events can have an important impact on employee benefits. Family Changes Benefits Checklist provides employees with details about which benefits are impacted by each life event. Employees have 31 days from most qualifying events to make changes to their UC benefits.

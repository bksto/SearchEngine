Online learning may be new to many of you.  And you need to know up front that learning online is something that is learned.  So this is a quick orientation that will help you "learn to learn online." Please click on the link below.  That will take you to SBVC's Blackboard login page. Enter "sbvconline" for the username (no quotation marks, of course).  Then enter "student" for the password (no quotation marks, of course).  Click "Login". Then you will be in Blackboard where you should be able to see the course "Learn to Learn Online."  Click that link and you will be taken to the orientation to online learning. Work through all the links on the "Course Menu" in that class (the "Course Menu" is the list of buttons on the left side of the screen) and you will see those elements of online learning that make students successful in the online world. Click this link to enter the orientation to online learning. https://sbccd.blackboard.com/ When you are finished, be sure to click the "Logout" button in the top, middle portion of the window. Have fun "learning to learn online!!!"

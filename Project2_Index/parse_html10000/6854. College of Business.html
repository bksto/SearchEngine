Learn More » ASU Home » Colleges » College of Business The recent economic challenges have highlighted the need for a new generation of intelligent, well-trained, ethical business leaders. Here at ASU, we are providing students with the type of world-class education they will need to meet these challenges and be successful in the global economy. Our curriculum is continuously evolving to help our students stay relevant and reach their full potential. Making a Global Difference   The College of Business is proud of its international reputation, as well as the individual attention our faculty gives students who hail from around the world. Our alumni move on to become respected corporate leaders and entrepreneurs who are making their mark on the global marketplace. Accredited by AACSB Arkansas State University is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business International, which represents the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide. Fewer than 20% of the business schools in the world achieve AACSB accreditation and it is the hallmark of excellence in business education.

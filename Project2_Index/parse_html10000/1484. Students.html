The UCSB Alumni Association is passionate about creating connections between students and alumni. We are also committed to providing programs that allow students to gain business, professional, and leadership skills. From networking events to scholarship opportunities, your Gaucho family at Mosher Alumni House is here to help! Meet Alumni in Your Desired Field "Alumni in" Panels Student and Alumni Industry Networking Series Grow your Gaucho network during these quarterly nights of informal networking with a panel of alumni speakers. Receive tips and advice about various industries, and see if this career path is right for you. Following the panel discussion, attendees can ask questions and meet panelists. Check back for panel events for the upcoming quarters.

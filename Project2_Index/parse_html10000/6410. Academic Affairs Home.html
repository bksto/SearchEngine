Under the leadership of the Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs, the Division’s primary responsibilities include: delivering instruction; developing academic programs; providing library and technological support services to students and the campus community; promoting opportunities for faculty, in their roles as teacher-scholars, to engage in research and creative activities; facilitating international exchange opportunities for students and faculty; and, offering programs for life-long learners. As you navigate the site, you will find links to the colleges and units which comprise Academic Affairs as well as information and resources that may be of interest to you.

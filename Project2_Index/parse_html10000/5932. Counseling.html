As a first time student at SBVC, you will have to complete SBVC online application, the online orientation and the Basic Skills assessment test before seeing a counselor: To schedule a same-day appointment, please come to the Counseling Center or call the Center at 909-384-4404. The appointments are given on a first-come, first-served basis. For an appointment to develop a two-year education plan, the recommended months are September and October for the fall semester, and February through mid-March for the spring semester. Mission Statement The Counseling Department is committed to providing high quality counseling services which promote the development and empowerment of a diverse community of learners.

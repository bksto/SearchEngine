Search Schedule of Classes You can get up to the minute, detailed information on course offerings by using MyCSULB. Once you sign-in select the “Search for Classes” option under “My Menu”. This option allows you to search by subject area, course, day or time, instructor, GE area, courses with available seats and much more! For assistance using MyCSULB, check out 'Using MyCSULB.' Click on “Search for Classes” under “My Menu.” Click on “Browse Catalog” and “Go.” For assistance using MyCSULB, check out “Using MyCSULB.” How to Register Students admitted to or continuing in a degree, credential or certificate program register via MyCSULB. Once you have signed on using your personal account, select “Student Center / Academics” under “My Menu.” For assistance using MyCSULB, check out “Using MyCSULB.” For additional information on procedures, eligibility and fees, review the Registration web pages. Other students may be able to enroll in CSULB courses on a space available basis through the College of Continuing and Professional Education's Open University Program.

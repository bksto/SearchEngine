T. Bauer College of Business. We are Houston’s premier business college, with five nationally recognized undergraduate academic departments and outstanding research and graduate programs. Bauer College prepares its students for success in a global economy. We are a pacesetter and Houston’s only fully accredited business school offering BBA, MBA, MS ACCY and Ph.D. programs. A world-renowned faculty, nationally recognized centers and institutes and exceptional students from nearly 70 countries combine to create Houston’s most comprehensive and dynamic business school. Bauer is located on the southeast corner of the University of Houston campus in Melcher Hall. With the latest stock updates flashing on the neon ticker in the first floor AIM Center lobby and multiple flat screen TVs, there’s a corporate ambiance not always found on college campuses. Bauer offers WiFi access throughout the building and there’s even a Starbucks in the main student hub on the second floor. An EMBA lobby on the third floor leads to tiered graduate-style classrooms, breakout rooms and the EMBA café. Bauer, one of the most diverse business schools in the country, has an international and global feel. Come visit us soon, and see what the buzz is all about!

Sharing Your Interests With Your Fellow Students Getting involved, meeting new people and participating in new activities is a major part of the college experience. UC Riverside has over 300 student organizations that you can participate in, including cultural clubs, student government, recreational, political and service-based groups. Associated Students UCR's Associated Students (ASUCR) is the student government body. Our student leaders serve on campus committees that develop and govern policies related to funding, academics, technology, new construction, student life and other important issues. See how you can get involved! Graduate Student Association The Graduate Student Association (GSAUCR) works in the general interest of the graduate student body at UCR. All graduate students can become members of GSA. Find out more about the GSA. Community Organization Portal Explore the 300 plus clubs and organizations on campus with Community, a database that allows you to search, gain information and express interest in the groups you want to join.

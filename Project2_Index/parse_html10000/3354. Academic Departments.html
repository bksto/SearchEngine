Bauer College of Business offers students the opportunity to learn from five academic departments that have been ranked among the top in business fields of study across the country by numerous publications. Bauer faculty members have been nationally recognized for their extensive experience and lend their knowledge to students in the classroom. Whether it’s Accountancy & Taxation, Decision & Information Sciences, Finance, Management or Marketing & Entrepreneurship, Bauer students and faculty are passionate about their subject matter and engaged in the real world of business. OFFICE OF GRADUATE & PROFESSIONAL PROGRAMS Powerful Choice. Houston's MBA. DEPARTMENT OF ACCOUNTANCY & TAXATION Delivering practical knowledge though innovative programs. DEPARTMENT OF DECISION & INFORMATION SCIENCES Management Information Systems and Supply Chain Management. DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE Teaching our students to succeed in the challenging world of finance. DEPARTMENT OF MANAGEMENT Developing strategic thinking, leadership skills and ethical decision-making capabilities. DEPARTMENT OF MARKETING & ENTREPRENEURSHIP Our mission is to make a difference. Office of Graduate & Professional Programs Bauer College of Business offers a full time day MBA program and a Professional MBA program to fulfill the needs of every type of student. GPP Department Department of Accountancy & Taxation From musicians, mathematicians and military veterans to hockey players, ballroom dancers and liberal arts majors, accounting has become a hot degree and career choice. ACCY Department Department of Decision & Information Sciences The Department of Decision and Information Sciences offers two areas of concentration: Management Information Systems, and Supply Chain Management. DISC Department Department of Finance Providing the knowledge, technical skills, and market relevance to allow our students to succeed in the challenging world of finance. FINA Department Department of Management Our management programs cover both micro and macro areas of management – strategy and organizational theory. MANA Department Department of Marketing & Entrepreneurship Bauer's Program for Excellence in Selling and our Sales Excellence Institute is a hub for sales research and education. MARK & ENTR Department Office of Undergraduate Business Programs Welcome to the C.T. Bauer College of Business! We have many opportunities such as learning from first-rate faculty, quality academic advising, career development, involvement in student organizations, and student leadership. The Bauer College of Business is committed to ensuring the support your need to be successful in your academic career and beyond.

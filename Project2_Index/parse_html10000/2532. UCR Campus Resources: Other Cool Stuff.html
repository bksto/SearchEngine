What Else Makes UCR Unique? The more you get to know UC Riverside, the more you'll see just how much there is to discover around here. Consider a tour of the College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences' famed 22-acre Citrus Variety Collection to explore the university's roots in agricultural research. Or check out our Entomology Research Museum and 28,000-square-foot Insectary and Quarantine Facility — the only one of its kind in the state. But that just scratches the surface.

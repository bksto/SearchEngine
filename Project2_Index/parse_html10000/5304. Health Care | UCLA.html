The UCLA Center for Health Policy Research is one of the nation's leaders in this field and California's premier source of health policy information. UCLA engages with the community by offering various health-enrichment programs — from weight loss to Freedom from Smoking® — making healthier lifestyles accessible to all. UCLA is also dedicated to educating the public about complex mental behaviors at the Neuropsychiatric and Behavioral Health Services, as well as offering low-fee testing and therapy at the UCLA Psychology Clinic. Ultimately, UCLA works to improve available medical resources and information, and continues to provide exceptional medical care to anyone who needs it.

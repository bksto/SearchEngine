Important Dates for May 2015 Graduation Undergraduate Online Intent to Graduate Form Due: Friday, January 16 (Friday after classes begin) When the online intent form is completed, an automatic email is sent to your campus email account, notifying you of the receipt and directing you to the Undergraduate Graduation Tracker. If you HAVE NOT filled out the intent form, please do so IMMEDIATELY, as you will not be included in any reporting if not coded. If an intent form has not been submitted by January 16, it will need to be submitted by the advisor, attached to the checksheet. Intent forms submitted late may result in the graduate’s name not appearing in the Commencement Program. In addition, the diploma and final transcript will also be delayed.  For additional information on the online intent to graduate form, please click here. If you are having difficulty submitting the intent form, please contact the Office of Admissions, Records and Registration at (870) 972-2031 or by email at graduation@astate.edu. Any student that files an Intent to Graduate Application but DOES NOT graduate during that same semester will be required to pay the graduation fee when filing the application again. Undergraduate Checksheets Due: Friday, January 23 Once the intent form is completed, your advisor will be informed that your checksheet is needed. These are the responsibility of the advisor, and must come directly from the department. Students SHOULD NOT submit checksheets to our office. Undergraduate Graduation Fee This fee is required of ALL graduating students, even if you do not participate in the ceremony. The $45 fee covers the processing of the paperwork, the printing of the Commencement Program, the Diploma and cover, as well as the attire. This fee will be processed once the online intent form is completed. For additional information on the online intent to graduate form, please click here.  Online Undergraduate Intent Form The Undergraduate online intent form can be submitted through Self Service. Instructions for the process can be found here. Graduation Tracker for Undergraduate Students Please visit the ASU myCampus portal to access the graduation tracker. The graduation tracker provides personal data on your graduation status. Visit the tracker today to see your information.  Students will have access to the tracker after their intent card is coded in the system.  Once the intent card is coded, students will receive an automated email acknowledging receipt.

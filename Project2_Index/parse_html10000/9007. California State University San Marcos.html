Programs FAFSA CSUSM Scholarships Considering Student Loans Complete the Loan Process Academic Requirements Additional Resources Education Pays fgj Financial Aid Basics Financial aid includes federal, state, institutional, and private fund(s) used to assist eligible students in funding their education. Financial aid can be a combination of scholarships, grants, loans, waivers, and student employment. Scholarships and grants are considered “free money” and the student is not required to repay; however, loans and student employment are considered self-help aid and most loans require repayment when a student becomes enrolled less than half time or graduates.

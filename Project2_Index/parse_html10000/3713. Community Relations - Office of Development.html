Ways to Give Other Links Points of Pride DevNet Office of Development COMMUNITY RELATIONS UC Santa Barbara strives to link campus and community by building relationships between UCSB and the greater Santa Barbara area communities. Our network of Community Support Groups provides a variety of opportunities for community members to become acquainted with UCSB. Each group offers an array of activities for members to access and support UCSB's educational and cultural resources. These outreach activities are listed on our Upcoming Events page, and the Campus Calendar of Events. Visit the group websites below or contact the Office of Community Relations for additional information.

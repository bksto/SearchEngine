To this end, the Institute is focused on encouraging the research community in Marketing to focus on issues affecting health care delivery and consumption. Our goal is to spark and sustain research in health care marketing that achieves the twin objective of economic vitality of the economic vitality of the health care industry and the physical and mental health of its customers.

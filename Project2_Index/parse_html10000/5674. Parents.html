We Ensure Your Student’s Safety Secure residence halls feature video surveillance, restricted card access and accessible staff. Our on-campus police department patrols campus and housing. Your student can opt-in to our sophisticated emergency notification system that sends text messages instantly. Award-winning Campus Safety Escorts are available to walk students across campus at night. See more safety information for parents. We Support Your Student’s Health Our Campus Health Center offers everything from walk-up and urgent care to dental and optical. With the UC Student Health Insurance Plan, doctor visits are free. Our unique safety and wellness programs are a perfect example of how we support your student’s physical, mental and emotional health.

For Good. Did you know that one of the nation's premier medical centers is located right here in Chicago -- on the west side of UIC's campus? It's the University of Illinois Hospital & Health Sciences System. This institution features a 491-bed hospital, specialty clinics, the College of Medicine and five additional health science colleges (Applied Health Sciences, Dentistry, Pharmacy, Public Health and Nursing). The Hospital is a leader in patient care, research and education, and boasts internationally renowned physicians and surgeons. The hospital is considered among the nation's best in treating diabetes and endocrine disorders. For more information, visit the University of Illinois Hospital & Health Sciences System website.

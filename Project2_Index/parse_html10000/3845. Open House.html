Gaucho Transfer Fridays Transfer students are welcome at Spring Insight, which features special sessions specifically focused on transfer student. They may also visit campus for Gaucho Transfer Fridays, which are tailored specifically for admitted transfer students. These events allow transfer students and their families to visit UCSB, tour the campus, speak with advisors and explore the academic and extracurricular opportunities available at UCSB.

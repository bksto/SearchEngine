FIND PHOTOS ON UCSB'S FLICKR What better way to stay connected with fellow alumni than through reunions and networking events? Each spring, the Alumni Association’s annual All Gaucho Reunion continues to grow in popularity, offering a weekend full of activities, events and opportunities to return to UCSB and visit with former classmates and professors. If you’re interested in organizing other reunions, contact John Lofthus at john.lofthus@ia.ucsb.edu and in the meantime be sure to stay connected year round through our online community. For information on the All Gaucho Reunion, held the fourth weekend of every April, click here: http://www.allgauchoreunion.com/ .

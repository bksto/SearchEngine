For more information, visit http://www.extension.ucr.edu/. Custom Training and Group Programs: UC Riverside is recognized as a leader in international education, in the U.S. and around the world. Our Custom Training and Group Programs service is designed for businesses, educational institutions, governments and industries that are upgrading technologies, training new staff, improving services, or developing management techniques. Subject areas include but are not limited to: Information technology Executive and corporate training Business management development Human resource development and planning Environmental management Forensic investigations Geospatial technologies Certificate Programs: A certificate is a non-degree program designed to provide specialized knowledge. A certificate from UCR Extension demonstrates that a participant has completed a planned, sequential program of study in a particular field of knowledge and that course of study.

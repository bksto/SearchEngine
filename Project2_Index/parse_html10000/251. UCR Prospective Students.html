Choosing where you’ll attend college is one of the most important decisions you’ll ever make, and making a good decision requires good information. No matter who you are or what your goals are, this is the place to start. Undergraduates UC Riverside combines the programs and prestige of a large, world-class university with the intimacy and personal contact of a much smaller school. Our Undergraduate Admissions office can help you with everything from admissions requirements to student-led campus tours and important deadlines. Check out our Undergraduate Admissions website and create an account today!

University Communications at Bradley believes that restrictions on news media personnel should be limited. However, respect for the privacy of our students, faculty, staff, and visitors require some rules regarding access. At the University’s discretion, journalists may report from the campus on news of public interest. Expectations: Media representatives are expected to observe parking and other regulations. Access to all residence halls is strictly prohibited without prior permission of University Communications. Entry to classrooms, offices and other University property by news media must be arranged in advance with University Communications.  University Communications staff will gain permission of occupants of the respective offices or professors in charge of a class. The following activities are restricted and require permission. Please contact University Communications for the following: Still and video photography on campus from which images will be used for commercial profit or distribution, or any use other than reporting for an accredited news media outlet. The campus may not be used for advocacy of political positions or candidacies. Emergency situations may require that these regulations and policies be suspended or altered.

The Alumni & Visitors Center serves as the "front door" to the campus - a distinctive arrival point that showcases alumni achievements while welcoming friends and visitors. The 13,865-square-foot, split-level building designed by local architects HMC, houses a large lobby, meeting rooms, a library of works by UCR authors, and a formal board room. The facility is available for special events, meetings and receptions. Are you an alumni artist or author? If you are an alumnus who would either like to have your art displayed or a book reading in the Goldware Family Library at the Alumni & Visitors Center please contact us at (951) 827-2586 or ucralum@ucr.edu.   Request more information & take a virtual tour Visit www.conferences.ucr.edu or contact Conference, Events, & Catering Services at (951) 827-5471 to reserve space or request more information. You can also take a virtual tour of the Alumni & Visitors Center. Legacy Brick Program For a tax-deductible gift of $500, you can purchase a 4x8 inch brick inscribed with a three-line, 20 character name or message of your choice that will be displayed in the entrance courtyard of the Alumni & Visitors Center.

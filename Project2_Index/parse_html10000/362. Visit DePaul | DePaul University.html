Each session includes information about academics, student life and the admission process. Following your session, you have the option to take a campus tour — to give you a better feel for what campus life is like. Undergraduate Transfer Admission Information Sessions and Campus Tours We offer transfer admission information sessions at our Lincoln Park campus. Each session includes information about academic programs, the transfer admission process, credit transfer and scholarship opportunities. Following your information session, you’ll have the opportunity to tour the Lincoln Park campus.  We also offer campus tours of our downtown (Loop) campus.  By touring either of our campus locations, you’ll get a feel of what campus life is like. Admission Counseling Appointments Prospective students can schedule an appointment with an admission counselor to discuss your academic plans and review your previous coursework and transfer credits. Schedule an appointment online. Note: Admitted students can visit our Admitted Student page for more information on their next steps to enrolling at DePaul and when they will meet with their academic advising office to register for classes. Accelerated Transfer Admission (ATA) Days Meet with an admission counselor who will review your application and give you an admission decision by the end of your appointment.  You must bring official transcripts in a sealed envelope from all colleges or universities you have attended. If you have less than 30 semester (44 quarter) hours of transferable credit, you must also bring your high school transcript and ACT/SAT scores. Learn more about ATA days. Schedule your appointment gaq['siloTracker._trackEvent', 'External Link - ATA Days Online - Undergraduate Transfer', this.href]online or call (312) 362-8300. Adult Undergraduate Admission Information Sessions and Campus Tours We offer Adult Undergraduate admission information sessions at the downtown (Loop), O’Hare, Naperville and Oak Forest campuses, as well as online. Each session includes information about academic programs, the admission process, credit transfer and earning credit for life experience. We also offer many events specifically for adult undergraduate students. Schedule your campus tour and register for information sessions and events​ online. You may schedule visits on multiple campuses.

Young Grand Salon, Kerckhoff Hall The 2014 Atmospheric & Oceanic Sciences Graduation Celebration will be held on Saturday, June 14 at 10:30AM in the Charles E. Young Grand Salon Room in Kerckoff Hall. All graduates should check in with Carol Yasutomi Fujino, no later than 5 minutes before the ceremony begins at 10:30AM sharp. All graduates can choose to sit with their fellow graduating classmates or with their families and friends. This is always a very pleasant and memorable event and a nice way to bring closure to your academic experience at UCLA. After the ceremony, which lasts about one hour and half, you and your guests are all invited to stay for a reception in the Grand Salon Room where you can: say goodbye to your fellow graduates; introduce your family to your friends and the faculty; and take pictures. Please note that tickets for this must be ordered through your MyUCLA and picked up at the Atmospheric & Oceanic Sciences Department Office. Per Commencement rule, the first 4 tickets are free and any ticket after that costs $12.00. No refund is given so make sure you have a proper count. Please go to the UCLA Commencement site for ALL you need to know about Commencement events and procedures. DAY OF CEREMONY All students, please check in my with no later than 5 minutes before the start of the ceremony (10:30am sharp). Please arrive at commencement venue no later than 10:15 a.m. Ceremony will last approximately 90 minutes. Entry to the Grand Salon is from the SOUTH SIDE, facing Math Sciences. PARKING AND DIRECTIONS Parking for the Commencement will be available in parking structures 2, 4, 6, 7, 8 and 9. Valid (not expired) student, faculty and staff permits will be valid on Commencement Day. There is a $12.00 fee (subject to change) per vehicle for those who do not have a student, faculty or staff parking permit. You can also use MyUCLA to buy pre-paid parking permits for your guests when you place your order for tickets to the Commencement ceremony. The fees for pre-paid parking permits are assessed when permits are ordered and are automatically billed through your BAR statement. No refunds are possible. Students are charged for parking permits whether or not they are picked up. PROGRAM The Commencement printed program will list the names of students whose degrees were conferred for Summer 2013, Fall 2013, Winter 2014; and candidates for Spring 2014, Summer 2014 and Fall 2014 unless they have a privacy restriction. If you want to release your privacy restriction and have your name included in the printed program you must remove the privacy restriction in MyUCLA.

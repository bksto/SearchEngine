Your student’s tuition & fees and room & board charges (if they reside in one of Bradley’s residence halls) can be paid online. For instruction go to Student Financial Services. When do financial assistance awards get mail? While awards for students new to Bradley are prepared in March/April (or November for mid-year starts), continuing student financial assistance is prepared during May and June and distributed in late June. Award notifications are not mailed to continuing students. Rather, your student will be notified via e-mail sent to their Bradley e-mail address that the award is available for viewing at SFSinteractive. Additional frequently asked questions.

It is both an Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3) organization, and an Internal Revenue Code Section 115 organization. For state income tax purposes, the University of California and its affiliated organizations are considered to be part of the state of California and its instrumentalities. As such, it is not subject to income tax. Tax ID numbers There are two gift-receiving arms for UC Davis: The UC Davis Foundation and The Regents of the University of California. Both are qualified charities that provide donors with tax benefits to the full extent of the law. UC Davis Foundation tax ID number: 94-6081352. UC Regents tax ID number: 94-6036494. Please call UC Davis at (530) 754-4438 to obtain the proper tax ID number for your gift.

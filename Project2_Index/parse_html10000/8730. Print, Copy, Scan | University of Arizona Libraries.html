Cost starts at $0.10 per page and can be paid with CatCa$h or credit card. Large-format and specialty printing are available at the Express Document Center, as well as self-publishing and book printing using our Espresso Book Machine. 3D Printing Create objects from digital models with our new 3D printing service. Cost is $0.10 per gram of filament used. Copying Self-service copy machines are available at each of our libraries, starting at $0.10 a page. Or go to the Express Document Center. Scanning Free, self-service scanners are available in the computer areas at each library. The Main and Science-Engineering Libraries have book scanners for large-format scans. We offer free electronic delivery of articles and book chapters from our collection for UA students and faculty. The Express Document Center can also scan items for you or digitize your collections.

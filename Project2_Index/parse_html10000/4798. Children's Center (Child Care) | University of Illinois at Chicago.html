Children must be between the age of 2 years and 9 months up to 6 years. The program is accredited by the National Association for the Education of Young Children and licensed by the Illinois Department of Children and Family Services. All children must be toilet-trained. UIC Children's Center 1919 W. Taylor St. Room 116 (M/C 525) (312) 413-5326 For more information, visit the Children's Center website.

Gifts to the Parents Fund enrich the educational experience of all UC Davis students by providing flexible funding that supports the university overall. Parents Fund donations are part of the UC Davis Annual Fund, but recognize and honor giving by parents in particular. Impacts of Giving to the Parents Fund Contact Information Eric Gifford Director of Development, Parent Giving 530-752-4903 egifford@ucdavis.edu Gifts to the Parents Fund enhance the educational experience for students in many ways, through: Undergraduate scholarships and the Chancellor’s Achievement Awards, which reward academic achievement Facility and equipment improvements, including new computer labs and updated classrooms in Wellman and Kerr Halls Chancellor’s Fellows Awards, which recognize outstanding faculty early in their careers Student and community events like Commencement and Picnic Day How to give to the Parents Fund Give online Call the Parents Fund at: (530) 754-1100 Mail your contribution to: Parents Fund University of California One Shields Avenue Davis, CA 95616-8574 Checks should be made payable to The UC Davis Foundation.

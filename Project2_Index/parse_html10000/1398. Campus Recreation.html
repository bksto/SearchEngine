Wellness Programs Wellness Programs include something for everybody!   From group fitness class to fitness challenges to special events and more there is sure to be something for you.  For more information on the current events taking place keep reading.  Intramural Sports The Intramural Sports program provides an opportunity for students, faculty, and staff to participate in more than 25 activities, within a structured and competitive environment.  Teams and/or individuals may be organized within Greek organizations, residence halls, independent groups, or other ASU associations.  Keep reading for more information on how you can get involved. Club Sports Club Sports are a great way to stay involved in a sport you love. Club Sports is designed for those students who desire to continue to compete at the collegiate level, but do not participate in NCAA sports.  The Club Sports Program help to develop student leadership skills, promote responsibility, and encourage a healthy lifestyle.

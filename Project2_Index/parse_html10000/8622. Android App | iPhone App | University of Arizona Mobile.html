FREE download for iPhone®, Android®, and available as mobile web. Classes, Maps, News, and everything you need to get where you're going. Need to see what classes are available? Or where that class is? Looking for a place to eat at 2 a.m., or just some hilarious Wildcat videos to pass the time? You guessed it, we have an app for that! Campus Map Navigate around campus by searching for classroom buildings, residence halls, and offices. The map displays the location, and you can zoom in, zoom out or scroll in any direction. You can also browse locations by building. AZ Athletics Get the latest scores and news about the Wildcats teams. Phonebook Search by first and last name for phone numbers, email addresses and other information about university students, faculty and staff. Schedule of Classes Browse available courses and course descriptions including meeting times, locations and instructor names. UAccess Student Connect to view schedules, grades, view financial aid, account balances, student notices and more. UANews Get the latest news from the University of Arizona. You can share articles using email, Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. CatTran Make your way around campus by viewing maps of shuttles and routes. Events Find out what's going on at the UA today or on any upcoming day. Share events via email, Twitter, or Facebook. Daily Wildcat This student-run newspaper serves the entire University of Arizona community. Dining Check out dining locations, hours, and menus. Social Get the latest about the UA community from Facebook and Twitter. Video Watch videos including special events, lectures, and more. Library Find hours and locations of campus libraries, use the catalog search, and view the websites of the University of Arizona libraries. Tours Guide yourself, or experience the campus remotely through audio, video or images. View detailed information about specific buildings and landmarks. Photos View photos from the University of Arizona photo albums. UA Alumni More than 260,000 UA Alumni live throughout the U.S. and in 150+ countries. The Alumni Association connects alumni, all Wildcats for Life. Emergency Info Be informed of critical notices on the campus and get easy access to key emergency contact numbers. Meal Plans Hungry Wildcats eat on campus. Save money every day with your UA meal plan. Campus Health The UA Campus Health Service promotes health, wellness and safety at the University of Arizona and in the surrounding community. UA Savvy Learn, engage, connect! UA Savvy introduces students to a wide range of deals and experiences happening on the UA campus.

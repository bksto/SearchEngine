Six Rice departments are represented in the BRC: Biochemistry and Cell Biology, Physics and Chemistry from the Wiess School of Natural Sciences, Psychology from the School of Social Sciences, and Bioengineering and Electrical and Computational Engineering from the George R. Brown School of Engineering.

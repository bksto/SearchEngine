University of Redlands > Academics > School of Education School of Education Redlands has infused its longstanding reputation and the cornerstone of Educational Justice into a variety of programs to suit today’s adult students. Whether you are already an experienced educator seeking to advance your skill set, or considering starting your second or third… or sixth new career, our School of Education offers a comprehensive set of programs. From credentials to master’s degrees to our doctorate program, from spatial literacy to special education to clinical mental health, and everything in between, we believe that we have just what you are looking for. We hope you’ll visit, and find out more about our focus on advocacy, research, policy development and professional training in education.

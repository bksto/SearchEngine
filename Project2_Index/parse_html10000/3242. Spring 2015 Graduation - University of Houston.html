Apply between February 28–March 27 for a nonrefundable fee of $50 per degree. Summer 2015 Candidates In order to participate in the Spring Commencement and Convocation, Summer graduates must apply by April 17, 2015. ﻿Participation Fall 2014 graduates will receive information on complimentary tickets for themselves and their families, and will join the University Commencement in a special honorary section with their families. Spring 2015 candidates should apply for graduation by the regular deadline of February 27 (late deadline March 27) and be approved to graduate by their Colleges. Summer 2015 candidates must apply for Summer graduation by an early deadline of April 17 in order to be considered for participation in the May University Commencement (the Summer graduation application opens April 3). Applicants will be "pre-reviewed" by their College to determine whether all remaining coursework and requirements for graduation can reasonably be completed during the Summer semester. Note that this is a pre-review process for the purpose of approval to participate in the University Commencement only; formal graduation review will still occur in the final graduating semester. Students planning to graduate in the Summer and interested in participating in Spring Commencement are encouraged to contact their academic advisors to confirm they are on a path to Summer completion. A degree is conferred once final degree requirements are satisfied, rather than by participating in the Commencement ceremony. ﻿Regalia Order online from Herff Jones from February 17–March 21. Can I participate in the ceremonies? Yes! You must apply for graduation through your myUH account by April 17, 2015 in order to participate in the ceremonies. If you apply for graduation through your myUH account by the designated date, your name will be printed in the Commencement and Convocation programs. Will my name be listed in the Commencement and Convocation programs? If you officially applied for graduation through your myUH account by the designated deadlines, your name will be printed in the Commencement and Convocation programs. Is it mandatory to participate in the Commencement and/or Convocation? We encourage all students to celebrate their hard-earned degrees at Commencement and Convocation; however, both events are optional. Will we be notified of the awards we receive before Convocation? Students will not be notified prior to Convocation of the awards they receive. Do I have to get tickets for Commencement and Convocation for my family and friends? Commencement Yes. Students must reserve tickets for their guests. Students may reserve up to 6 tickets for their family and friends. Convocation No. However, since seating is limited and fills quickly, encourage your guests to arrive early. We will have seating available in the atrium along the railings on the second and third floords, as well as the Theater (room 150) with a live broadcast of the event. *Participation in the University of Houston Commencement and/or the College of Architecture Convocation is ceremonial only. Degrees will be reviewed and conferred the weeks following graduation. Confirmed Spring graduates will receive their diplomas approximately 6-8 weeks following the May graduation ceremonies. Confirmed Summer graduates will receive their diplomas approximately 6-8 weeks following the close of the Summer term (August 14).

> Admission & Aid > Types of Admission > Graduate Student > Graduate College of Science and Health Graduate College of Science and Health Main Page Content General Admission Requirements Please read through the instructions below before filling out the online application.  Each of the graduate programs in the College of Science and Health (CSH) requires different types of supplementary application materials. In order to determine what materials are necessary to complete your application, please see the program-specific admission requirements listed below.  If you have any questions about general admission requirements for College of Science and Health graduate programs, please contact us at (773) 325-7315 or GradDePaul@depaul.edu​.  Please send all supporting application materials for College of Science and Health  programs to this address:  Office of Graduate Admission  College of Science and Health  DePaul University  2400 N. Sheffield Avenue  Chicago, IL 60614  For all CSH graduate programs, the application fee is $40.00, payable online, by personal check or money order. Checks should be payable to "DePaul University" and mailed to the address above. If you write a check, be sure to put your name on the check. When it arrives at DePaul, we will match it up with your application. You should send in your application fee at the same time you submit the application online.

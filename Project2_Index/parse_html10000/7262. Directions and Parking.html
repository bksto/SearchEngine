These three buildings are located on the corner of University Drive and Calhoun Road, UH Entrance #1. Use the map below to help guide you. Need Directions to Bauer College? From George Bush Intercontinental Airport/Houston (IAH) Follow the exit signs from the airport to I-45 South Stay on I-45 South through downtown Houston Exit Spur 5 South Turn right on University Dr. (the first stoplight) Melcher Hall is the first building on the right after the first stop light. From Hobby Airport Follow the exit signs from airport to I-45 North Stay on I-45 North Exit Elgin-Lockwood Cullen Turn Left on Elgin at the stoplight Turn Left at the (second) stoplight to Texas Spur 5 South service road Turn right on University Dr. (the first stoplight) Melcher Hall is the first building on the right after the first stop light. Parking Meters Located throughout campus, on-street meters have a maximum stay of one hour, while off-street meters have a two-hour maximum. Gated Visitor Lot Pre-arranged visitor parking may be available in Lot 1E, by UH Entrance 1. Please visit the parking booth on University Drive for further parking instructions, tokens, and permits. Visitor Parking Garage Visitor parking garage is available on the third and fourth levels at the UH Welcome Center, accessible from Calhoun Road and on the first level of the East Garage, accessible from Calhoun Road (Spur 5 feeder), by exiting I-45 South at Cullen.

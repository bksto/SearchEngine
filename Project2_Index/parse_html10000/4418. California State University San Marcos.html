Gordon Clarke Field House/ University Student Union (affectionately known as "The Clarke") serves the campus community by providing meeting rooms, lounge spaces, and fitness and recreational facilities. For assistance, call The Clarke at (760) 750-7400 Regular Hours of Operation Monday - Thursday 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM Friday 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM Saturday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM Sunday 12:00 PM - 8:00 PM http://www.csusm.edu/theclarke/ Fitness & Recreational Facilities The M. Gordon Clarke Field House / University Student Union offers a wide variety of fitness facilities including a full weight room, a gymnasium, outdoor basketball and sand volleyball courts, soccer field, and track and field facilities. In addition to the Fitness Center, the ASI Campus Recreation center (a tenant of The Clarke) coordinates other recreational programs which include fitness leisure classes, intramurals, outdoor excursions, and club sports.  For information on these programs visit ASI Campus Recreation in The Clarke (office CFHUSU 119). © Copyright 2012 California State University San Marcos.

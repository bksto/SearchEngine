Students can fill out a request form online; replies are given within 3 working days. Students also may call (909) 384-8975 or (909) 384-8972.  Students can email a librarian and look up class schedules at the Library website.  Online book orders may be done through the Bookstore. San Bernardino Valley College's Tutorial Center provides individual and small group tutoring sessions on campus. Sessions are free for current students in subjects including Biology, Chemistry, English, Math, Psychology and Spanish. Tutoring sessions are done by peers to help students improve their time management and study skills. Sessions may be scheduled online or by calling (909) 384-8566. The college's Writing Center offers free tutoring for students taking all disciplines and 100 level courses and above. Students may submit their assignments for review by email. The service is first-come, first-serve. Assignments are read Monday through Thursday and each submission is available for a 45-minute tutoring session. Papers are returned within 24 to 48 hours.

An accomplished researcher, Dr. Campuzano has made significant contributions to the science of high-temperature superconductors and to the phenomenon of photoemission spectroscopy. He is recognized as one of the two undisputed world leaders in the use of angle resolved photo-emission spectroscopy (ARPES), now universally recognized as one of the most powerful experimental tools used to probe novel materials.

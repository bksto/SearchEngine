Previous students and Alumni will need to use this link to access GOLD and then enter their PERM and PIN to login (UCSBNetIDs are deactivated after 13 months of separation from the University). Upon your initial use of this new service, you will be prompted to provide some contact/mailing information. You will only have to provide it that one time; your information will be saved after that initial prompt. A full list of products, descriptions and pricing is available once logged into this new service, but for quick reference here is what is offered: Product Price eTranscript  $16.00 Mailed/Paper Transcript (FedEx options available for additional fees)     $16.00  Paper Transcript for Pickup in the Office of the Registrar $26.00  Paper Transcript with Notarization  $36.00  For any students who placed an order using Credentials Solutions on or before 11/18/2014, and it has not been fulfilled yet, you can check your order status here: Check your order! Unofficial Transcripts Unofficial Transcripts of a student's record are available free of charge to all current and former students through GOLD. Please note - students who attended UCSB prior to 1987 may need to contact the Office of the Registrar before this option is available to them.

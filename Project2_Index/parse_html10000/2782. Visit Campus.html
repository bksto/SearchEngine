At Arkansas State, we welcome your visit and offer a variety of campus visitation programs to suit your needs. Scheduling Your Visit Our visits are very popular, so be sure to request your visit at least one week prior to visiting campus. The Office of Recruitment provides tours and recruitment information for students in 9th grade and above. Our visits range from general campus visits that showcase Arkansas State University's campus, but we also offer students the opportunity to meet with an academic advisor in their intended area of study through our Academic Campus Tours. High School Seniors Arkansas State provides several options for seniors to explore our campus. To ensure adequate time to prepare for your visit, please register at least a week in advance for all types of visits. We want you to have a great experience at A-State! General Campus Visits These visits are offered on Monday-Thursday at 2 different times: 9:30am and 1:30pm. During your general campus visits you will have the opportunity to: Meet with the Campus Experience Recruiter. Tour Campus with a Chancellor's Ambassador tour guide (approximately 45 minutes, weather permitting). The CA will guide you through our academic buildings, state of the art Red WOLF Fitness facility, and our Student Union. You will also see the residence halls that will be your home while at A-State. View schedule of visits >> Honors Visits Personalized Honors visits are offered to Fall 2016 prospective students with an ACT composite score of 28 or higher AND a cumulative high school GPA of 3.5 or higher. Honors visits are available Monday through Thursday at 9:30 am throughout the year. During your Honors visit, you will have the opportunity to: Meet with a recruitment advisor Tour campus, including the Honors Living Learning Community, with a student tour guide (approximately 45 minutes, weather permitting) Meet with a representative from The Honors College Request an Honors Visit >> Transfer Students Transferring from one college to another or simply returning to college can be intimidating, that's why Arkansas State is happy to offer campus visits specifically for transfer students. These visits will allow you to meet with a Recruitment Advisor and to ask questions you may have about transferring to Arkansas State. Individual Transfer Visits We understand that every transfer student experience is unique. These visits are offered on Monday-Thursday at 2 different times: 9:30am and 1:30pm. During your general campus visits you will have the opportunity to: Meet with the Transfer Coordinator (approximately 30 minutes). Tour Campus with a Chancellor's Ambassador tour guide (approximately 45 minutes, weather permitting). The CA will guide you through our academic buildings, state of the art Red WOLF Fitness facility, and our Student Union. You will also see the residence halls that will be your home while at A-State We strongly suggest contacting the department of your intended major to schedule a meeting with an Academic Advisor. Because each transfer student has completed different classes, you should plan to bring your unofficial transcripts with you. Prior to your arrival on campus, we encourage you to use our Transfer Equivalency Tool to determine how courses earned at another university will transfer to A-State. If you have specific questions for other offices (i.e. Residence Life, Financial Aid & Scholarships, etc.), please take advantage of your time on campus to set up meetings with representatives from those areas. Request a Campus Visit >> Transfer Group Visits Transfer Group Visit days are for our prospective transfer students. These visits will be held exclusively on Fridays. The dates are February 6th, March 6th, and April 3rd. All visits begin at 10 am. Please call 870-972-2782 to request a transfer group visit for your students. The Transfer Group visits include a transfer admissions presentation along with an opportunity to hear from the colleges and various academic units on campus. You will hear from a student panel and receive a campus tour. Students are encouraged to meet with the academic advisor of their intended major while on campus, however, the student is responsible for schedule those appointments. Group Visits High School Group Visits Arkansas State is happy to provide Group Visits tailored for high school groups of 25 to 60 students on Fridays during the spring. The dates for high school groups are January 23, January 30, February 13, February 20, February 27, March 20, April 3, April 10, April 17, and April 24. All visits begin at 10 am and will be scheduled on a first come, first served basis. Request a High School Group visit, please call 870-972-2782. We will do our best to accommodate your groups, but please keep in mind activities may be limited for larger groups. We will do our best to show your students a great Arkansas State experience! Group Visits are approximately 2 hours in length and consist of an admissions presentation and a student-led walking tour of campus. After you tour you may also visit the Arkansas State Museum or contact other offices for additional presentations. While Group Visits are free of charge, please note that we do not provide lunch. However, you may bring sack lunches, try one of the countless food court options on campus, or experience a meal in Acansa Dining Hall by calling 870-972-2059.

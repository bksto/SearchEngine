And the Newsweek guide to America's best colleges has named UCSB one of the country's "hottest colleges" twice in the past decade. UCSB is the largest employer in Santa Barbara County with career staff employees occupying management and professional, computing and technical, scientific and laboratory research, administrative and clerical support and service/maintenance and public safety positions. With a strong commitment to diversity, UCSB fills approximately 500-600 jobs annually. Total campus employment of over 10,000 includes staff, students, teaching assistants, librarians, tenured faculty and lecturers. The University of California is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer.  All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, or any other characteristic protected by law including protected veterans and individuals with disabilities.

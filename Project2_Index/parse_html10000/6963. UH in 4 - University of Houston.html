Lazo, UH Libraries Thank you for exploring UH in 4, the University of Houston's comprehensive plan for on-time graduation in four years. Please check back often as we continue to add additional resources to this site, providing you with the tools you need to achieve your goals. Yes, College is a New Frontier. It can be overwhelming. UH in 4 is a clear road to get you where you want to go. All you need to do is drive. UH in 4 is a program that helps students like you to graduate in 4 years. We'll provide academic maps, seasoned navigators and enhanced online tools to make your college experience the ride of a lifetime. We also offer fixed tuition to eliminate uncertainty about future tuition increases.  Talk with your family about how long your college experience should take. Talk about what it means to finish on time, in 4 years. They're along for the ride too.

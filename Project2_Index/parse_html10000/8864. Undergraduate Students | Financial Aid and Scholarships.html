Apply for Financial Aid To be considered for financial aid, you must file either the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) or the California Dream Act Application. To meet the priority deadline for the 2015-2016 academic year, file one of these applications once they become available on January 1, 2015 and submit it by March 2, 2015. Types of Aid Financial aid may be a combination of federal, state, institutional, and private fund(s) used to assist eligible students in paying for their education. Financial aid can be a combination of scholarships, grants, loans, waivers, student employment, veterans educational benefits, and child care assistance.

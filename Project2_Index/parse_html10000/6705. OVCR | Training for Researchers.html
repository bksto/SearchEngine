Maps A-Z Campus Index Directory Illinois Research Funding Policies & Compliance Training for Researchers About the OVCR Home » Training for Researchers Animal Research & IACUC Conflicts of Commitment and Interest Human Subjects & IRBs Lab Safety Office of Sponsored Programs and Research Administration Research Integrity Other Training Resources – Menu – Training for Researchers The Office of the Vice Chancellor for Research facilitates safe and responsible research as well as regulatory compliance by providing required training to faculty, staff and students. The specific research compliance trainings required for an individual are largely dependent on what their research involves and most individuals will require compliance training provided by more than one compliance unit, as well as laboratory specific training provided by faculty members or facility supervisors. This website contains links to the specific areas of compliance training within the University. Please contact the compliance units for assistance or if you have questions about training requirements. The University will require additional training not available through this website.

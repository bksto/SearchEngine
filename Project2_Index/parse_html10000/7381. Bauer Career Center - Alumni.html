mentor Bauer College Office of Alumni Relations Bauer Gateway Bauer alumni may access job postings, internships, information sessions, career fairs and workshops specifically for Bauer business majors through the Bauer Career Gateway. Access is easy, simply login using your MyUH (peopleSoft) ID. If you do not know your MyUH (peopleSoft) ID click here to retrieve this information. If you have any questions or need additional assistance with the Gateway, contact the Rockwell Career Center. Gateway Instruction Sheet Student User Guide Rockwell Career Center Code of Ethics   On-Campus Recruiting Orientation On-Campus Recruiting (OCR) is a program that allows alumni to interview with employers who visit Rockwell Career Center to find qualified applicants for full-time, part-time and internship positions. To submit your resume to these positions, your resume must be uploaded and approved on Gateway. In addition, applicants must meet employers’ criteria which may include but is not limited to: GPA, graduation date range and major. If you do not meet the criteria, consider attending an employer information session to network and learn more about the company.. To learn more about the On-Campus Recruiting program, please review both section 1 & section 2 of the short On-Campus Recruiting Orientation which are about 4 minutes each: Section 1 - Campus Recruitment Section 2 - Preparing for Your Interview   NOTE: Alumni are strongly encouraged to complete this orientation to fully understand how the On-Campus Recruiting program works. For any questions, concerns or for more information, please contact us at hirebauer@uh.edu or 832-842-6120. Consider helping your community. Offer your time to organizations that rely on volunteers to function. Gain additional experience and contacts to help you transition in to your new career. Here are some benefits of volunteering. You can make a difference, and contribute to things you care about. Volunteering may help you find a new career path and explore new interests. Volunteering provides opportunities to work with diverse populations. Meet students, alumni and members of your community. Employers like professionals who have leadership skills and experience. Volunteering is an opportunity to gain, build upon and fine tune these skills.

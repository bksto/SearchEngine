University Communications maintains and enhances the campus's main website and other top-level UC Merced sites, which feature: News, features and videos about people and programs, as well as campus events Links to individual academic, research and administrative information We research and provide information for news stories; issue press releases; facilitate interviews with a variety of campus sources, including faculty experts, staff and students; and we publicize events, programs and updated campus information. Services We Offer Marketing tools, such as publications, are another avenue with which our office provides support to ensure campus messages are clearly communicated and understood. Services we offer include: Editing and writing assistance Website guidance and design Strategic communications planning Video production Photography ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ Communications Newsletters We also distribute electronic newsletters to specific audiences. Panorama is a bi-weekly publication for faculty and staff. Update, which is for alumni, campus friends and donors, is distributed in the middle of each month. Submission deadlines vary, so please contact our office if you have submissions or questions.

Our Financial Aid Office administers a variety of programs to assist qualified students. Sources of student aid include Federal Pell Grants, Perkins Loans and Work-Study. A number of scholarships are awarded to new and continuing students who attend CSUSB. Students need to apply to FAFSA for access to federal assistance programs. Scholarships administered by the CSUSB Financial Aid Office can be applied for online.

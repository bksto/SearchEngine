The primary goal for most of these organizations is to support students in reaching their academic, personal and professional potential. In addition to hosting guest speakers and workshops, these organizations can help you meet new friends and contacts while discovering potential scholarship, internship and career opportunities. UIC encourages you to make time to get involved in a student activity that will enrich your educational journey! For more information and links to our various clubs and organizations, visit the Student Organizations website. To find out how to volunteer, visit uicvolunteer.org.

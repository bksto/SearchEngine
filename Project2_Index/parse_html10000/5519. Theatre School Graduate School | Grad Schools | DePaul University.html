> Admission & Aid > Types of Admission > Graduate Student > Graduate Theatre School Graduate Theatre School Main Page Content General Admission Instructions Please read through the instructions below before filling out the online application.  New students are admitted only at the beginning of the autumn quarter of each year and must enroll full time.  Application Fee  The application fee is $25, and should be paid when completing the online application. Transcripts  You must arrange to have an official copy of your academic transcripts from every college or university you've attended sent to our Admissions Office. Program-Specific Admission Instructions In addition to the general admission requirements, here are the program-specific admission requirements for each graduate program in The Theatre School:  Acting, MFA  Arts Leadership, MFA  Directing, MFA  Information For International Students Learn more about graduate admission information for international students.   Department Office Send all of your application materials to:   The Theatre School Admissions Office  DePaul University  2350 N. Racine Avenue Chicago, Illinois 60614 For More Information If you have any questions about the program or about the status of your application, please contact The Theatre School Admissions Office:  1. Online: Complete the Contact Us form  2. E-mail: Send an e-mail message to theatreadmissions@depaul.edu​.  3.

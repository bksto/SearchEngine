We take pride in our nationally-recognized diversity, working together to prepare the next generation of world leaders and to support pioneering research with economic, scientific and social impact on real-world challenges faced by Californians and society at large. The Office of Academic Personnel is a key resource for the recruitment, development and retention of faculty at UCR. Learn about the process of applying for and receiving a faculty appointment to UC Riverside. University Scholars Program: Recognizing Great Educators The university recognizes its outstanding young faculty through the University Scholars program, which is designed for tenured faculty members who are in the early stages of their careers. The awards are selected and given by the Office of the Executive Vice-Chancellor and Provost , and are designed to help young scholars reach their full potential. Going Abroad — Not Just for Students The Summer Travel Program features UCR faculty teaching a general catalog course to students in an academic environment in a foreign land. Participation of faculty is what makes this program work. Visit the Summer Travel Program website for more information on how to submit a proposal.

Our ultimate goal is to assist in providing a positive and rewarding experience to every graduate student in our College. Sincerely, Jonathan Schwartz Ph.D. Associate Dean for Graduate Studies, College of Education Mission The College’s Office of Graduate Studies (OGS) provide leadership and continuous support for the graduate programs, faculty and students in the College of Education.  Our main goal is to articulate a vision of excellence in our actions and policies that affect students, faculty and curriculum. OGS acts as a liaison between the University and the College’s graduate programs and advocates in terms of resources, services and other activities that support graduate student endeavors and goals. OGS serves as a liaison with the Graduate School and other administrative offices that serve the graduate community. Vision Leadership: Promotion and advocacy for current and new degree programs to achieve national rankings. Excellence: Assist graduate students with achieving program milestones for degree completion. Transparency: Maintain accurate and efficient record keeping for graduate admissions and academic processes. Integrity: Advise on University and College graduate policies and procedures to support student success. Service: Evaluate and administer fellowships and funding to support research and professional endeavors of graduate students. Student experience: Provide opportunities for graduate students to connect through outreach partnerships with the University and surrounding community. Continuous Improvement: Track and analyze graduate student data to improve the College's recruitment, admissions and academic processes.

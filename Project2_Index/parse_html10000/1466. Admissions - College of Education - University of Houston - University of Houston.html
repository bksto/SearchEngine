The College of Education at the University of Houston offers a wide spectrum of programs.  From teachers to health professionals, and superintendents  to psychologists -- the College of Education prepares students to achieve their dreams.  Visit our Degree Programs to learn more about these careers and the many others available through the College of Education. In all of our programs, you will learn from distinguished faculty who are breaking ground in their field through state-of-the-art Research. You will benefit from the College’s commitment to providing students with opportunities to participate actively in research. Become a leader in your chosen field with the knowledge and skills gained here. Are you ready?  Choose the level to which you wish to apply (undergraduate, graduate or other) to get started.

This convenient central location puts students close to all the best the city has to offer, including the Worcester Art Museum, the EcoTarium, and the world-renowned American Antiquarian Society. There’s also diverse dining, arts and entertainment, and a lively nightlife. Like professional sports? Come cheer on the Worcester Sharks hockey team and the Worcester Tornadoes baseball team.

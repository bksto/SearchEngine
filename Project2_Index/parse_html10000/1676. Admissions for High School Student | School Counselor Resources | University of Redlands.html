When Can I Apply to Redlands? The College of Arts and Sciences is an exclusive user of the Common Application. We process applications in two phases: Early Action (Nov. 15) and Regular Decision (Jan. 15).  Applications submitted beyond these deadlines will be considered on a space available basis. Further details about notification, scholarships and financial aid deadlines are outlined in the following pages. Spring admission is offered to applicants who apply no later than November 1 of each year.

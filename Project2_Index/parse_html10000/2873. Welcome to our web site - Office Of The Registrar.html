Cal Student Central (CSC) Provides customer service regarding registration and enrollment, financial aid, fees and billing, payments and disbursements. Visit them in 120 Sproul Hall, Monday-Friday, 9 a.m. - 4 p.m. Online at studentcentral.berkeley.edu, call 510-664-9181 or use our web form to open a case. Academic Calendar News The Academic Calendars for the 2014, 2015 and 2016 Summer Sessions have been revised. Start dates for the 8-week Summer Session in 2014, 2015 and 2016 and the 1st 6-Week Summer Session for 2014 and 2015 only have been adjusted to align with past practice. These revisions do not impact any other dates on the approved academic calendars. See the new Summer Sessions calendars for details.

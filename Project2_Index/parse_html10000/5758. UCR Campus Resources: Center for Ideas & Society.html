Open Your Mind From hip hop theatre to conferences on violence and religion, UC Riverside's Center for Ideas & Society takes a unique approach to studying and understanding the human experience. By encouraging collaborative and interdisciplinary research in the humanities, arts and social sciences, the center has teamed scholars from diverse disciplines to gain new perspectives. Social scientists might work with arts professors on center projects. Environmental scientists might team up with film scholars. The results are performances and conferences that have gained national and international recognition for promoting and advancing humanistic research. Programs sponsored by the center include: Residency fellowships for UCR faculty and graduate students. Visiting faculty fellowships. Faculty and graduate student Focused Research Groups Conferences. Lectures, seminars, and colloquia. Performances, readings, screenings, and exhibitions. Curriculum and pedagogy Initiatives. The Distinguished Humanistic Achievement Award. The publication of occasional papers.

Read More   Page Left Item 1 Item 2 Item 3 Page Right Main Page Content ​Search Mentor Directory Mentor Login Welcome to ASK DePaul Alumni Sharing Knowledge (ASK) is a network of alumni and friends who serve as career mentors, working with students one-on-one, in practice interviews and at job fairs, and speaking at networking events and open houses throughout the university. ASK mentors were once DePaul students, so they know what you are going through. Whether you are exploring majors or career options or searching for a job that aligns with your passion, talking to someone who's been there can help. There are over 1,000 mentors in ASK at any given time who represent a variety of professional expertise.

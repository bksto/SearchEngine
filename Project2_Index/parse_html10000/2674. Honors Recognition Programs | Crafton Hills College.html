The majority of these are sponsored by college and campus organizations; however, a number are made possible by community organizations and interested citizens. Graduating with Honors: Please see the Academic Honors and Graduation page for information regarding honors at graduation. Alpha Gamma Sigma: Students who have a cumulative grade point average of 3.00 or higher may join Alpha Gamma Sigma, the California Community College Honor Scholarship Society. For more information, visit the Alpha Gamma Sigma web page or contact the Student Life Office. Dean's / Honors Lists: Outstanding scholastic achievement by Crafton Hills College students is recognized through the Dean's Honors List. The Dean's List is generated twice in each academic year, once during the fall semester and once during the spring semester. Recognition is based on GPA earned during the individual semester as opposed to a cumulative GPA. Only units earned at Crafton Hills College will be used in calculating a student's GPA for the Dean's List and Honors List. Dean's List: To qualify for the Dean's List, students must complete 12 or more units of college-level courses during the semester with a grade point average (GPA) of 3.5 or higher.   Honors List: To qualify for the Honors List, students must complete between 6.0 and 11.9 units of college-level courses during the semester with a grade point average (GPA) of 3.5 or higher.   Dean's Lists Honors Lists Spring 2015 | Fall 2015 Spring 2015 | Fall 2015 Spring 2014 | Fall 2014 Spring 2014 | Fall 2014 Spring 2013 | Fall 2013 Spring 2013 | Fall 2013 Spring 2012 | Fall 2012 Spring 2012 | Fall 2012 Spring 2011 | Fall 2011 Spring 2011 | Fall 2011 Spring 2010 | Fall 2010 Spring 2010 | Fall 2010 Spring 2009 | Fall 2009 Spring 2009 | Fall 2009 Spring 2008 | Fall 2008 Spring 2008 | Fall 2008 Honors Recognition Programs College Honors Institute Crafton Hills College 11711 Sand Canyon Road Yucaipa, CA 92399 909-794-2161 © Crafton Hills College Campus Map Driving Directions Site Map Annual Security Report Contact Us, Comment or Complain Give someone a thumbs-up with an Applause Card SBCCD | SBVC | KVCR TV/FM San Bernardino Community College District does not discriminate on the basis of age, color, creed, religion, disability, marital status, veteran status, national origin, race, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or gender expression.

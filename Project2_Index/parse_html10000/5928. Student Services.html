The Vice President of Student Services provides leadership and oversees the overall coordination of programs, services and resources within the Student Services division.  Students and staff are invited to bring their concerns, recommendations and complaints to this office so that we may work to bring about the most positive outcomes for all concerned. Student Life and Associated Student Government, Enrollment, Financial Aid, Student Health Services, Records, Counseling, Career Services and Job Placement, DSP&S, EOPS/CARE, Assessment and Matriculation, International Students, Transfer Services, Outreach and Recruitment, STAR Program, Veteran's Resource Center and other specialized services designed for those who qualify are all covered under student services.   Student Services Mission:  To provide a system of support services that enhances student success and achievement of educational goals.    Student Services Office Hours Student Services Documents: ADA Compliant Process Disruptive Student Incident Report  Student Code of Conduct  Student Rights and Responsibilities  Office of the Vice President of Student Services Counseling Student Life Library Tutoring & Academic Support Transfer and Career Services Specialized Counseling Services Food Services Policies & Procedures Google Map™ Campus Map San Bernardino Valley College 701 South Mount Vernon Ave. San Bernardino, CA 92410 909-384-4400 A college of the San Bernardino Community College District Copyright © 2015 San Bernardino Valley College.

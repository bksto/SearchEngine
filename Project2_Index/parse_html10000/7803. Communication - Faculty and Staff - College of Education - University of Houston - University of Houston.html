We partner with faculty, students and staff to reach diverse internal and external audiences with timely, informative, effective and brand-appropriate messages. What We Do We inform the world about the UH College of Education, our academic and extracurricular programs and our events. We celebrate the achievements of our alumni, students, faculty and staff through our website, social media, news media relations, photography, graphics, print publications and other forms of communication. We assist all departments and offices in achieving their communication and marketing goals. Templates, Tutorials and General Resources The Office of Communications and Events is committed to helping faculty and staff members reach the UH College of Education’s diverse internal and external audiences with timely, informative, effective and brand-appropriate messages. Toward that end, we offer templates, tutorials and general resources to help you achieve your communication and event goals.

First-Time Freshman & Undergraduate California Resident WICHE/WUE-Western Undergraduate Exchange Housing & Meal Plan Options Books, Supplies, & Materials Financial Aid and Scholarship Options *The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum. Tuition fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate. Therefore, CSU must reserve the right, even after initial tuition fee payments are made, to increase or modify any listed fees, without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun. All CSU listed tuition fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by The Board of Trustees.

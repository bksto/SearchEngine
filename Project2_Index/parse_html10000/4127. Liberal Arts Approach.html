About Redlands University of Redlands > About Redlands > Student Life > Liberal Arts Approach Liberal Arts Approach At the heart of the Redlands experience is the belief that education should be highly personalized, relevant, and interdisciplinary. No student’s experience is exactly like another’s—from First Year Seminar to travel abroad, students are given the flexibility to chart their own path. Much more than simply choosing a major and a minor, your time with Redlands will be a holistic academic experience. As you begin your career at Redlands, you will be guided through the process of choosing your Liberal Arts Foundation courses—classes designed to help students explore a variety of topics before deciding upon a major. With hundreds of classes to choose from, students are encouraged to consider the world from a global perspective, and engage in topics that pique their interests.

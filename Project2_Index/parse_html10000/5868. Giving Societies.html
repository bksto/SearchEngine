We offer a number of options for giving which also allow us to acknowledge your thoughtful support. They include: The President’s Circle, a group of alumni and friends of the University who help President Gonzalez respond to emerging issues on the campus. Several giving individual and corporate giving levels are available, beginning at $1,500. The Dean’s Leadership Circle provides a critical source of support, enabling deans to direct funds to the areas of greatest need in their College. Individuals become members of the Dean’s Circle through a tax-deductible annual gift. Corporate Associates support student scholarships while assisting the College of Business Administration. Giving levels allow donors to choose their degree of visibility and involvement. The Legacy Circle recognizes those who have made provision for the University in their estate plans or some other type of planned gift. The 1947 Society encouraging students to help fund leadership opportunities and scholarships to honor the University’s founding year.

San Jacinto College in which a student receives a commitment of provisional admission to CSUSB provided all requirements, GPA minimum and prerequisites have been met. Enrollment into this program provides for application fee waivers to CSUSB for the terms the student plans to be admitted. Students must complete the agreed upon requirements no later than the term preceding entry.

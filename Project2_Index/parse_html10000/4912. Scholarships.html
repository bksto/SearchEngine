CSUDH offers a tradition of academic excellence, with a wide selection of academic programs. With tuition more than 30% below the national average for state schools, CSUDH remains one of the best values in higher education. We are committed to making it possible for more students to access and afford a quality university education from a recognized institution of higher learning. As part of this commitment, CSUDH offers a wide range of scholarship programs to help deserving students finance their degrees. Each year, we award over $79 million in scholarships and grants. Whatever your economic and academic background, we strongly encourage you to apply for scholarships today. Premier Scholarships CSUDH offers three Premier Scholarship Programs, awarded to exceptional students who demonstrate academic excellence, community and campus involvement, and leadership qualities. University Scholarships These renowned scholarship programs are available exclusively to CSU Dominguez Hills students. Get more information and apply today!

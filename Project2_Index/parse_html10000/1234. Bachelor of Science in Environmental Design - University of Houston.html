Degree Plan The program for the Bachelor of Science in Environmental Design degree is a pre-professional four-year curriculum of at least 128 semester hours. The curriculum includes 73 hours of Architecture courses, 34 of which are advanced hours (3000-level or higher). The degree includes the University’s required core curriculum and 4 semester hours of elective courses.

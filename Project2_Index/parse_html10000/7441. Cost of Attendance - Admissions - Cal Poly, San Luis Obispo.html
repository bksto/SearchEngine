Parent VIP Page Cost of Attendance After Cal Poly Graduate Programs International Cost of Attendance 2014/2015 Cal Poly Student Costs The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum. Fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate. Therefore, the CSU must reserve the right, even after initial fee payments are made, to increase or modify any listed fees, without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun. All CSU listed fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by The Board of Trustees. Budgets are estimated and subject to change.

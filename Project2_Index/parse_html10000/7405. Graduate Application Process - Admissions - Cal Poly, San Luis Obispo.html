View the deadlines for your intended program and plan accordingly. You then submit your online application through www.CSUMentor.edu. Once your application has been received, you will immediately receive email confirmation from Cal Poly confirming its receipt. Your application is later loaded to the student database and you are emailed instructions on accessing Cal Poly’s university portal:  MyCalPoly.  Applicants should access MyCalPoly to view further application requirements specific to the program of application and, later, the admission status through MyCalPoly. After all application requirements are met and final documents are received by the Admissions Office, your admissions file is submitted for consideration to the graduate/program coordinator. You are notified of the application decision after your file has been evaluated by the graduate coordinator by the Admissions Office. Applicants will only be able to submit one application per term.

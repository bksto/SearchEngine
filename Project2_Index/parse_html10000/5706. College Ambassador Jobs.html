Check out our recruiting flyer and more information on this page. College Ambassadors represent the university to 40,000+ visitors each year. We give the tours for prospective students and families. What's it all about? Are you a current student who's excited about your experience at UC San Diego? Do you want to make an impact on students visiting the campus? Interested in sharpening your leadership skills? You could be a paid student employee who conducts walking tours of the campus, appears on student panels, and participates in special events related to recruitment. And you'll be working with a great group of people. Here's a news article about us. What's the job like? Five hours minimum work time per week, with summer hours available. Paid training time. Lots of walking. Meet people — meet a lot of people! Learn about UC San Diego's history. Practice your public-speaking skills. Get some walking exercise, and get paid to do it. Download the information recruiting flyer. Apply to be a College Ambassador The Office of Admissions and Relations with Schools is not currently hiring, but check back soon! Take a campus tour before you apply, just to see what it's like. Available nearly every day at 10 a.m., except Sundays and holidays. Sign up online for a Triton Tour. Questions? Let us know you're interested by emailing the Campus Tours Office or call us at (858) 822-4750.

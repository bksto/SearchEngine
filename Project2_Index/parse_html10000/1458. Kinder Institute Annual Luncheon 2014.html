Kinder Institute for Urban Research Kinder Institute Luncheon 2015 Thank you for visiting the registration page for the Kinder Institute Luncheon 2015, hosted by Rice University’s Kinder Institute for Urban Research. We invite you to register for the luncheon below. The Kinder Institute for Urban Research and new director William Fulton will celebrate the institute’s year of research and community partnership. The Kinder Institute Luncheon 2015 will also feature keynote speaker Dr. Stephen Klineberg and the official release of the 34th annual Kinder Institute Houston Area Survey.

Enrollment is voluntary and open to young men and women who are U.S. citizens of good moral character and sound physical condition who seek the challenge of being an officer in the U.S. Air Force upon graduation from college. In addition to WPI students, students at any of the Worcester Consortium for Higher Education institutions are also eligible to apply for Air Force ROTC at WPI. Bussmann, Capt Vito A.

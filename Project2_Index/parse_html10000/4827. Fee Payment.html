Credit Card Payments Tuition and fees charged to your American Express, Discover, MasterCard, or VISA account (2.75% convenience fee). Credit cards only accepted in person for fees other than tuition and fees. Personal Check or Money Order Payment of tuition and fees may be mailed, dropped in the Payment Drop Box outside Student Accounting & Cashiering Services or made in person. Cash Payment of tuition and fees in person at Student Accounting & Cashiering Services. Installment Payment Plans (PDF) Payment of tuition and fees in two installments for California residents or three installments for non-resident students. Short-Term Loans (PDF) Loans up to a maximum of $500 of full in-state fees for qualified students who have resources to pay the loan by the repayment date.  Financial Aid and Scholarships Payment of tuition and fees by grant, loan and scholarship programs available to eligible students. ﻿Employee/Dependent Fee Waivers Waiver of state fees for employees to improve their current job skills or obtain a degree for career development or for dependents of elgible employees. CSU Mandatory Fee Waiver Programs Waiver of fees for students qualifying for  the Alan Pattee, Dependents of Victims of September 11 Attacks, and Non-Resident California High School Graduate programs. Third-Party Sponsor Programs Payment of tuition and fees by third-party sponsors, such as vocational rehabilitation. Veterans Benefits Deferment of tuition and fees available until veterans benefits are received. Cal Vet Fee Waiver Waiver of fees for eligible dependents of Veterans.

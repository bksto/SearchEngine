News Archive Alumni Newsletter Alumni Newsletter Archive: Gevirtz + Common Core About The Gevirtz School The Gevirtz Graduate School of Education (GGSE) offers M.A.s, M.Ed.s, and Ph.D.s from the Department of Counseling, Clinical and School Psychology, the Department of Education and the Teacher Education Program, which also prepares students for teaching credentials. The School also offers three minors in Education, Applied Psychology, and Science & Mathematics Education. The GGSE is part of UC Santa Barbara, a member of the Association of American Universities, and located on the Pacific coast 90 miles north of Los Angeles. To learn more about the school please use the links at left, examine the pages about our school available from the top page navigation, or see the GGSE by the Numbers page.

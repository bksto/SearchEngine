Alumni Association Alumni Awards Alumni across the globe are encouraged to nominate their fellow Bulldogs for a variety of awards given out annually. There are eight categories highlighting a variety of skills, talents or benchmarks. The Alumni Board reviews all nominations and votes for the most outstanding candidate in each category. Please review the summary of awards below to see what category you believe best suits your nomination. Then, fill out the corresponding nomination form.

The study is more intense, but so is the support to help you succeed. Science and engineering may be notoriously tough to master, but far back in our history we decided that a failing grade is as much a failure on our part as the student’s. We want you to branch out, cross disciplines, and experiment. From our award-winning title="First Year Experience">first year programs, to the low student-to-faculty ratio (14:1), you will not get lost in the crowd here. Departments & Areas of Study WPI students pursue bachelor’s, master’s, and Ph.D. degrees in more than 50 disciplines.

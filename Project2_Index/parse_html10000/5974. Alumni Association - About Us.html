The May 11 event attracted 10,000 spectators, 15 pachyderm entrants, a telegram from Richard M. Nixon and worldwide news coverage. Associated Press rated the story among the top 10 for 1962. Day of the Titan was born. The singular noun for a male graduate is "alumnus"; plural for male or coed graduate is "alumni." Singular for a female graduate is "alumna"; plural for more than one female graduate is "alumnae." But once a Titan, always a Titan!

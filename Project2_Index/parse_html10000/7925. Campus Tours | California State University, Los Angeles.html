Campus tours are a great way to familiarize you with Cal State L.A. and to see all that we have to offer. Our Cal State L.A. Eagle Guides are highly-motivated students trained to walk you through the campus, and introduce you to all the unique services and opportunities that are available at our university. We currently offer special group and individual tours. If you are interested in scheduling a tour or have any questions, please contact the Campus Tours office at (323) 343-3175, or via e-mail at tours@calstatela.edu. And, to ensure that the tour is a pleasant experience, please remember to dress comfortably. We hope to see you soon! Individual Tours Individual tours are available to prospective students, their families and friends. Tours last approximately an hour and meet at the Student Information Center in the Student Affairs building, room 101. To make a reservation, please contact Campus Tours at (323) 343-3175, or via e-mail at tours@calstatela.edu Special Group Tours Special group tours are available for groups of 10 or more. We will make every effort to accommodate the needs of your group. If you would like to arrange for a group tour, please contact Campus Tours at (323) 343-3175, or via e-mail at tours@calstatela.edu.

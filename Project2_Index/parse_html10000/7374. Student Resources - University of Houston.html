At UH, we support your success with wellness programs, counseling services, a professional police service and a lot more. We also offer specialized programs for international students and students with disabilities.  More... University Career Services will help you refine your career goals and achieve them through individual career counseling, career fairs and a lot more. UH Wellness is committed to providing a variety of wellness services to help the Cougar community achieve their health and fitness goals. These services include both individualized programs and group fitness activities that specifically concentrate on improving both mind and body. The Center for Students with DisABILITIES provides accommodations and support services to UH students who have any type of health impairment, physical limitation, psychiatric disorder or learning disability. Commuter Student Services to provide support services and effective communication to help facilitate commuter students' success and connection to the UH community. Counseling & Psychological Services with mental health professionals are available to all students, faculty and staff. The LGBT Resource Center at the University of Houston seeks to create an environment of inclusion and acceptance for all LGBT students, staff and faculty. The UH Health Center offers the convenience of on-campus health care provided by a qualified and caring staff with board-certified physicians. The International Student & Scholar Services Online office works with UH students and scholars from outside the U.S. to answer questions, streamline paperwork, explain financial information and more. The Student Advocacy And Support Services helps students resolve both academic and nonacademic problems such as issues with residency status, registration, fee payment, housing, financial aid, grades, parking and more. University Eye Institute offers services that range from complete vision exams to the medical and surgical management of eye disorders. University Testing Services administers admission and placement exams, psychological batteries, correspondence examinations from other universities, and professional certification and licensing examinations. The Women's Resource Center offers a comfortable place where faculty, staff and students can seek information, obtain confidential referrals and discuss issues of concern. The UH Police Department maintains a safe environment on campus and employs many programs to assist the UH Community. These programs are designed to improve your safety and security on campus.

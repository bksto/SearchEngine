You will have the option of paying in two (2) installments for Summer 2015, and one (1), two (2), or six (6) installments for the 2015-16 Academic Year. Double refers to two persons per bedroom. Due to increasing demand for housing, effective Summer 2015, University Housing will no longer offer a single room option. All utilities (electricity, gas, and water), basic cable television and wireless Internet service are included in the rental fees. Please be aware, however, that telephone service is not included. Each resident is responsible for getting his or her own telephone line connected. A refundable $300 Security Deposit is also due before the start of occupancy. Please refer to the Application Checklist for more information on the deposit. All fees are subject to change by the executive order of the Chancellor of the California State University.   Cancellations and Refunds Cancellations and refunds are subject to the following conditions: Cancellations must be done through the Housing Portal before the start of occupancy. The Security Deposit is refundable as follows: - If you are denied admission to the University. - If we are unable to assign you to a space. Your deposit will be forfeited if your cancellation is not submitted PRIOR to a space being assigned to you. Rent payments are refundable as follows: - If you are denied admission to the University. - If we receive your cancellation PRIOR to the official check-in date for the term you are applying. If we receive your cancellation AFTER the official check-in date, you will be refunded all fees paid less the pro-rated rent charge for the number of days after the check-in date your cancellation was received. Please refer to our Cancellation Policy for more information.

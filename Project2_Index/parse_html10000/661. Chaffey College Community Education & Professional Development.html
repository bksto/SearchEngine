Our mission is to make high-quality education accessible to everyone, creating opportunities for our students and contributing to the economic development of our community. Community Education offers programs that provide you and your family opportunities to learn, grow, and develop through youth, medical, business and income, technology, and interest and enrichment programs. Our programs are funded through program enrollment fees and are not funded with tax dollars. Community Education programs are scheduled throughout the community during the day, evening, and on weekends. Community Education programs do not result in college credit nor do they meet credit course prerequisite requirements. Getting Started Community Education programs are available to anyone who registers online or in person. You are not required to enroll in credit courses at the college. Use the navigation links on this page to browse through current program offerings.

Supporting the Chancellor and Provost in identifying UC Davis faculty with expertise in countries where the campus already has or plans to pursue international exchange, academic programs, international programs, and alumni relations. Helping potential UC Davis graduate students and researchers to identify international expertise and collaboration with UC Davis faculty. The database is an important campus tool showcasing the breadth of international linkages and expertise of UC Davis faculty in the Academic Senate and Academic Federation. Submit or Edit Faculty Records If you are a faculty member wishing to add or update your record, click below to: ADD YOUR ENTRY UPDATE YOUR ENTRY *First time and returning faculty please note this is a 3-step process. A pop-up window will ask you for your Kerberos ID and password. You will then be routed right back to the Add/Update entry page that you came from.  Scroll down to ADD/UPDATE YOUR ENTRY and CLICK THE LINK ONE MORE TIME. You will then be routed to the Faculty Linkages page. Please contact IT Express at 754-HELP if you need assistance locating your Kerberos ID. If you experience any difficulty submitting your record, you can fill out an e-mail version of the survey, by clicking here. This will open a new message in your e-mail application, with the survey questions included. To learn more about the database, read the Dateline feature article (link).

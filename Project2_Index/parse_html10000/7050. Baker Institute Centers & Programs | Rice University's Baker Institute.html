In conjunction with its more than 20 programs, the institute attracts many domestic and foreign leaders who provide their views and insights on key issues. The institute ranks 9th among university-affiliated think tanks worldwide, 18th among U.S. think tanks and fourth among the world’s energy- and resource-policy think tanks, according to a 2014 study by the University of Pennsylvania's Think Tanks and Civil Societies Program.

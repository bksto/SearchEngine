Texas A&M enrolls over 50,000 students, including over 10,000 graduate students. More than 5,000 international students from more than 130 countries attend classes here. The average SAT score for incoming freshmen is 1220 (Verbal + Math). 25% of the freshman class are first generation college students. The family-friendly atmosphere makes Texas A&M a favorite not only of students, but their parents as well. More than 800 student organizations, including a uniformed Corps of Cadets, Greek Life, and The Big Event - the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation. Bryan/College Station is located within easy driving distance of four major metropolitan areas — Houston, San Antonio, Austin, and Dallas/Ft. Worth — making it easy to go home, or get away, for the weekend.

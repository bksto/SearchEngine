University Advancement is responsible for alumni affairs and charitable gifts to the institution, as well as arranging and managing institutional partnerships with a wide variety of regional groups. University Advancement is also the official liaison to organizations affiliated with the University, including Capital Public Radio, the University Foundation at Sacramento State and the Sacramento State Community Advisory Board.

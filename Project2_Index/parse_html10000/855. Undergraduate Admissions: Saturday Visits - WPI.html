We will offer student-led campus tours and information sessions, conducted by the admissions staff. So, bring all your questions about admissions, academics, and social life. We'll be ready and waiting for you! We will be open the following Saturdays in Spring 2015: March 21, 28 April 4, 11, 18, 25 May 2 At 10 a.m. and 1 p.m. on each Saturday we'll offer an information session followed by a student-led campus tour. Together the information session and tour will last just over two hours.

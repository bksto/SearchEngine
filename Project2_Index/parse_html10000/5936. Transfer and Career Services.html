San Bernardino Valley College Transfer Center is here to help you with your smooth transition to university life by providing services, information, and assistance. A variety of services such as university appointments, transfer fairs, admissions workshops, and university tours are available to you. It is time to start your journey to your chosen university whether it be a CSU, UC, private or out of state university. SBVC is a great place to fulfill your lower division general education as well as your major preparation for transfer. Counselors are available to assist you in developing your education plan specifically designed to meet your transfer goals. Transfer Begins Here Let us Help you! Transfer Services Individual appointments with UC and CSU university representatives Online university application/information workshops Free university brochures Assistance with the UC Transfer Agreement Program University/College Transfer Fairs Computer workstations with internet access to college website and research Information on special Transfer Programs Historical Black Colleges/Universities Information Honors Program Access to university catalog online Online Calendar of Events Workshops on transfer related topics University tours Transfer and Career Counseling is available online at transfercenter@valleycollege.edu. Students can call at (909) 384-4410 or stop by the Transfer and Career Services office in ADSS-203 to make an appointment to see a Transfer Counselor. Transfer Recognition Award - In acknowledgement of achieving your educational objective, the Transfer Recognition Award enables the college to personally congratulate you. All students who are eligible for the Transfer Recognition Award will be given a framed certificate and an Academic Medallion to wear at the graduation ceremony. Mission Statement Transfer Services mission is to design, develop and implement a variety of strategies that increase the number of SBVC students prepared to transfer to a baccalaureate level institution, the University of California (UC), California State University (CSU) and the independent colleges and universities statewide.

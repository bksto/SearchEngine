You are considered an international student if you are neither a U.S citizen nor a U.S. permanent resident. Visit the University of California's website for international students Questions? Nearly 2,000 international students and 2,200 international scholars (faculty and researchers) come to UC San Diego from almost 90 different countries/regions. Get involved at the International Center to connect with other students students from the United States and from all around the world. Join a club to learn a new skill, build connections, or create change. Community Centers celebrate diversity, and offer a home base to be comfortable, relax, make friends, or work for social justice.

This page is for: prospective students intending to apply to UC San Diego - use this as a guide for planning. current applicants who are waiting for a decision and who graduated from a secondary school outside the U.S. - make sure you meet this condition. If you are a newly admitted freshman or transfer student, please visit the Required Documents from Schools Outside the United States page. You will need to request official records from the school(s) you attended. You must achieve excellent results on your final secondary school exams.

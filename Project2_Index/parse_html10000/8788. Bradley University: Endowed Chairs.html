They enable Bradley University to offer a more diverse curriculum that is independent of usual income sources, and they provide long-term financial stability for teaching, research and scholarship in specific fields. Endowed chairs empower Bradley University to attract, retain and honor distinguished faculty members, recognizing them for their academic excellence.

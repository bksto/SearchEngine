Through our strong and relevant academic programs, dedicated faculty mentors, supportive staff, and attractive campus and student amenities, CSUDH is committed to connecting our students to an affordable, high-quality and transformative education while providing our communities with a vital resource for talent, knowledge, skills and leadership needed to thrive today and tomorrow. HISTORY, MISSION & VISION CSUDH’s mission is to provide education, scholarship and service that are accessible and transformative. Learn how CSUDH supports this mission, the values that serve as our institutional pillars, our long history of educational excellence and our vision for the future of our university as well as the communities we support. CAMPUS AMENITIES CSUDH’s scenic campus includes many amenities for students, staff and faculty, and the community at large. These include our award-winning Loker Student Union, our beautiful newly expanded University Library, and the 250-seat Claudia Hampton Lecture Hall, and the James L. Welch Hall featuring mediated-instruction classrooms and computer-based laboratories. Students also enjoy the campus’s many open green spaces, perfect for enjoying Southern California year-round sunshine. COMMUNITY RESOURCES CSUDH serves as a vital educational, economic, cultural and recreational resource for the South Bay area of Los Angeles. We welcome you to attend our year-round events listed on our campus calendar, and held at our renowned University Art Gallery, University Theater, and the StubHub Center located on our campus. We also welcome CSUDH graduates to get involved in our active Alumni Association. Help us continue to be a vital community resource by making a gift.

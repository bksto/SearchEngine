Jump to Navigation Admissions Apply Visit Info Application Deadlines You are hereHome › Application Deadlines Fall Applications Available online beginning in early August for entry in the following fall semester (e.g., Fall 2014 application for entry in the Fall 2015 term). The fall application-filing period is Nov. 1 through 30 for the following fall semester. Applications must be submitted online by 11:59 p.m. Pacific Standard Time (PST) on Nov. 30. The fall application review and selection process begins in January. Freshman admission decisions are posted in the portal (https://my.ucmerced.edu) in March.   Spring Applications CLOSED FOR SPRING 2015. Available online beginning in July for entry in the following spring semester (e.g., Summer 2014 application for entry in Spring 2015). The spring application filing period is July 1 through 31. Applications must be submitted online by 11:59 p.m. Pacific Standard Time (PST) on July 31. The spring application review and selection process begins in September. Freshman admission decisions are posted in the portal (https://my.ucmerced.edu) in October.   Application Notification You will receive an acknowledgment notice from the UC Application Processor within a week or two of submitting your application. If you do not receive that acknowledgment, contact the UC Application Processor at 800-523-2048 or ucinfo@ucapplication.net For information on using the online application, visit the University of California Application Website.

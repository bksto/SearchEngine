The Disability Services team assists academic, staff and student employees with temporary or long-term disability planning, including:  Counseling on salary continuation options, including accrued paid leave, short-term disability, supplemental disability, and long-term disability.  Advising and co-ordinating leave entitlements, including Family and Medical Leave, Pregnancy Disability Leave, etc. Providing temporary modified or alternate work under the Transitional Work Program to help employees transition back to their regular position. Facilitating reasonable accommodations for disability-related limitations. Any information on this site should be read concurrently with the Personnel Policies for Staff Members (PPSM) or appropriate Collective Bargaining Agreement or with Academic Personnel Manual. If you have additional questions after reviewing the material, please contact Disability Services.

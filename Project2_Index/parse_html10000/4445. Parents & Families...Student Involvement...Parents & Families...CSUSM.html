We encourage students who are interested in leadership and becoming engaged members of the CSUSM community to consider pursuing one (or all) of the following opportunities: Orientation Team: Applications to be part of the 2103 Orientation Team will be available on Tuesday, January 22nd. Orientation Team, also known as O-Team, is a team of students who volunteer their time welcoming our new students and families at Orientation. Interested students should keep checking our website at http://www.csusm.edu/sll/onsp/oteam.html. Resident Advisors: Students interested in becoming Resident Advisors in the University Village Apartments or The QUAD for 2013-2014 can apply now. For more information on the position and to apply, students can stop by either complex or visit their facebook page at http://www.facebook.com/uvaquadrarecruitment. Applications are due February 22, 2013 at 11:59pm. Fraternity and Sorority Recruitment: Students interested in becoming a member of our Fraternity and Sorority community are encouraged to take part in Formal Recruitment, February 11 -18, 2013. For more information on our chapters and how to take part in recruitment, visit our website at http://www.csusm.edu/sll/greek/membership.html. LEAD Retreat: 1st and 2nd year students are invited to apply to attend our annual Emerging Leaders retreat, March 8 – 10, 2013. There is no cost for students to attend. For more information and to apply, students should visit our website at http://www.csusm.edu/sll/leadership. © Copyright 2013 California State University San Marcos. All rights reserved.

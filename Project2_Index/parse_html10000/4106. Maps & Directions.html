The University of Redlands is situated on 160 acres in the historic city of Redlands. The campus is conveniently located just off the I-10 freeway. We are approximately 60 minutes east of Los Angeles and 40 minutes west of Palm Springs. Traveling eastbound from the Los Angeles area: Exit the I-10 freeway at University St. Turn left onto University St. Travel on University St. to Colton Ave. Turn right onto Colton Ave. and drive into the campus. Traveling westbound from the Palm Springs area: Exit the I-10 freeway at Cypress Ave. Turn left onto Cypress Ave. Turn right onto University St. Travel on University St. to Colton Ave. Turn right onto Colton Ave. and drive into the campus.   If you are visiting the main campus in Redlands for the first time, we recommend that you park at the Willis Center on East Colton Avenue. You'll find visitor parking spaces and inside the Willis Center, you'll be welcomed by University representatives who can help you find your way around campus. For driving directions to one of our regional locations, visit Branch Campuses.

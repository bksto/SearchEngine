A student who registers for classes is assessed the following per term: Tuition, Registration Fee, Michigan Student Assembly Fee, Student Legal Services Fee, and a School/College Government Fee. Additionally, a student may be charged a laboratory or other supplemental fee per class. Some programs may have particular fees associated with them, and students should contact their School or College to verify if their program carries additional fees.

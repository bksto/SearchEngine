Walamo Waray Washo Welsh Wolof Xhosa Yakut Yao Yap Yiddish Yoruba Yupik languages Zande Zapotec Zenaga Zhuang Zulu Zuni | Reset form Search Hints Searches are not case sensitive For more complex searches, use the connectors AND or OR. Example: surgery AND (heart OR cardiac) finds all records having either heart or cardiac in them, together with surgery. Use NOT to exclude words. Example: Kipling NOT India. Use * to substitute for zero or more letters. Example, fish* finds fish, fishes, fishing, etc. Use # to find variant spellings where 0-2 characters may vary. Example, arch#eology finds archaeology and archeology. Use ! to limit variant spellings in words where only a single character may vary. Example: theat!! finds theatre or theater but not theatrical.

You can tour our campus on your own using downloadable tour apps for Apple products and Android platforms. These are Cal Poly's options: 1. GPS iPhone/iPad/iPod and Android Tour Cal Poly offers campus tours downloadable from Apple through the App store to your iPhone/iPad/iPod or Android (this option is not available to other smart phones at this time). Follow these instructions to download the tour: Click on the App store search for "Tour Cal Poly" For iPhone/iPad/iPod: Select the application titled : Tour Cal Poly and install For the Android: Select the application titled : Tour Cal Poly and install Once installed, open the application and select a Cal Poly Tour from the list 2. Self-Guided Map Tour Campus visitors can also print out a downloadable self-guided tour map and use these to navigate the campus. The Admissions office and the University Union Information Desk are open Monday-Friday, 8:00 am – 5:00 pm and the University Union Information Desk is also open on the weekends from 10:00 am – 7:00 pm on Saturdays and Noon – 7:00 pm on Sundays. To better navigate the campus, we recommend that visitors print and bring a campus map prior to visiting campus. Before you begin: Cal Poly takes pride in being an open campus and happily welcomes visitors all year long.  However, if you would like to view a class in session, it is requested that you contact the department or college you wish to observe to make prior arrangements. We appreciate your cooperation as unplanned visitors to a classroom can cause serious disruptions to the learning and teaching process.

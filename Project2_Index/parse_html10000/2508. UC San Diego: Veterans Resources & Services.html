We can help you find the many available services and get you to the right office or people to meet your specific needs. Every year we host welcome and appreciation get-togethers for veterans and military service students, bringing together faculty and staff from across the campus. Interested in applying? Need more information? Sign up to connect with our veterans/military service specialists. Then take a look at the Transfer or Freshmen tab at the top of the page for academic requirements.

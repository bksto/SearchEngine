Acceptance of payments by mail or online at my.csudh.edu as an alternative to payments in person at the Cashiers Office windows. Starting on April 2, 2015 a 2.75% non-refundable convenience fee ($3 minimum) will be charged to all credit card payments. This fee goes directly to the third party processor TouchNet® PayPathTM. Credit and debit cards will not be accepted at the cashier’s window and students who want to pay by credit card will need to do so online at MY.CSUDH.EDU. Students are fully responsible for meeting the payment deadline. Students will not receive a bill. Failure to make a full payment or establish a payment plan by the deadline may result in dis-enrollment from courses. Students who did not apply for Financial Aid or are not meeting the Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) Standards are required to pay their own fees or establish a payment plan by deadline or they may be dis-enrolled from courses. Students should check their account at my.csudh.edu Important information regarding Student fees can be found in the Class Schedule: Student Fee Payment Deadlines and Refund Information Term specific fees information Term specific refund information - pro-rata refund percentages Western University Exchange Fees (WUE) Employee/Dependent Fee Waivers The CSU makes every effort to keep student costs to a minimum. Fees listed in published schedules or student accounts may need to be increased when public funding is inadequate. Therefore, CSU must reserve the right, even after initial fee payments are made, to increase or modify any listed fees, without notice, until the date when instruction for a particular semester or quarter has begun. All CSU listed fees should be regarded as estimates that are subject to change upon approval by The Board of Trustees.

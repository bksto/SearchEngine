Watch the full discussion here » Twitter Tweets from https://twitter.com/BakerInstitute/bi-levant-program About The Program >> Building on the Baker Institute’s long engagement with the Levant as a core component of the institute’s Center for the Middle East, the Levant Program examines the domestic politics and political economy of Syria, Lebanon, Iraq and Jordan. The program additionally explores these states’ relations with the wider region and, more broadly, with the United States, the European Union and Russia. Employing original data and in-country fieldwork, the Levant Program provides practitioners, scholars and policymakers with substantive research publications.

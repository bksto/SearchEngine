In the middle of one of the most vibrant global cities, with accomplished faculty and extensive resources, UCLA is one of the most desirable academic destinations in the world. The UCLA International Education Office offers a range of different programs for international students. Between Summer Study and Reciprocity Exchange programs, we're bound to identify the right means for accommodating an enriching multicultural educational experience. Our international student and scholar community is 5,000 strong. And providing these talented individuals with useful resources is paramount to assist their acclimation to UCLA. The Dashew Center provides a wide variety of services, resources and programs, from assistance with immigration regulations to orientation and English Conversation programs. At UCLA, you're not alone—even when you're far from home.

Join one of our many arts organizations, reach out to local students in the Cambridge and Boston communities, or launch your own start-up business. Take a Wintersession course in a field you’ve always been curious about. Become active in your House programming and find new ways to connect with your fellow residents. Take a tai chi class in the middle of Tercentenary Theater. There are thousands of ways to deepen and enrich your years at Harvard; we hope you will count them among the most challenging, rewarding, and happiest times of your life. Begin exploring the possibilities here.

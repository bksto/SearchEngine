The research so many faculty members have applied and the international reputation many have earned has turned the university into a strong and steady resource for the Inland Empire and beyond as CSUSB helps meet the challenge of living and working in this growing region. Using the Guide Any school, club, media representative, professional organization or public service group may contact the experts listed in this guide. If you cannot make contact directly or cannot find a specific topic that meets your needs, call the public affairs office, (909) 537-5007, we will be glad to help. While many of the experts listed make appearances and presentations as a civic or community service, others find that extensive preparation or travel make it necessary to charge a fee. We recommend the matter of expenses and honoraria be arranged with the individual and confirmed in writing to avoid misunderstanding. Most of the people listed in this guide are full-time faculty and staff members. Therefore, we are grateful for this important service they so willingly agree to perform beyond their usual responsibilities. This directory of faculty experts and speakers bureau is maintained by the public affairs office. Please contact us with comments, additions or corrections at (909) 537-5007 or e-mail Joe Gutierrez at joeg@csusb.edu.

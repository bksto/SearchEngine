Duration: 5 years OpenX (oaid, oageo, oacblock, oasccap) We use OpenX for some advertising campaigns on StatCounter. These cookies are unique identifiers for a browser so we know if a specific ad has been displayed. This helps us to avoid repeatedly showing you the same advert. No browsing history or other info is stored in these cookies. The information in these cookies is not shared with third parties. Please note that opting out of a network does not mean you will no longer see online advertising.

proposal development), please see the Office of Research section within the Faculty Intranet. Research Listserv Faculty interested in receiving funding opportunity announcements, information related to externally funded research, funding support opportunities, and other resources are encouraged to sign up on the COE-RESEARCH-FUNDING LISTSERV To subscribe Send an email (leave the subject blank) to LISTSERV@LISTSERV.UH.EDU with the command: SUBSCRIBE COE-RESEARCH-FUNDING You will then receive an email from the email address above that you have been added to the list.

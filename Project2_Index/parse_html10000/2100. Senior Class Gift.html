Class of 2015 Campaign This fundraising campaign challenges the Class of 2015 to make a leadership gift that represents their class year. All seniors are encouraged to participate to ensure the success of this program while making a difference for tomorrow’s students. Live up to the challenge. The Class of 2015 voted to raise money for additional hydration stations for the campus. Thank you to those who have contributed to the Senior Gift. Please visit our list of Class of 2015 Donors. Once a Toro, Always A Toro Prior classes left their legacy: The Class of 2014 supported the University Fitness Center to ensure continued maintenance and enhancement of the facility. The Class of 2013 supported the University Library so that additional iPads can be purchased and meet the demands of the laptop loan program. The Class of 2012 left their legacy by donating two scholarships to the winners of ASI Toro Tuesdays. What is a Senior Class Gift? It is a fundraising campaign among the Class of 2015 seniors that raises money to support undergraduate financial aid, student group and community center funding, and academic programs. The Senior Class Gift has an immediate and lasting impact on the next generation of CSUDH students. The money does not go toward an endowment. It will be spent immediately and used to help current students. By making a gift of $20.15 or more to the iGave campaign, you will help CSUDH continue to meet the needs of students. How to Give Pick up a 2015 Class Gift form at the University Bookstore, ASI, and the Office of Alumni Relations. Fill out the form and pay with cash or check. Make checks payable to "CSUDH Foundation" and write "Senior Gift" in the memo section. Make a gift now by visiting the secure website at www. csudh.edu/onlinegiving. Visa or MasterCard accepted. Show proof of a $20.15+ donation to the Office of Alumni and Family Programs, WH-C490, and receive a free T-shirt! Can’t pay now but would still like to participate? Make a pledge! You will have 90 days from the day you make your pledge to fulfill your gift. When Can I Participate You can contribute anytime through May 15, 2015. Give Now. Building a Legacy One Student at a Time Your participation in the 2015 Senior Class Gift is what's most important, and imagine the impact the Class of 2015 could make if every senior made a gift of $20.15. Collectively, you and your classmates could raise more than $50,000. Make your gift today by clicking here. Remember, you are students today, but alumni forever.

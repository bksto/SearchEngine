Question 1: How can I obtain a copy of my transcript, obtain a copy of my diploma, or receive degree verification for another university or employer? A: Visit the Registrar online at http://registrar.ucr.edu or contact the office by phone at 951-827-7284. [top] Question 2: Where do I go to retrieve letters of recommendation on file? A: Visit the Career Center online at http://careers.ucr.eduor contact the office by phone at 951-827-3631. [top] Question 3: How do I become a member of the Alumni Association and what are the benefits? A: There are three easy ways to join. You can join online at www.alumni.ucr.edu/membership, call us at 951-827-2586/800-426-2586 or visit us at the Alumni & Visitors Center on campus. Members receive the UCR Magazine, gain access to all UC campus libraries and enjoy discounts off travel and entertainment. For more information, please go to http://alumni.ucr.edu/benefits. [top] Question 4: How do I contact other alumni? A: You may contact our office to see if we have current information for the alumnus/a you are searching for and if we do we can send him/her a contact letter with your information to contact you. [top] Question 5: My webmail account was deactivated. What do I do? A: The Office of Computing & Communications is responsible for all UCR Webmail accounts and will be able to provide you with assistance. [top] Question 6: What alumni events are taking place in my area? A: Please visit the Club and Chapter Events page: http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/events [top] Question 7: How do I give to the alumni scholarship fund? A: Please visit the Scholarship page: http://www.alumni.ucr.edu/involved/scholarships [top] Question 8: How do I get my Alumni Association discount for classes at the UCR Extension Center? A: Please contact the Office of Alumni & Constituent Relations at 951-827-2586 for the appropriate code. [top] Question 9: How do I purchase alumni gear? A: UCR alumni gear can be purchased at the UCR campus bookstore. UCRAA members receive save 10% on clothing and emblematic items. Join Now!

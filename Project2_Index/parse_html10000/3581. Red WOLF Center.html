State of the Art Fitness Facility Opened in 2010, the Red W.O.L.F. Center is an $18 million state of the art fitness facility. The center includes an indoor track, three basketball courts, a MAC gym (soccer), a dance studio, free weight and cardiovascular areas, a spin studio, and assessment rooms.

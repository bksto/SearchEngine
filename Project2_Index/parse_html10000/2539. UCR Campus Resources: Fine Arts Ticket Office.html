Find Your Seat The UC Riverside Fine Arts Ticket Office is your source for tickets to the UC Riverside Presents performing arts series as well as on-campus student performances in dance, music and theatre. We're located in front of the University Theatre. University Theatre Box Office hours: Monday through Friday 10 a.m.–4 p.m. and one hour prior to show time. Walk-up tickets for performances at the Arts Building are generally available one hour prior to show time at that venue, not at the University Theatre Box Office.   Order Tickets Now More Information  General Campus Information University of California, Riverside 900 University Ave. Riverside, CA 92521 Tel: (951) 827-1012 UCR Library Campus Status Campus Store Career Opportunities Diversity Maps and Directions Visit UCR Additional Information Campus Events Calendar Visit UCR's Facebook page Follow UCR on Twitter Visit UCR's YouTube channel Subscribe to UCR Today via RSS Related Links UCR Student Life Associated Students Program Board Intercollegiate Athletics Schedule a Tour Campuswide Navigation UCR Home About UCR Academics Athletics Happenings Research Campus Resources Giving to UCR Footer Feedback Privacy Policy Terms and Conditions © 2010 Regents of the University of California Last modified: 2010-Nov-16

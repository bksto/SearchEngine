The Alumni Association of the University of Michigan provides a community for verified U-M alumni and students to network, gain career advice and professional development. Please keep posts relevant and on topic. Welcome to the community! About this Group Created: September 19, 2007 Type: Alumni Group Members: 36,589 Owner: Lily J. Managers: Alexa A., Laura T., Jenny H., Jordan E., Kate M., Website: http://www.umalumni.com Group Rules This is a closed group intended for University of Michigan alumni, students and U-M community members to create virtual and real-world connections to enhance professional development. Official acceptance into the group is closely monitored and official University records are consulted when admitting members. We welcome career and professional-development related conversations and discussions to make this an effective place for the U-M community to connect. We have put a few policies into place to help frame discussions. Please use the “Promotions” tab to highlight services and products that are relevant to this group and clearly promote a U-M connection. Commercial advertisements will not be accepted. Topics should relate to professional development, career and U-M news. Repeated posts from an individual’s personal or professional blog or website will be removed. Periodically, the Alumni Association of the University of Michigan will advertise relevant events, programs and career development products that may benefit alumni through "discussions". Please do not flag these items as "promotions". Please use the "jobs" tab to advertise job postings or open positions. Profanity, personal attacks and hate speech are prohibited. This policy is in place to help conversations grow and promote discussion. Membership may be revoked without notification if the member violates the group rules. Please help us keep this group clean by flagging any inappropriate posts that violate these group rules. This group has been verified by LinkedIn.

This partnership helps UCLA identify potential corporate funders, and also provides our campus with opportunities to connect with complementary projects. Another resource available to UCLA's faculty and student body, as well as industry scientists, is the Faculty Research and Expertise service. This database provides descriptions and links to over 3,000 entries, and assists researchers in finding collaborators.

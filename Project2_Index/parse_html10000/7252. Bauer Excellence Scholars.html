Excellence Luncheon Excellence scholars sit down to lunch with Dean Ramchand The Bauer Excellence Initiative They are a diverse group, with a range of interests in studies, research and career. The Bauer Excellence Scholarship Initiative Houston is emerging as one of the world’s foremost business environments, and the C. T. Bauer College of Business is training a workforce to sustain the city’s global advantage. The Bauer Excellence Initiative is a recruiting scholarship campaign aimed at attracting high-quality applicants to the college’s programs. Supporting our students with Tier One resources, programs and scholarships will help pave the way for new generations of global business leaders. This section of the website is meant to serve as a centralized location for information about The Bauer Excellence Initiative, both for current scholars, prospective students, potential donors, and others.

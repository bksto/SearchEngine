Change Your NetID PasswordMarch 7, 2015 We are currently investigating a report from an employee of a serious compromise of personal data. To best protect your personal information, we are asking all UMass Amherst faculty, staff, students, and graduate students to spend a few minutes to change their NetID password.  For questions, contact the IT Help Center (it@umass.edu) or the IT professional in your department, as applicable.

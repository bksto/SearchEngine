You must bring valid legal photo ID with you and a pen/pencil to your appointment. To begin the assessment process: Start by submitting a new or updated application to attend the upcoming semester. Go to Orientation. Select and complete the New Student Online Orientation. If you complete the Orientation as a GUEST you will NOT receive credit for it.  You must complete it as a student in order to receive credit. There will be a survey to complete at the end of the orientation. There are no certificates of completion.  View the CHC Assessment Calendar and choose a day/time for assessment.  Then, click on the calendar day you choose for more information on CHC Assessment. *Be sure to arrive fully prepared as you will only be able to test once in two (2) years.

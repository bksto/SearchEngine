MISSION: The mission of the Task Force is to examine issues, programs, and services that support AB 540 and undocumented students including university policies, the admission process, retention and graduation. The Task Force will explore the campus climate to identify potential barriers that impede the academic success of AB 540 and undocumented students. The Task Force will support and advocate for these students and make recommendations regarding university policies. Download the AB540 Task Force Roster.

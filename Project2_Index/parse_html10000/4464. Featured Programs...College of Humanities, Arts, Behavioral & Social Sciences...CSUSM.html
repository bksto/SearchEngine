Learn more about "Career Readiness Initiative" The Cal State DC Program allows students to enroll in full-time academic units while interning in Washington! Internships can be with congressional offices, executive agencies, political parties, advocacy groups, non-profits, and a variety of other offices in and around Washington, D.C. Juniors, seniors, and graduate students from all majors are encouraged to apply. Learn more at an upcoming Information Session!

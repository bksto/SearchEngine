Navigation Graduate School of Arts and Science About Arts and Science | Departments and Programs | The Faculty About GSAS|Programs|Admissions|Students|Alumni Home > About GSAS About GSAS Welcome to the Graduate School of Arts and Science (GSAS) of New York University - a portal to exploration, discovery, and opportunity. GSAS offers a broad spectrum of programs including doctoral, master's and joint degree programs as well as global programs and advanced certificates. Founded in 1886, the Graduate School of Arts and Science is one of the oldest graduate schools in the United States, and we build on a long tradition of excellence while looking to the future: our aim is to prepare the world's next generation of scholars, thinkers, and leaders. Ready to learn more? View the Dean's Message, join us at a graduate fair or open house, or learn more about our admissions process.

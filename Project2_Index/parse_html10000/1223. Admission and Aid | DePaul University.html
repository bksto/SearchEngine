DePaul may award scholarships​ to newly entering students who exhibit academic excellence, artistic talent or strong leadership or service to their communities.  Each year, DePaul awards more than $24 million in scholarships. (Eligible music and theatre applicants may be considered for academic as well as talent scholarships.) Financial Aid DePaul seeks to manage its resources effectively in order to control the costs it charges students for programs and services. Recent tuition increases have been held to between 5 and 7 percent, less than the majority of our peer institutions. ​Moreover, we offer a full range of financial aid opportunities to ensure access to a diverse population of students.  ​​ Interested in becoming a DePaul student?

Your Gift to UCR Makes a Difference Give to UCR and you’ll be making a lasting contribution, furthering our mission to provide life-changing teaching and world-changing research. You’ll be investing in the promise of our students and faculty, and providing an excellent start for tomorrow’s scientists, engineers and entrepreneurs. You’ll be putting your trust in the potential of students at the University of California’s most diverse campus, which is trying to make a world-class education accessible to all promising students, regardless of their economic background. Ways of Donating There are many ways to show your support of UCR, and our Office of Gift Planning is ready to work with you to meet your goals. You choose the way in which you want to donate and the area to which you want to see your gift appropriated. Learn about areas you can support. Make a Gift Now Giving to UCR online is easy. Just choose the program(s) you wish to support, tell us how you would like to pay, and receive an online confirmation that you can print for your records. Make your online donation now or call the Office of Development at (951) 827-3486 or e-mail us .

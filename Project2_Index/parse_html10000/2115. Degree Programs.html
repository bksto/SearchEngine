Covering a wide range of fields and disciplines, CSUDH undergraduate degrees have many qualities in common: Rigorous and well-rounded curriculum, dedicated and expert faculty, unique research and internship opportunities, and a practical education that prepares you for success beyond the classroom. DEGREE ROADMAPS Want to know how long it will take to finish your degree? Curious about what courses you’ll need to complete? Our Degree Roadmaps provide a useful online tool for planning your path to degree completion and graduation.   GRADUATE DEGREES Ready to advance your education? Tailored for working adults, CSUDH’s graduate degree programs are designed to help you reach your professional and academic goals while balancing your other obligations. Graduate students collaborate closely with faculty mentors, learn in small supportive cohorts, put classroom theory into workplace practice, and enjoy access to scholarships and grants.   READY TO APPLY? If you’ve already found your degree program, it’s time to get ready to apply. Whether you’re applying as a first-time freshman, transfer student, graduate student, or international student, we’ll walk you through the application process step-by-step. ACADEMIC ADVISING If you’re a first-time freshman or transfer student planning to complete a CSUDH degree, be sure to schedule your academic advising to make sure you’re taking the right classes for your degree path. CREDENTIAL PROGRAMS From basic teaching credentials, to service credentials, to special education credentials and state certificates, CSUDH’s credential programs will help you meet the requirements you need to become a qualified educator in California. Learn more about becoming an educator or teacher at CSUDH’s School of Education website.   CERTIFICATE PROGRAMS If you’re seeking professional development for career advancement, take a closer look at our wide selection of onsite and online certificate programs, many offered through our College of Extended Education. We also work closely with employers to develop customized training programs to meet specific workforce needs.

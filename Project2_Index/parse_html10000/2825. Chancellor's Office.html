Biography of the Chancellor Dr. Tim Hudson took office as the second chancellor of Arkansas State University in Jonesboro on May 1, 2012. He was appointed by Dr. Charles L. Welch, president of the Arkansas State University System. He leads the campus as we strive for progressive excellence in academics, research, fiscal management and student life.

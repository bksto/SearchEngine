Our Mission To serve students and the campus community by providing quality service that facilitates enrollment, retention and degree completion, while maintaining accurate academic records for the University. Our Vision The Registrar’s Office will be a respected partner in the University’s mission to prepare students for learning, service and leadership.  With the highest level of integrity, the department will create a friendly and welcoming environment in which to serve all campus constituents.  Adhering to the University’s polytechnic philosophy of learn by doing, the Office will explore use of best practices and advanced technologies to provide the most efficient and effective delivery of enrollment, record and matriculation services.

The intellectual enterprise at NYU is characterized by a diversity of viewpoints, including voices of affirmation, of dissent, and of protest; at NYU a voice that is silent elsewhere is heard, and the value of the message is tested on its merit. Whatever the level of study and whatever the background, NYU students are part of a brand new journey in higher education.

How to register as a job-seeking MBA on MBA Focus On-Campus Recruiting Orientation On-Campus Recruiting (OCR) is a program that allows students to interview with employers on-campus for internship and full-time positions. To be considered for an interview opportunity, students must indicate their interest by submitting their resume online via the Bauer Career Gateway system. To learn more about how the On-Campus Recruiting program works, please review the short On-Campus Recruiting Orientation (2 sections, each about 4 minutes). Section 1 - Campus Recruitment Section 2 - Preparing for Your Interview NOTE: Students are strongly encouraged to complete this orientation to fully understand how the On-Campus Recruiting program works. For more information, please contact us at hirebauer@uh.edu or 832-842-6120.

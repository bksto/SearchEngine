Stay for a stellar education. At CSUDH, we believe the best way to learn about our campus and programs is to visit us in person. We welcome the opportunity to show you around our beautiful 346-acre campus, tell you about our excellent academic programs and degrees, provide information about financial aid and housing options, introduce you to faculty, staff and current students, extend our help with applying to CSUDH, show you a slice of campus life, and answer any questions you may have. We offer potential and new students, as well as their families, a number of options for visiting and touring our campus. CAMPUS TOURS From individual tours, to group visits, to self-guided tours, we’re ready to welcome you to our campus community. Be our guest as our friendly, knowledgeable tour guides introduce you to our facilities, student services and resources, and the campus itself. Schedule your tour or visit now. OPEN HOUSE EVENTS If you have been admitted to CSUDH for Fall 2015, you’re invited to join us on campus for our Explore Dominguez Hills preview day on Saturday, April 18, 2015.

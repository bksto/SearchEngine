It is home to a culturally, racially, and internationally diverse population of more than 100,000. There is also a long history of supporting and sharing an amazingly talented and diverse art community. Berkeley has a strong community feel, with easy access to the major metropolitan cities of Oakland and San Francisco just a short commute away.

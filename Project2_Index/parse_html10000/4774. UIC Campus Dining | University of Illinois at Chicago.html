Perhaps something more substantial? Either way, you’ll find a variety of comfortable dining venues throughout the UIC campus, including UIC Dining Services facilities, “All-You-Care-To-Eat” locations, the Dean’s Room, plus various fast food options (Burrito Bravo, Café Descartes, Frank N’ Fries, Port Center Café, Sbarro, Subway and Wendy’s).   And don’t forget about Dragon Dollar$, the debit account program that links to your i-card. Dragon Dollar$ can be used at any campus eatery. For more information, visit the Dining Services website. Back to Top Michael Pharmacy Meet Michael, a second-year pharmacy student.

The campus community is known as a friendly one, where differences are embraced and students have the freedom to express themselves openly. Our professors recognize that learning goes beyond the classroom and often can be found hosting events in their homes, eating lunch with groups of students or encouraging them to have their own out-of-class discussions and gatherings to explore and debate material introduced in class. We want to help you explore everything Redlands has to offer. For a snapshot of our highlights, check out the Why Redlands page.

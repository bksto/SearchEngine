Our Tier One faculty are world-renowned scholars with real-world experience in our communities. In addition to innovative, federally funded research projects, we have more American Education Research Association (AERA) fellows than any other Texas university.  Our alumni have deep ties in the community and continue to make an impact felt well beyond graduation.

The University of Redlands is proud to offer professional development  free of charge to alumni of the College of Arts and Sciences, School of Business and School of Education.   Professional Development genuinely cares about your professional success- one more of many reasons why it’s so great to be a Bulldog for Life! The Online Career Center offers a suite of award-winning tools designed and customized for you by experts. With these tools, you can easily build a resume, cover letter, online portfolio or professional website.  A video resume option and real-life multimedia scenarios also are offered.  Learn about and network with employers who recruit through the University of Redlands, too. Going Global  career and employment resources include more than 10,000 pages of constantly updated content on topics such as:  job search sources, work permit/visa regulations, resume writing guidelines and examples, employment trends, salary ranges, networking groups, cultural/interviewing advice… and much more!  Take time to explore the USA/Canada Career Guides, job and internship opportunities and key employer directory of more than 400,000 country-specific company profiles in a variety of industries. Online resources are available 24/7.   Alumni of the College of Arts and Sciences and School of Education may contact Professional Development via email at cascareerservices@redlands.edu.  School of Business alumni may email sbcareerservices@redlands.edu for more information.   Home Connect Bulldog Showcase Professional Development Maroon & Grey Student Ambassadors Regional Alumni Chapters For Parents Traditions Serve Alumni Awards Nominations Annual Bulldogs in Service Hire a Bulldog President’s Circle Leadership Committee Tag Week Give Make a Gift Now The Power of the Redlands Fund President's Circle Leadership Committee Leadership Giving Endowment Planned Giving Town & Gown Scholarships for Inland Empire Students Meet the Development Team Join Bulldog Bench Events All Events Alumni & Friends Travel Town & Gown Events Johnston Events Alumni Founders Weekend 2015 Town & Gown Town & Gown Join and Support Town & Gown Awards of Distinction Calendar of Events History and Governance Scholarship Endowment Contact Us Johnston Alumni Activities and Hub The Coz McNooz Newsletter Support Johnston Alumni Assoc.

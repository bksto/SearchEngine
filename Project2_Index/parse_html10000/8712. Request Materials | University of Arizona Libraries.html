Select the "Request this Item" button from the catalog record and we'll pull it from our shelves and place it on hold for you. We can also mail books and other library materials to distance students and faculty for free. Request a book by mail. Checked-out Recall it by selecting "Request this Item" from the catalog record. We'll notify you when it is ready to pick up (within 10 days). You can also submit an interlibrary loan request for the item. Not on the shelf Ask for help at any of our information desks. We can search for it and notify you when we find it. Something we don't own Submit an interlibrary loan request and we'll try to get it from another library. Suggest a purchase if you think we should add it to our permanent collection. Streaming Video (instructors only) If you would like to show a film in your class, whether it's one we already own or need to buy, we can investigate streaming options. Learn more about video streaming. Request a streaming video Distance Students & Faculty  We can ship library items to UA faculty, staff, and students who reside more than 30 miles from the University for free. Be sure to enter your mailing address as the delivery location when requesting materials.

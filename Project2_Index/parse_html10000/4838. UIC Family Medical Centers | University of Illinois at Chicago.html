School Events Calendar Student Life / Student Services / Family Medical Centers Students enrolled at UIC are eligible to receive many preventative outpatient services at the Family Medicine Center, including early treatment of illness and injury, and testing for pregnancy and a number of sexually transmitted diseases. Read more about Student Health. There is no co-payment or charge for these services; they are covered by the Student Health Service fee in your tuition. However, please note, these basic services are not the same as the more comprehensive, primary care services covered through the CampusCare plan. Family Medicine Center University Village 722 W. Maxwell St., 2nd Floor Chicago, IL 60607 (312) 996-2901 For more information, visit the Family Medicine Center website.

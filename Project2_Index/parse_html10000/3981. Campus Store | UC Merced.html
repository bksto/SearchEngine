In addition, The Campus Store has joined forces with Apple Computers to provide the best products at academic pricing. The store offers an array of books, course materials, computers, apparel and food.  Books and Class Materials: The Campus Store brings a modern touch to the tradition of high-quality bookstore service with online ordering. New or used textbooks can be purchased through the Campus Store website. You can shop from the comfort of your own home — or anywhere with internet access. Books will be delivered to campus, bundled together by our staff, and marked with your name and ID for pick up in the Textbook Annex. At the end of the semester, the bookstore buys back used textbooks for cash t o you at the best possible price. With academic pricing, there is no need to shop anywhere else. Apple Computers & Electronics The Campus Store has partnered with Apple to provide the best quality computers, iPods and accessories to you at the best possible price. With Academic Pricing, there is no need to shop anywhere else. The UC Merced Campus Store also carries a large assortment of batteries, calculators, cables and peripherals to link you up to the digital lifestyle student’s demand. Lantern Café: Located on the first floor of the Kolligian Library, the Campus Store and Lantern Café are sure to be a hub of activity. The cafe will serve up top-quality brewed coffee and your favorite espresso drinks. You can also choose from a variety of café-style menu items. Spirit Gear and More: Find all of your Golden Bobcat gear at the UC Merced apparel headquarters: T-shirts, sweatshirts, hats and more. Decorations for your room and convenience items are available, too. The Campus Store accepts cash, checks, credit cards and the Cat Card.

UC Riverside offers a variety of on-campus housing options including three residence hall communities, seven apartment communities and a 55-acre community for students with families. Some living styles include fully furnished rooms and the option of a dining plan. Learn More About Your Housing Options See The Answer Can I afford it? You can afford UCR! Not only are we a Top 25 Best Value School, we rank higher than any other UC campus. Eighty-three percent of our undergraduate students receive financial aid. Of those students, 70% have all of their system-wide fees covered with scholarships and grants. See How Affordable it is to Attend UC Riverside Learn More About Scholarships, Grants and Loans See The Answer Will I get accepted? UC Riverside seeks extraordinary students and admission is competitive. We consider your academic record, test scores and the challenge of your coursework. We give special consideration to first-generation college students and those from low-income families. We also offer programs that guarantee your admission. See Admission Requirements and Learn More About Our Selection Process. Guarantee Your Admission See The Answer When are the deadlines? Remember, November! Submit your UC application for undergraduate admission, November 1-30. Other dates and deadlines, plus detailed timeline checklists, can be found on our Dates and Deadlines page.

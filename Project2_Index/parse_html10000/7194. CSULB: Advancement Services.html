As part of University Relations and Development, Advancement Services supports and enhances the university’s development activities through management of the data infrastructure, reporting mechanisms, prospect research and management, gift and pledge processing and technical support. Advancement Services maintains all the records of the university’s fundraising activities, including fundraising efforts conducted outside University Relations and Development. Alumni Records Office The Alumni Records Office organizes, maintains and updates the records of more than 300,000 graduates, parents, friends and community organizations. Staff members collect and update biographical information such as names, addresses, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses and business information. The office serves both University Relations and Development and the entire campus community. Alumni may submit biographical data changes via e-mail to: alumnirecords@csulb.edu. Updates help assure prompt delivery of alumni communications, including the Beach Connection and the Beach Review. Development Information Systems Office Maintaining and supporting the biographical database at California State University, Long Beach is the responsibility of the Development Information Systems Office. Database specialists ensure information is recorded properly, as well as develop reporting tools for University Relations and Development. Office of Prospect Management and Research Prospect management encourages strategic, focused communication between prospective donors and the university community. Prospect research helps identify and qualify individuals, corporations and foundations with potential to donate to the university. Through these efforts, the Office of Prospect Management & Research assists University Relations and Development in securing support for the initiatives and educational mission of California State University, Long Beach.  Division Technical Support California State University, Long Beach is committed to alumni and donor confidentiality.

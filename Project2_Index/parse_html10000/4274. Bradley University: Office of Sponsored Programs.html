Masi-- International Scholars award deadline : Friday, April 3rd.  Mission and Purpose of the Office of Sponsored Programs (OSP) Mission: The Office of Sponsored Programs supports Bradley faculty and administrators in the pre-award processes of seeking and securing external funds to support their research, scholarship, and creative work, to enhance their curricular activities and pedagogy, promote their professional development, and in other ways support the mission, vision, and strategic plan for Bradley University. Goals: The Office of Sponsored Programs seeks to meet its mission by addressing the following goals: 1. To increase the number and dollar amount of sponsored programs requests submitted to external funders by members of Bradley’s faculty and administration each year; and 2.

London and Spain are waiting for you!  Picture yourself living in London during the fall or Salamanca, Spain in the spring as you continue your college studies. Plan now to secure your enrollment in the Crafton Hills College semester abroad program for the upcoming year. The semester abroad program at Crafton Hills College is entering its 25th year as a member of the Southern California Foothills Consortium  for Study Abroad.  As a member of the consortium, CHC partners with Citrus College in Glendora to offer a variety of semester abroad programs including semesters in both London and Salamanca.  Eligibility requirements include a minimum of 12 completed college credits with a 2.5 GPA, and financial aid is available to eligible students. Program announcements and enrollment deadlines occur  the semester before the start of the intended study abroad program. Students interested in participating in the study abroad program can visit the Citrus College website for current information and application materials. Brochures and application materials may also be available in the library or faculty wing of the SSA building adjacent to EOPS. Learn more about the experience of spending a semester abroad in London or Salamanca.

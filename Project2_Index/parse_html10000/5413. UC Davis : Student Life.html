Take part in the Aggie active lifestyle! We're proud of the many opportunities our campus provides for academic enrichment, recreation, community service, and much more! Student government Exercise your political savvy and get involved with Associated Students of UC Davis or ASUCD. Aggie Pack Join the largest organized student spirit group in the country and root for your fellow Aggies at events like the Causeway Classic, the Aggies' longstanding football rivalry with the Sacramento State University Hornets, or the Battle for the Golden Horseshoe with Cal Poly Pomona.

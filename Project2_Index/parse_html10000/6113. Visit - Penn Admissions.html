There is an energy here that can only be appreciated in person. We offer a variety of ways for you to get to know our dynamic community. Campus tours, led by current Penn students, are the best source of information about Penn's history and traditions, academic experience, and residential life. To delve deeper, you can attend a class or a school-specific information session. Your visit will culminate in an information session with an admissions officer, who will guide you through the application process, provide an overview of Penn's all-grant financial aid policies, and address any specific questions or concerns you might have. In short: there are many things to do and we encourage you to explore. Group Visits Groups of fifteen or more should visit our group tour page for information.

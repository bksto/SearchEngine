Interactive Campus Map Directions to UCSB The UCSB Visitor Center is located adjacent to the Mesa Parking Structure 18 on the ground floor of the Student Affairs Administrative Services Building (SAASB). Get directions to Ocean Road at Mesa Road, Goleta, CA 93106 Getting to UCSB The University of California, Santa Barbara is located 100 miles northwest of Los Angeles, California and can be easily accessed by plane, train and car: The Santa Barbara Municipal Airport (SBA) is less than two miles from the UCSB campus. Taxis from SBA to UCSB usually cost $18 for the one-way trip so please bring cash.  Amtrak trains service the area with terminals in downtown Santa Barbara and in Goleta. Directions from Highway 101 North or South Parking on Campus Visitors must purchase and display a parking permit. Please park in designated Student/Visitor parking lots or structures, which are identified by lot entrance signs. Permits are sold at the self-pay machines in each lot and parking structure. For information on where to park on campus, visit Transportation and Parking Services. Parking Map (PDF) Download the campus map for information on where to park when visiting the UCSB campus.

Welcome to San Bernardino Valley College where we offer a wide variety of specialized services!  The programs, services and facilities provided by our various departments are designed to create a stimulating and supportive environment that enhances the personal development, learning, educational success and career preparation of all students. Our departments provide Disability Services (DSP&S), Student Health Services, Veteran's Services, Valley-Bound Commitment, EOP&S/CARE, CalWORKs, Puente Project, Tumaini Program, Success Through Achievement & Retention (STAR) and International Student Services.

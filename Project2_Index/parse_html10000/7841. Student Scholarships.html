The Alumni Association awards almost $20,000 annually to new and continuing students. UCSB Alumni Association Scholarship Fund awarded $1,000 to 10 students in Fall 2009 based on good academic standing and financial need. Alumni Legacy Scholarship awards $1,000 to students who are relatives of UC Santa Barbara alumni. Application deadline Nov. 24, 2014. Buchanan Award presents $500 each year to a graduating senior majoring in History. Cheadle Award provides $300 to an outstanding student who shares Dr. Cheadle's enthusiasm and passion for research and scholarship. Frame My Future Scholarship: Church Hill Classics is giving back to students interested in pursuing their dreams with a college education If you are interested in being a part of Gauchos Giving to Gauchos, and donating to the Alumni Association Scholarship Fund, please visit our Giving section or go directly to our secure online giving form. .

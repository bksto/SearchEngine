Email and Data Policies Use of the bConnected platform, the types of data that can be stored and communicated online, and other campus- and UC-wide security and privacy policies are available.  bConnected is the Berkeley name for our campus-wide collaboration tools. bConnected includes: Google Apps for Education (bMail, bCal and bDrive), Box, CalShare & bCourses Project Sites. Read more about each of these tools below: GOOGLE APPS FOR EDUCATION With a single login, the online bConnected service provides access to all your campus communication tools from your desk, your smart phone, or anywhere you have internet access. Each bConnected account provides  email (known as bMail), including an IM Instant Message 'chat' feature  calendar (known as bCal) bDrive for document storage and sharing (Google Docs) Contacts, where you can create and save lists based on the campus email directory and/or your own email contacts Learn more about Google Apps for Education BOX Berkeley Box is a cloud-hosted platform that allows users to easily store and share documents, photos, research materials and other files for collaboration. Berkeley Box is available to all current faculty, staff and students. The storage quota assigned to each account holder is 50GB. Learn more about Box BCOURSES bCourses is the learning management system replacement for bSpace. In Spring 2014, campus began the transition to bCourses, Berkeley's new Learning Management System (LMS) powered by Canvas. Over the next two years, Educational Technology Services (ETS) will guide campus faculty and students in the adoption of bCourses culminating in the eventual retirement of bSpace in the Summer of 2015. Learn more about bCourses CALSHARE CalShare is a tool for creating and managing web sites for collaboration purposes. You can easily create and share sites, documents, images, lists, discussions and surveys.   Learn more about CalShare BROWSER REQUIREMENTS All your tools are available from any computer.

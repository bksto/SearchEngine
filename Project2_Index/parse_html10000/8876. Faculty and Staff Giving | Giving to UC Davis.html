Start your payroll deduction today by filing out one of these forms: Faculty Payroll Deduction Form Staff Payroll Deduction Form UC Davis' dedicated employees work hard every day to ensure every Aggie students receives the most from their educational experience and that UC Davis' land-grant mission of excellence in scholarship, research and public service continues to improve the lives of all citizens of California and the globe. They also are dedicated to the Aggie tradition of giving back. As ardent supporters all throughout The Campaign for UC Davis, faculty and staff have made gifts, both of their time and finances, to support their philanthropic passions — everything from supporting ground-breaking research initiatives and student scholarships to strengthening academic programs, staff scholarships and athletics.  If you are a UC Davis employee, you can join your peers by making a gift to UC Davis today. It is as easy as making an online donation now or enrolling in payroll deduction for faculty and staff. Remember, every gift makes a difference.  For more information on ways you can support your philanthropic passions by giving to UC Davis, view the A-Z listing of online giving funds, download the Giving to UC Davis brochure or contact the UC Davis Office of Development.

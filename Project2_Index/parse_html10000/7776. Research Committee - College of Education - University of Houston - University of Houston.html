The College Research Committee shall be responsible to the Faculty of the College for: recommending policies that encourage and support research; recommending the designation, distribution, and disbursement of available funds to facilitate research in the COE; generating, recommending, reviewing, and disseminating university and college policies affecting research to include: evaluating proposals for COE research opportunities and Faculty release opportunities, and recommending recipients for Faculty research excellence awards; advising the Dean’s office regarding research issues, external funding opportunities, policies, and procedures related to and affecting research; providing input and advice to administration regarding promotional materials and publicity of research activities; and promoting awareness of COE Faculty research. 2014 – 2015 Committee Members: Dr. Ezemenari Obasi  (Chair) Educational Psychology emobasi@uh.edu Dr. Sheng Chung  Curriculum & Instruction skchung@uh.edu Dr. Milena Keller-Margulis Educational Psychology mamargulis@uh.edu Dr. Sissy Wong Curriculum & Instruction sissywong@uh.edu Resources Apply for and Administer Grants Search UH Library Databases Find out more about College of Education Grants Committees for the Protection of Human Subjects Research Administration Management Portal (RAMP) Online Human Subjects Applications, Revisions and Renewals Quick Links Academic Calendar Bookstore Campus Map Get Help Human Resources Library Parking Tuition & Fees More UH A-Z Index Directory Emergency Houston Public Media Jobs at UH President Social Media UH System Legal Info Copyright Fraud Reporting Institutional Résumé MySafeCampus Policies Public Information Act Required Reports State of Texas Twitter Facebook Google Plus LinkedIn Instagram Pinterest Tumblr YouTube University of Houston  ·  4800 Calhoun Rd.

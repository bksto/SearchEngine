The campuses in Urbana-Champaign, Chicago and Springfield offer hundreds of undergraduate, graduate and professional programs taught by internationally respected faculty, including Nobel laureates, Pulitzer prize winners and MacArthur fellows. UIC is also a leader in providing access to an excellent education for students from diverse ethnic/racial groups, religions, sexual orientations, physical conditions, and ages. In fact, UIC is ranked among the top 10 most racially diverse universities in the nation. The University's mission?

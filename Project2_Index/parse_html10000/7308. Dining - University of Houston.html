As a student, your dining options include Starbucks, Chick-fil-A, Pizza Hut and a lot more, in addition to traditional residence-hall restaurants. Online Menus will give you a better idea of the feasts and fare that is available. Student meal plans are flexible. You can choose a weekly or semester meal allowance, and load up your plate as much as you like at each of the meals you choose. Or, sign up for our declining balance plan and use your Cougar Cash to buy food anywhere on campus. UH Dining Launches Mobile Menus APP for Android and iPhone Are you ever in the middle of campus  trying to decide where to go for lunch, but you don’t want to pull out your laptop to check menus? UH Dining Services now offers an app for Android and Apple devices that will give you menus and nutritional information for both residential restaurants on campus.

View My Saved Places in a larger map Getting to Huff House & Founder's Court Visitor Parking Enter campus via Entrance 1, near the intersection of Main Street and Sunset Boulevard.  Drive straight until you reach the stop sign and fork in the road.  If you look to your right at the stop sign, you’ll see Huff House.  Cars parked in the Circle Drive at Huff House are subject to ticketing, so you’ll want to park in the Founder’s Court Visitor Lot.  Turn left at the stop sign, then take an immediate right.  Founder’s Court Lot will be on your left.  A fee is assessed upon exiting the parking lot and payment can only be made via credit card.      ABOUT Welcome Meet the Staff Directions & Parking Historic Huff House Contact   STUDENTS Events Calendar Explore Career Options Jobs & Internships Career Counseling Events & Workshops Guides & Presentations Peer Career Advisors Connect with Alumni & Mentors Graduate Students & Post Docs Resources EMPLOYERS Why Rice?

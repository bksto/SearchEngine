(Update form available only from the upenn.edu domain). Enter a name or word, or simply enter a string of several characters if you don't know an entity's exact name or want to find related entities. For example: Entering chem finds the Biochemistry, Chemistry, and Chemical Engineering departments as well as other entries that have the string of characters "chem" somewhere in their name. Entering art finds Arts and Sciences Computing, Arts House Theatre, Wharton Evening School, and Department of Academic Support Programs as well as other entries that have the string of characters "art" somewhere in their name. To key in on entries that have the exact word art, select the "match exact word" option to find entries like Institute of Contemporary Art and Art and Archaeology. Entering center finds many Penn research centers.

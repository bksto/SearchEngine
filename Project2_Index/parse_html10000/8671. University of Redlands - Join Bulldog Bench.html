Recently, the Bulldog Bench has provided resources for the Baseball Centennial Celebration, equipment for live event web-streaming, upkeep of facilities and much more. To say the least, the Bulldog Bench is vital to our continued success. We invite you to join the Bulldog Bench for an annual fee of $100, with 100 percent of your donation benefitting Bulldog student-athletes. If you graduated in 2010, 2011, 2012, or 2013, then you can become a member for just $50. If you graduated in 2014, you can become a member for exactly $20.14. To show our appreciation of your support, you will receive an official Bulldog Bench window cling and regular updates from Director of Athletics Jeff Martinez and his team.

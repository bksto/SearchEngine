Academics Campus Life Money Matters Beyond CSUSM IN THIS SECTION Alumni Association Putting Education to Work Community Engagement Impact fgj In October and November 2011, Career Centers from 20 of the 23 campuses of the California State University conducted a statewide survey of employers who recruit students for professional postgraduate positions. Nine-hundred seventy eight (978) employers responded to the survey. On average, employers responding to the survey recruited for full time positions on 3.2 CSU campuses, and recruited for internships on 2.5 CSU campuses.

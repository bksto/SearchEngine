Scholars, athletes, student organization members, community activists, volunteers, and all Golden Eagles who have impacted their community in a positive manner are encouraged to apply for this award which recognizes outstanding student achievement. The Golden Eagle Awards of Excellence recipients will be honored at a reception with family, friends and campus dignitaries during the winter quarter. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA for consideration.

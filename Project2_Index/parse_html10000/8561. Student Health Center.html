We offer universal, hassle-free appointment-based and walk-in medical and counseling services at either no cost or very reduced cost to all NYU students, regardless of insurance coverage. Our goal is to add great value to the NYU experience for students and great comfort in knowing that a safety net for basic healthcare exists at NYU. The SHC is accredited by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care, placing it among the top university health centers in the country.

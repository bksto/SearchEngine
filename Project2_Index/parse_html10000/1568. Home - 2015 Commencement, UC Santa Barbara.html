Commencement weekend will be held on June 13-14, 2015 College of Creative Studies: Sunday, June 7, 2015 Bren School of Environmental Science and Management: Friday, June 12, 2015 Commencement Weekend: Saturday, June 13, 2015 and Sunday, June 14, 2015 For a full list of the 2015 Commencement schedule please click here. Undergraduate Registration Step 1: Declare Candidacy on GOLD Declare candidacy on GOLD to receive an official degree from UC Santa Barbara. Step 2: Confirm a reservation in a Commencement ceremony Commencement Pass Ⅰ February 23, 2015 - April 10, 2015 Seniors with at least 164 units completed or in progress during the winter 2015 quarter are eligible to make a reservation for the spring Commencement ceremony assigned to their major on GOLD. Commencement Pass Ⅱ April 13, 2015 - May 22, 2015 Seniors with at least 164 units completed or in progress during the spring 2015 quarter are eligible to make a reservation for any open spring Commencement ceremony on GOLD. Note: During Pass 2, eligible undergraduates may make a reservation in any open and available ceremony, regardless of major. Graduate Registration Doctoral and Master's degree candidates must register by Friday, May 1, 2015. For more information about participating in the Graduate Division Commencement, please visit http://graddiv.ucsb.edu/commencement. The official hashtag of 2015 Commencement is #UCSB2015. Join us on social media to celebrate and congratulate our graduates. If you have questions regarding Commencement, contact the Commencement Hotline at (805) 893-8289 or email Commencement@ia.ucsb.edu.

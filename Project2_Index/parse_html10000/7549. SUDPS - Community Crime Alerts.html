The Stanford Department of Public Safety urges members of the Stanford Community to take safety precautions both on campus and off campus. Be aware of your surroundings Walk and travel with friends. At night, walk in well-lighted pedestrian routes. Carry a cell phone. While on the campus, familiarize yourself with the locations of the tall blue Emergency Telephone Towers and use Suggested Travel Routes whenever possible.

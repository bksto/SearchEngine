The Official Harvard tour departs from the Harvard Information Center, in the Smith Campus Center. The tour is student-led and comprises an outdoor walk (please dress appropriately) through Harvard Yard, providing a history of the university, general information, and a unique view on the student’s individual experience! The tour is around one hour long, and completely free of charge. There is no pre-registration for general visitors, however tours are capped at 35 attendees. We do recommend arriving 30 minutes early to guarantee a spot at your desired time. Currently we are only able to accomodate groups of up-to 14 people on our free, public tour. If you have a group of 15 or more, reservations are required (please see below). Please view the calendar for more details » Please Note: The university does offer another guided tour of the undergraduate campus. Offered through the Admissions Office, this student-led tour of Harvard Yard is designed for prospective students and those visitors wishing to learn more about student life. For more information on these tours, visit the Office of Admissions. Group Tours and Reservations (Private) NOTE: Please review the updated tour fee information here » Reservations are required for tours of 15 or more people, with a absolute maximum of 125 people. We request 2 weeks notice to schedule a tour. Please note, we cannot accommodate groups that arrive without a reservation. Call about the tour schedule: 617-495-1573 or e-mail info_center@harvard.edu. Wheelchairs are available with a week or more of advance notice.  Please note we can take requests from 9:45-3:15 pm, but during our public tour start times, (every hour from 10 a.m to 3 p.m.), we cannot accommodate requests.   Spring tours begin on February 17, 2015.  Spring tours end on May 8, 2015. The Harvard Information Center is open Monday - Friday 9 a.m. - 5 p.m. throughout January, but does not offer Historical Tours during that time. To submit a group tour reservation request please click here  Late Arrivals Reservations will be held for 15 minutes. Tour guides will be released at the discretion of the Information Center. A Self-Guided Walking Tour of Harvard Yard If you are interested in touring the campus on your own you may want a self-guided walking tour. These tour pamphlets, offered in nine different languages, can be purchased from our office for a minimal charge. You may also download audio files of historical information on each of the buildings in Harvard Yard. These files, which together comprise an Audio Tour can be obtained here. If you have a mobile device, you can download our new Mobile Tour. Harvard College Virtual Tour For prospective college students and their families, the Harvard College Virtual Tour offers an exclusive look into Harvard, including dorm rooms, libraries, classrooms, athletic facilities, and more. Visitors are led through the walking tour by video guides and can explore each tour stop in detail through 360-degree panoramas, images, and videos. The tour is accessible on desktop and mobile. You can also download the app on iPhone and Android.

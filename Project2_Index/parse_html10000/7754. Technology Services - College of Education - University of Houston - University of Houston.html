CITE is located on the third floor of Farish Hall in the 300N suite of rooms. Our team contributes to the success of our college by providing the following services: Computing and multimedia environments; Computer and Equipment support; Web services; Hardware/software recommendations, solutions, upkeep, or repair; Consultation and support for Instructional Design; Training on academic software and technology; Informal hands-on training to users on the use of computers; Core and non-core technology application programs and ad-hoc programs; Support to the academic public regarding intrinsic operating systems problems; Administer the local area network for the college, including the installation, configuration, maintenance and upgrading of the LAN; Insure compliance with university and division policies and procedures regarding network/computing acquisitions; Collaborate with administration and campus leaders to implement information technology support services; and Facility planning and maintenance For assistance feel free to stop by the CITE Help Desk, call 713-743-9833, or learn more by reading about our services.

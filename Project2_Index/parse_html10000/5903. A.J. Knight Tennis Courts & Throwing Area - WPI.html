Knight Tennis Courts & Throwing Area A.J. Knight Tennis Courts & Throwing Area Venue A.J. Knight Tennis Courts & Throwing Area Overview Just across Park Avenue from Alumni Stadium, the courts are used by the men's and women's club tennis teams and physical education classes. These six asphalt courts are also available for recreational use. Additional Features Playing Surface Hard court Size Six Courts Seating Standing Room Scoreboard No Directions & Parking Park Avenue Parking Garage at WPI GPS directions: 161 Salisbury Street, Worcester, MA From I-290 Eastbound: Take Exit 17 (sign says Route 9), go up the exit ramp, take a left onto Belmont Street. Go 1/4 of a mile straight through Lincoln Square. Belmont Street becomes Highland Street. Turn right onto Grove Street and follow to first light. Take left onto Salisbury Street. WPI Park Avenue Parking Garage entrance is up on the left.  From I-290 Westbound: Take Exit 18 (sign says Route 9), go down the exit ramp. Take a right onto Lincoln Street, then an immediate right onto Concord Street. At second light, Concord Street becomes Salisbury Street.

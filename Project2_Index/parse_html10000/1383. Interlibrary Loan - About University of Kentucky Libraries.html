Nunn Center for Oral History Collection Catalog Statistical Insight Wall Street Journal Web of Science Core Collection WorldCat Interlibrary Loan About ILLiad Login First Time Users Policies FAQ - ILL FAQ - ILLiad Borrowing from UK Related Services   About   Interlibrary Loan (ILL) attempts to fill gaps in the UK Libraries' collection. If the collection lacks items needed for research, University of Kentucky faculty, emeritus faculty, students, and staff may be able to obtain loans or reproductions of the articles needed for their research. The ILL staff will attempt to obtain the material from another library or document vendor. Three ILL departments are available to help UK Researchers. Quick ILL Login Links:  Main Campus ILLiad login Medical Center ILLiad login Law Library Interlibrary Loan information   Young Library Interlibrary Loan (main campus) serves all colleges and departments except those served by the Medical Center and Law Library ILL departments.  Contact our staff for help during these available hours. Please see our Policies page and FAQ regarding policies and procedures for using the Young Library ILL service.   Medical Center Library Interlibrary Loan serves the Colleges of Dentistry, Health Sciences, Medicine, Nursing, Pharmacy, and Public Health. Please see the Medical Center Library's Interlibrary Loan page for additional information. Law Library Interlibrary Loan serves the college of Law.

Personnel in Professional Consultative Services are represented by the formula of one senator per each fifteen members or major fraction thereof. Senators acting in an at-large capacity are the current Academic Senate Chair, the immediate Past Chair of the Academic Senate, and the two CSU statewide academic senators. Ex officio (nonvoting) members include the President of the University or designee, the Provost or designee, one representative from among the academic deans, the President and Chair for the Board of Directors of Associated Students, Inc., and the Vice President for Student Affairs.                                                       * Designates College Caucus Chair By College: CAED, CAFES,

Watkins, honors donors who have made a commitment to the university’s continued excellence by establishing a legacy gift. These gifts provide essential support to the outstanding students and world-renowned faculty at UCR for generations to come. Members of the Watkins Society are honored at an annual luncheon and program, which showcases an outstanding facet of UCR.

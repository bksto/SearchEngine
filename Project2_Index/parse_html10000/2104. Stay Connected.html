Campus News and Events For the latest CSU Dominguez Hills news and stories, visit our Dateline Dominguez online news center. CSU Dominguez Hills provides on and off campus community opportunities and access to educational conferences, workshops, programs, events, and activities that are relevant to the mission of CSU Dominguez Hills and its role as a cultural center for the region. Campus events include plays, concerts, athletic games, alumni mixers, multi-cultural gatherings, literary readings and film screenings, to name a few. Visit the campus calendar for the latest events and programs. Volunteer Opportunities Join alumni and friends by contributing time to various alumni events. Volunteering for the CSUDH allows you to socialize with other members and enhance your professional network while helping support current students, recruit future Toros and strengthen the university community. Our Alumni Association volunteers help with our major events such as mass mailings and Commencement as well as support our presence at other campus and community events. If you would like to participate, please email alumniprograms@csudh.edu or call (310) 243-2237. toroMail Email Service CSU Dominguez Hills uses the toroMail email service powered by Google. Your toroMail account is the e-mail account that receives communication from university offices. Click to log in to your toroMail. Log into toroMail with the same username and password you use to log into MyCSUDH. Your e-mail address is username@toromail.csudh.edu. Your toroMail powered by Google account does not expire, and CSUDH alumni can continue to use it for life.

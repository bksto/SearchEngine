Exercise of Powers 7.1 Determinative Vote. Except as otherwise specifically provided herein, all matters coming before the Board or a Committee thereof for determination shall be determined by a majority vote. 7.2 Roll Call Vote. Upon the demand of any member, a vote by the Board shall be by a call of the roll. 7.3 Suspension of the Bylaws or Standing Orders. Any provision of the Bylaws or Standing Orders may be suspended in connection with the consideration of a matter before the Board by an affirmative vote of not less than two-thirds of the members of the Board. Includes amendments through June 13, 1975.

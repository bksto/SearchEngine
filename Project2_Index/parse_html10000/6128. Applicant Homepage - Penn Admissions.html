Helpful Tips: To be considered for admission, an applicant must submit both the Common Application and Penn Writing Supplement. Once you have submitted the Common Application and Penn Writing Supplement, you will receive login information within 48 hours to be able to submit the Penn Financial Aid Application.  Should you need assistance submitting the Common Application and Penn Writing Supplement, contact the Applicant Help Center. Questions after the submission of the Common Application should be directed to eapps@admissions.upenn.edu. Send application documents directly to documents@admissions.upenn.edu. This email address is ONLY to be used for submitting documents for attachment to your application record. All required documents need to be sent from your school official's email address. Connect with Dean Eric Furda through the Dean's blog, p.217. The blog is an introduction to and resource for the world of higher education. Learn helpful tips about the admissions process, writing essays, making your college choice, and more.

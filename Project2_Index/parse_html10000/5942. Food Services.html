At other hours, food items can be obtained from the vending machines located at other locations around campus. During the fall and spring semesters, the college's award-winning Culinary Arts program operates an on-campus restaurant - The Sun Room. They offer a variety of meals, sandwiches, salads and daily specials at a nominal cost. Please take our Cafeteria Service Quality Survey. We would like to know how we are doing, and how we can improve.

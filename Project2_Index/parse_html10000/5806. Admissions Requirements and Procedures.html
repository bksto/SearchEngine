Before you start the online application: Explore our list of academic degrees and program List. Research your degree or program of interest by visiting their website. Review what you need to apply and check degree and program specific deadlines and admissions requirements. Contact the department if you have questions regarding the program.  Contact information can be found on the degree and program directory.  For general admissions questions, please refer to our FAQ. How to Apply Online: The application process for is entirely online through Grad Apply.  You will need a valid email address to create an account. This email address will be your login name. It will also be the primary line of communication between you and UC San Diego. Be sure to use an email address that will not change: do not use a temporary address. The activation email and additional instructions to complete your registration will be sent to this address once you create your account. Admissions Apply Now!

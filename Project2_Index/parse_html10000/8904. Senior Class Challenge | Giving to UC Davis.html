The Senior Class Challenge is an annual giving program designed to encourage and continue this tradition of philanthropy among graduating seniors. Senior Class Challenge gifts can be allotted to one of these sources: 1) Annual Fund, 2) your college or school, and We Are Aggie Pride, an emergency fund for students in a time of need. This year, graduating seniors were asked to give $20.12 or more in honor of their graduation year.

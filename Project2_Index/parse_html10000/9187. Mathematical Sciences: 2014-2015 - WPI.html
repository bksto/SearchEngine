Silverman of Brown University. Joseph H. Silverman, the Royce Professor of Mathematics at Brown University, will present Taxicabs and Sums of Two Cubes: An Excursion in Mathematics on Tuesday, March 3 at 4:00 PM in Salisbury Labs, Room 115.  This talk is the opening event for Math Awareness Month, with its theme of "Math Drives Careers." Professor Silverman, a leading expert in the theory of elliptic curves, received his PhD at Harvard University in 1982.  He  is the author of eight books and nearly 150 publications, is a co-inventor of the NTRU cryptosystem and an active researcher in cryptography.

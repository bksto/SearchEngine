The College of Education Scholarship Application Process for funds to be used during the 2015-2016 academic year will open March 2, 2015 and close March 27, 2015. Additional information below. For information on Graduate Fellowships, students admitted to a graduate program may contact their Departments. For information on student loans, grants, and scholarships offered by the University of Houston, please visit the University of Houston Office of Scholarships and Financial Aid. Scholarship Application Process: This Scholarship Application Cycle for use in Fall 2015 and Spring 2016 will open March 2nd and close March 27th with awards being made prior to May of 2015.  All application information is submitted online. The link to the application is available in step 3 below.  Learn more about the Scholarship Application Process. Step 1: Review Carefully review the list of scholarships to familiarize yourself with the criteria, both general criteria and those specific to individual awards.  Step 2: Prepare You will complete one application and upload one set of materials.  The committee will review your application only for scholarships for which you meet the criteria. Prepare the following materials:  A written narrative  (written on 1 page, double-spaced, 12 point font, saved as a PDF document): What makes you a strong candidate for a COE scholarship? (Successful applicants will review the scholarship criteria on the scholarship website to ensure that the written narrative highlights the fit with relevant scholarships.) Reference Contact Information (will be part of the online application submission) Please supply the following information for two professional references, at least one of which is a College of Education faculty member. Your references will be emailed a link to the confidential reference form and will submit the form directly back to the Scholarship Committee. Please make sure you have asked your references first for permission to list their name and email address, and be certain the email addresses are exactly correct, as this is an automated process. Those applicants who do not have two completed references will not be eligible for a COE scholarship. Name of Reference: Title of Reference: Institution/Organization: Email of Reference: A professional resume An unofficial transcript Tax documents (if applying for need-based scholarships only). Please submit the first page of the Form 1040 (Individual Income Tax Return - see example). FERPA statement (Form No. OGC-SF-2006-02). The form is available on the online application. Note: Please enter College of Education Scholarship Committee next to This information may be released to on the FERPA form. Step 3: Submit Complete the College of Education Scholarship Application and upload the required documents. After you have completed the application you will have until midnight of the final due date to complete document submission. All required documents must be submitted in order for your application to be considered complete. If you have questions about the application process or if you encounter any difficulties, please contact Brandie Cleaver at bmcleaver@uh.edu.

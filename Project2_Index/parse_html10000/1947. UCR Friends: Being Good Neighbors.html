We invite you to join with us as we work to create an environment conducive to research, education and a high quality of life for all. Good Neighbor Guidelines UCR students who live in the neighborhoods around the campus are expected to be good citizens and represent the university positively, following our Good Neighbor Guidelines. If you feel that UCR students are failing to abide by these guidelines, you may communicate questions, concerns or reports on specific incidents to the Student Conduct and Academic Integrity Program by the utilizing the online Good Neighbor Report Form.

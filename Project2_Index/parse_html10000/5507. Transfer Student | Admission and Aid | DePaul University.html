University > Admission & Aid > Types of Admission > Transfer Student Transfer Student Admission Process Main Page Content Transfer students are an integral part of DePaul’s diverse community. And we’re here to make the process easier. Visit our transfer center for information on what courses transfer, accelerated admission and more. Admission Steps Transfer students are admitted to DePaul each quarter -- except applicants to the School of Music and The Theatre School, which only have fall admission. Apply Submit a completed application gaq['siloTracker._trackEvent', 'External Link - IS Apply Online', this.href]online or download the gaq['siloTracker._trackEvent', 'Internal Link - Paper Application', this.href]paper application. Pay Application Fee Submit your application fee as soon as possible after applying. Fees are non-refundable, but must be submitted in order to process your application. $25 for online $45 for paper applications Checks: Make checks payable to DePaul University. Include your name and date of birth so we can match it with your application. Submit Official Transcripts Send official transcripts from all colleges and universities you’ve attended – regardless of the amount of credit earned or grades. Admission will be based on college performance if you’ve completed 30 semester/44 quarter hours of transferable credit. Admission will be based on high school and college performance if you’ve completed fewer than 30 semester/44 quarter hours of transferable credit. Send Materials Send everything to: Office of Undergraduate Admission 1 East Jackson Boulevard, Suite 9000 Chicago, IL 60604 Electronic transcripts and test scores may be sent to electronictranscripts@depaul.edu. Tell Us About Classes You’re Taking Now If you’re currently taking college courses, mail or email us a list of the courses you are enrolled in for the current term or will be for a future term. Updating Your Application If you need to change information on your application, use our information update form. GPA Requirements Transfer applicants to the College of Communication, Computing and Digital Media, Liberal Arts and Social Sciences, and Science and Health must have a minimum 2.0 GPA. Applicants to the Driehaus College of Business, College of Education and School of Music must have a minimum GPA of 2.5.

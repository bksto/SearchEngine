The Council will be comprised of leaders in the education reform and advocacy community from the Greater Houston area as well as leaders and external constituents from the national and global community along with committed UH alumni.  A Council Member’s role is to: Offer the College an outside perspective on issues affecting education that may have educational practice, and/or research implications and/or opportunities; Serve as an ambassador for the College and sounding board for new initiatives, research projects, and external activities; Advise and assist in programmatic and capital financial development goals; Offer counsel to the College on matters for which external expertise is critical, such as long-range planning, strategy execution, programs and projects, and facilities; Assist in the development of strategic partnerships and external relations; and Establish and strengthen ties between the pipeline from high school to college to the professional world.

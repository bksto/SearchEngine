Online Resources Learn to Learn Online Faculty Resources: Comprehensive Quality Control Process Online Program Committee Out-of-State Online Students Office of the Vice President of Student Services Counseling Student Life Library Tutoring & Academic Support Transfer and Career Services Specialized Counseling Services Food Services Policies & Procedures Blackboard Access & Information Home / Online Classes / Blackboard Access & Information Blackboard is SBVC's Learning Management System....To access the Blackboard Learning Management system, click the Quick Login link in the upper right corner of the website. Or use this URL:  http://blackboard.valleycollege.edu. Users will enter their seven digit faculty or student id number as both the username and the password.  After entering Blackboard for the first time, users should change their password. Please allow 24 hours after registering for a course for that course to appear in Blackboard. Michael Torrez, professor of Chemistry at SBVC, has created a video tutorial showing students how to log in to Blackboard and how to navigate in a class.  Although this tutorial focuses on his particular section of a Chemistry class, it is valuable for any online student as an example of how an online class is structured and organized.                                                       Browse All Online Classes Succeeding in Online Classes Computer Requirements & Support Online Courses Access (UAP) Handbook for Online Students International Students and Online Classes Blackboard Access & Information Are Online Classes For Me?

Programs can be found by date of departure, location of the trip, or type of trip.   Explore 2015 Trips PDF Download Explore the World HAA offers over 50 programs a year to all seven continents. Accompanied by Harvard academics and fellow alumni, trips range from week-long concentrated stays to extended journeys (two weeks or longer). Whether by land, rail, or cruise, these travel programs can sustain your enthusiasm for learning and establish new friends among your peers.

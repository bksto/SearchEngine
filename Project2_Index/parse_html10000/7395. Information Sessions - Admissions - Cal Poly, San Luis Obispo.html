During the session, an admissions officer speaks about the admissions process and answers questions from interested prospective students. The sessions are held at 1pm and follows the general campus tour. Interested visitors should check at the University Union Information desk for the day's session location. If you are planning to visit Cal Poly between the dates of March 30th to April 3rd, and April 6th to April 10th, please note that the daily information session will take place at 12 P.M. Register to attend the session through your VIP page (If you don't have a VIP page, complete the interest form here for immediate access). Login to your VIP page and register for an Admissions Information Session! Once you register for a session (and/or tour) you will be emailed a confirmation.

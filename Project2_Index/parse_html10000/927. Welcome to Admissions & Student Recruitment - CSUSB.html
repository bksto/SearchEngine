Courses taken in the summer immediately preceding the fall term one has applied to will not count toward admission requirements for the fall. CSUSB is No Longer Accepting Applications for Fall 2015 CSUSB is no longer accepting applications for Fall 2015. We are not accepting undergraduate applications for the Winter and Spring 2016 terms. CSUSB will start accepting applications for Fall 2016 starting Oct. 1st, 2015 through Nov. 30th, 2015. Apply online during the filing period at www.csumentor.edu. Impaction Declared for All Undergraduate Students for 2015-2016 Cal State San Bernardino has declared campus-wide impaction for 2015-2016. Eligible applicants from high schools and community colleges located within CSUSB's defined local area will be admitted based on the standard CSU system-wide admission requirements. Undergraduate applicants from outside our defined local area who meet admission requirements will be admitted on a space-available basis. Are you in CSUSB's local area? High School Community College There are four majors at CSUSB where admission requirements for the major are set higher than other majors at CSUSB. These majors are Criminal Justice, Kinesiology: Pre-Physical Therapy, Nursing, and Social Work. Applicants to Nursing and Social Work will be required to complete a supplemental application in order to be fully admitted into the major. California residents are the only students who will be accepted into the Nursing major.

Resources for Media Looking for Photos, Footage or AudioRequests for general purpose photography of UC Riverside, its people or facilities and B-roll footage should be directed to UCR's Office of Media Relations at: news@ucr.edu or the appropriate media contact. Even better — we have collected a sampling of pre-approved high-resolution photographs you can use now, or that will give a preview of what you would see on campus. And in the video world, UCR has a YouTube channel that features a sampling of campus video. Talking to Our PeopleExperts on Demand includes a list of faculty experts on a variety of newsworthy topics. Just let us know if you would like us to get them in the campus studio for an interview over the AT&T connection to the Hollywood hub or our ISDN line at KUCR radio. What's Happening at UCR?Campus news is archived and in many cases includes print quality art. Sign up for an RSS feed that will automatically ship a release to you. Or just tell us if you want to receive UCR news. Need to know more about campus administration or history? About UCR has the answer you are looking for. Visiting Campus Coming to campus? Be careful about the parking rules because tickets can get expensive. Working journalists can get free parking, but we need to know you are coming.

Whether you drive, bike, carpool or use public transportation, Transportation and Parking Services (TAPS) provides parking and transit services. With a strong commitment to sustainability, UC Merced supports several alternative transportation programs to provide affordable and convenient options to personal-vehicle use, including CatTracks, the bus transit line that provides service to and from campus to nearby neighborhoods and communities and downtown Merced. The Commuter Club assists faculty and staff members with finding cost-efficient and environmentally-friendly alternatives to driving to campus. If you are without a vehicle and need access to one, TAPS offers Hertz 24/7, a car-sharing program. You can also find information regarding local and regional taxi, train, bus and air transit options. TAPS also serves commuters who drive personal vehicles to campus through its parking permit services.     The first new American research university in the 21st century, with a mission of research, teaching and service.

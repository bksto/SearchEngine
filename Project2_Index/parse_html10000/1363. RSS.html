Subscribe to CSUF's RSS (Really Simple Syndication) feeds to automatically receive updated content about Cal State Fullerton without having to log onto this site regularly to check for new content. By using our RSS news feeds, new content will come to you as a series of links. The new updates can be delivered on a free RSS reader (ex: Google, My Yahoo!, etc.). Choose any of our news feeds on the right to subscribe. How Can I Receive RSS Feeds? If you are unfamiliar with how RSS works, you will first need an RSS Reader that can instantly collect CSUF news updates. There are many free RSS Readers available for you to choose from.  Some of the most popular include: Google Reader My Yahoo! NewsGator Netvibes PageFlakes My AOL Select any of these free readers and follow instructions on how to get started.

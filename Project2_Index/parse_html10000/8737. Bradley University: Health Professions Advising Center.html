Our Mission In keeping with the vision of Bradley University’s founder, Lydia Moss Bradley, “to furnish its students with the means of living an independent, industrious, and useful life by the aid of a practical knowledge of the useful arts and sciences," the Health Professions Advising Center provides guidance, support, and resources on choosing and pursuing careers in the health professions so that all of Bradley’s pre-health students can reach their fullest potential and achieve their personal best.

The San Francisco Bay Area is a world-class tourist destination, and the recreational and tourist activities in the Bay Area are unparalleled. It's a wonderful place to be for the summer, but you really have to work hard on your class days in order to enjoy a carefree weekend. Berkeley is generally considered to be in the "East Bay" area of the San Francisco Bay Area, so if you want to stay nearby, look for events and activities in the East Bay.

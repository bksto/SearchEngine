Dean’s Letter to Students Dear Future Graduate, Congratulations on your approaching graduation! What an exciting time! You will soon join over 224,000 UH alumni. Just as your family and friends share in the joy of your achievement, your UH family is also happy for you. The UH faculty and staff know that you will touch lives and bring excellence to whatever you pursue. As Dean of the College of Education, I want to say how proud I am of the accomplishments of the Spring 2015 graduating class. It has been an honor to serve you. Let me also say thank you for entrusting the UH College of Education with the task of teaching, training, molding, and mentoring you in preparation for your future success. We hope that you celebrate your graduation milestone with us at both cermonies.  You will receive important details about the graduation via email and can find more information on this page. Our best wishes are with you as you change the world.  You are the pride. Go Coogs, Robert H. McPherson, Ph.D. Professor and Dean, Elizabeth D. Rockwell Chair Arrival Graduating students should arrive at the Athletics and Alumni Center – enter doors on Cullen Blvd. The processional will form on the Basketball court in Yeoman Field House.  Your family and friends should proceed directly to Hofheinz Pavilion. RSVP It will help us a great deal if you tell us if you're participating in Graduation. Please R.S.V.P online using our Graduation RSVP Form.  *It is very important that you include phonetic or other type of assistance with the correct pronunciation of your name. Guests You can invite as many guests as you like.  Given the limited number of seats in Hofheinz Pavilion seating will be on a FIRST COME FIRST SERVE BASIS. Please advise all guests that wish to attend your ceremony to arrive ON-TIME.  Doors will open one hour prior to start of ceremony.  Security will enforce release of saved seats 15 minutes prior to ceremony start.  There will be overflow seating with a large screen in a nearby building. Cap and Gown, Printed Announcements You should make arrangements with the Bookstore in the University Center for your cap and gown and printed graduation announcements and invitations. There will be a Graduation Fair during the beginning of each semester at the Bookstore.  Students may order caps, gowns, hoods, announcements and invitations online at http://colleges.herffjones.com/college/uh/ If you have any questions regarding these items, please call the Bookstore directly at (713) 748-0923 or visit their website at http://bncollege.com. Remember, you are responsible for picking up and dropping off your rented cap and gown. The bookstore will have drop off stations for rentals on the concourse of Hofheinz Pavilion and in Yeoman Field House after our ceremony.

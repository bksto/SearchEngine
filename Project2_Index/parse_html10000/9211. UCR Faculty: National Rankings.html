National Rankings UCR has long been known as a community of scholars dedicated to excellence. Faculty members make discovery a priority and their work makes a difference across the region, the state and the nation. This work has been recognized across the world and has resulted in our university being highly ranked in a number of prestigious areas. UCR is one of 10 in the University of California system and the only public research university in Inland Southern California. Learn more about UCR at our Rankings and Quick Facts page.

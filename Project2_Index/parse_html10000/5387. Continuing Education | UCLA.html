At UCLA, we offer continuing education — from courses to conferences on the latest medical topics — ensuring medical professionals stay at the forefront of their respective fields. Courses range from a Transcranial Doppler Course at the David Geffen School of Medicine Continuing Medical Education to the weekly Psychiatry Grand Rounds conference at the Neuropsychiatric Institute Continuing Education. Classes are also offered through UCLA’s School of Dentistry Continuing Education and UCLA Extension, which feature courses in public health, nursing and mental health.

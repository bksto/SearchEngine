Deciding Students Undergraduate Advising guides students who are exploring academic options and aids them with their academic decisions. Students can get assistance from academic deans and advising staff with choosing a major, making a transition between majors, developing an academic plan, and advancing towards graduation in a timely manner. Students with Declared Majors All students with declared majors are assigned an academic advisor and academic dean within their school or college. To determine which school or college, refer to the list of majors. For students with majors in the College of Humanities and Fine Arts, your academic dean and advising services are located in E20 Machmer Hall. For students with majors in the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences, your academic dean and advising services are located in 128 Thompson Hall (this also includes advising services for pre-law students). For students with majors in the College of Natural Sciences your academic dean and advising services are located in 220 Morrill II. Students in the following schools and colleges should refer to the appropriate websites listed below for advising information.

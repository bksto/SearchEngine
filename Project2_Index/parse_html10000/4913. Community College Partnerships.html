As part of this commitment, we’re partnering with a range of local community colleges to put their students on the right path to their degrees. CSUDH’s Community College Enrollment Partnerships prepare students for success at CSUDH with: Guidance on which classes to take now. Simplified credit transfers and application process. Support and resources to ease the transition to CSUDH. Introduction to the CSUDH campus, classes, staff and student life. An opportunity for guaranteed admission to CSUDH. To learn more, email transfer@csudh.edu or call the Transfer Hotline at (877) U2-CSUDH or (877) 822-7834.

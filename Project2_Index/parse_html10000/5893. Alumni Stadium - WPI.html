This state-of-the-art outdoor sports facility is the home of intercollegiate field hockey, football, and men’s and women’s soccer, and provides an impressive facility for club sports, intramural programs, and recreational use by the WPI community. The stadium allows WPI to host championships at the local, regional, and national levels.

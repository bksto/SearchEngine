The Office of the Secretary oversees the fulfillment of legal requirements for reporting and record keeping for DePaul Univeristy. It manages the work of the Board of Trustees and the Members of the Corporation to facilitate their services to the university. The office coordinates the services offered to the committees and subcommittees of the Board by university staff members.

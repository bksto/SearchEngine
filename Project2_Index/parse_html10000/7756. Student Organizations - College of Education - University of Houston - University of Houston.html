Graduate Student Organization: Department of Educational Psychology (EPSY) Promotes social community among educational psychology graduate students Serves as a forum for sharing scholarly ideas Provides opportunities for professional networking, development and innovative research Graduate Student Organization: Department of Curriculum & Instruction (CUIN-GSO) The work of the CUIN-GSO is to establish and promote a sense of community and connection among students in Curriculum and Instruction and the College of Education. BESO: Bilingual Education Student Organization Promotes bilingual education Provides professional development through leadership conferences and workshops Unites students and communities through bilingual education PDK – PHI DELTA KAPPA International PDK’s mission is to support education, particularly public education, as the cornerstone of democracy. Offers scholarships to undergraduate and graduate students who have a connection to PDK through Future Educators Association® chapters. Great outlet for publishing and presenting research. Please read the most current PDK UH-University Park Newsletter. Human Development Student Association (HDSA) Unites those interested in a career in human services such as psychology, sociology, health, education, social work, and particularly those pursuing Human Development and Family Studies Degrees. Provides its members with the opportunity to explore career options and opportunities within the field of Human Development and Family Studies. Provides opportunities for participating in volunteering, networking, and community service within the human services field. Offers support and guidance for academic excellence through social activities and professional development. Council for Exceptional Children The purpose of this chapter shall be to advance the education of individuals with exceptionalities and to promote educational, scientific, and charitable purposes. Health Student Association Student run organization backed by faculty and staff of the COE Health Board. Designed to promote health and wellness along with helping students succeed.

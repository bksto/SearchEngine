The funds are made available to undergraduate students who will be presenting research or creative works at a professional conference or meeting. Each student is allowed up to $400 once a year to aid in covering conference costs such as registration fees, meals, travel and lodging. There is a 50% match requirement that must come from a named source. These sources can include the following: a Student Undergraduate Research Fellowship or other sponsored award; funding from the college, department, faculty sponsor, or student’s own resources; or another named source. Please be aware that Undergraduate Research Travel Funds are limited.

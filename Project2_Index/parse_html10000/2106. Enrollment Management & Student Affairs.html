As a CSUDH student, you have access to a wide range of resources available right here on campus, to support your success in the classroom and beyond. We encourage you to take advantage of the services and programs provided through our Enrollment Management/Student Affairs Division, as well as throughout our University. We're here to see you succeed! If you cannot find the information you are looking for on the website, please contact us, we will answer any questions you may have. Our Mission The Division of Enrollment Management and Student Affairs provides services and programs in support of the educational development of students. This is achieved by a student-centered philosophy, student life activities, and an array of support and developmental services. We are committed to assisting students in enhancing their sense of purpose, autonomy, integrity, identity, and tolerance, in a caring yet challenging environment.

The Association has sponsored a group travel program to international and domestic destinations since 1984. Each tour not only offers adventure, but also provides an educational element.   Tour participants, whether UCR alumni or not, must be members of the UCR Alumni Association. A member may bring one companion to travel as a guest. View information on the benefits of membership.

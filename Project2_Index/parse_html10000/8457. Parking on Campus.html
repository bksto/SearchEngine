About Redlands University of Redlands > About Redlands > Visiting the University > Redlands Campus > Parking on Campus Parking on Campus Admissions Visitors Visitor parking is available in front of the Willis Center. Athletics Fans Campus Events Tickets for campus events can be purchased from Hospitality Services located in the Hunsaker University Center. Parking is available in the Armacost Library parking lot. School of Education Visitor parking is available directly in front of the entrance doors to the School of Education; which are on the east side of University Hall. School of Business Visitor parking is available in front of the Willis Center. Students The University of Redlands offers its students convenient, free, on-campus parking. Most residence halls have adjacent parking lots or curbside parking.

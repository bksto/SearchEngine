Panelists include: Noa Ben-Asher, Visiting Associate Professor of Law, Harvard Law School Elizabeth F. Emens, Isidor and Seville Sulzbacher Professor of Law, Columbia Law School Gerald L. Neuman, J. Sinclair Armstrong Professor of International, Foreign, and Comparative Law, Harvard Law School Matthew J.B. Lawrence, Academic Fellow, Petrie-Flom Center Moderator: I. Glenn Cohen, Professor of Law, Harvard Law School, and Faculty Director, Petrie-Flom Center This event is free and open to the public. Lunch will be served. Cosponsored by the Human Rights Program at Harvard Law School and Lambda at Harvard Law School.

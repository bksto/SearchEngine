To learn more about your giving options, please contact a member of our Development Staff. Important Facts Gifts to California State University San Marcos are tax deductible as provided by law. Your gift — whatever its size — supports the University's commitment to excellence and opportunity. Please note your personal information will remain confidential and will not be shared with or distributed by any other party. Your confidential and secure online credit card or electronic check transaction is in U.S. dollars. Charitable gifts can be a valuable component of your overall tax and estate plan. CSUSM encourages donors to consult their personal tax and legal advisors in regard to all charitable gifts. It is the policy of California State University San Marcos and the Cal State San Marcos Foundation to reinvest a portion of the gift principal and/or the short-term investment income on gifts and grants to support the cost of raising and administering funds. © Copyright 2013 California State University San Marcos.

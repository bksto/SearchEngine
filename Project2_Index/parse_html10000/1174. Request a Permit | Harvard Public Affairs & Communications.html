For this reason, the University has put in place a number of policies to ensure that students, faculty, and staff can pursue academic endeavors with as little hindrance as possible. Reporting, photographing, and videotaping are prohibited on campus without prior permission. To apply for a permit, please first review the guidelines to ensure your request adheres to University policies, and then fill out a permit request form. News media may also use public sidewalks to photograph or film campus.

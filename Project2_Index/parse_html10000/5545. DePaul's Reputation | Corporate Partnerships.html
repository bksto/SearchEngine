A premier academic reputation. DePaul is the largest private, nonprofit university in the Midwest — ranked in the top 12 nationally and widely renowned for academic excellence.  Distinguished faculty. Our faculty comes from distinguished academic, professional and research backgrounds and most are active in their fields, providing real-world experience that translates into practical application. In tune with the unique needs of working adults. DePaul has a strong history and reputation teaching adults. Whether your employees have some college credit and are looking to complete their bachelor’s degree or are interested in a graduate or certificate programs, we have the flexibility to meet their needs. A vast community of successful alumni. DePaul has a reputation for training stellar employees — with more than 160,000 DePaul alumni working in virtually every field, all over the world. And nearly 106,000 are in the Chicago area.

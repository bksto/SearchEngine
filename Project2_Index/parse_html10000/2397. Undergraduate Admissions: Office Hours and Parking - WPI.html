Parking Information and Directions We look forward to welcoming you to the WPI campus! We encourage you to arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your scheduled visit time or appointment in order to locate parking and the Office of Admissions in Bartlett Center. Visitors should place a WPI admissions visitor parking pass in their car dashboard while on campus. Visitors who pre-register will be provided a link via e-mail confirmation to print out a parking pass.  Passes are also available in the Bartlett Center when arriving on campus. WPI is located at 100 Institute Road, Worcester, MA 01609. Handicap and very limited parking for Admissions visitors is available via the main entrance located at the intersection of West Street and Institute Road. Additional free visitor parking is available in the West Street Lot (corner of West Street and Institute Road) in designated visitor spaces and in the Park Avenue parking garage (all spaces) accessible via Park Avenue or Salisbury Street (for GPS: 151 Park Avenue, 151 Salisbury Street, or intersection of Park Avenue and Salisbury Street, Worcester, MA 01609). Additionally, a map of campus and parking areas is available for your use. If traveling from out of the area, you may view detailed directions to campus.

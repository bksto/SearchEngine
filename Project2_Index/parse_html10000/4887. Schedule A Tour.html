We offer a range of campus-tour options to choose from. All guided tours are lead by current undergraduate students who give a quick history of our campus, review admission requirements, provide an overview of academic departments and student services, discuss student life, and share from their personal experiences at CSUDH. Prospective Student Campus Tours Prospective students and their families are encouraged to join us for a personally guided 55-minute walking tour of our park-like campus. Individual tours are offered daily at 9:00 a.m., 1:30 p.m. and 3:00 p.m. Complimentary parking permits are available to those who register for tours in advance. We’ll send you a tour confirmation email within 24 hours of registering, including parking and tour details. For visitors who do not pre-register, the daily parking fee is $5.00. Parking tickets can be purchased at kiosks located in all campus parking lots. To schedule your individual tour, You‘ll first need to sign up for a ToroVIP account here: First-time freshman, Transfer student, Graduate student, International student. Once you‘ve completed this step, you‘re ready to schedule your tour. Schedule an individual tour. Group Campus Tours Groups and organizations with 12 or more prospective high school or community college students are welcome to join us for a guided 55-minute walking tour of our park-like campus. Reservations are required for group tours. Group tours are offered weekly on select mornings. Before reserving your group tour, please review the group tour Visitation Guidelines. We’ll send you a tour confirmation email within 24 hours of registering, including drop-off and meeting location. Teachers and counselors, please email campustours@csudh.edu if you need help in planning your students‘ group visit. Schedule a group tour. Self-Guided Campus Tours For those unable to schedule an individual or group tour, we offer free self-guided brochures with an easy-to-follow walking route and descriptions of key buildings. Self-guided brochures can be downloaded here or picked up at the Information Center located in Welch Hall, room 245. Download brochure for self-guided tour.

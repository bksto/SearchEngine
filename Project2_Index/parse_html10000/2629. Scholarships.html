In order to maneuver the scholarship process, you need to understand the difference between these two types of scholarships. You can find detailed information about both types by selecting the menu items on the right. The SBVC Foundation Office  provides over $150,000 in scholarship each year exclusively to SBVC student and the 2014-2015 online scholarship application is open  from October 1, 2014 to January 21, 2015. We encourage students to research outside scholarships for additional educational funding. Get started by selecting the Outside Scholarship link on the right. Students with Outside Scholarship checks or enrollment verification forms should come to the Scholarship Office, located in the Financial Aid Office (AD/SS-106), for assistance in order to receive their scholarship funds from other schools and agencies.

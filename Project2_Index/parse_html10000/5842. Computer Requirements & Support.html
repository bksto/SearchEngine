And what if I need help? Online and hybrid classes will require a computer with a modern operating system (such as Windows 7 or Mac OSX).  That computer must have access to the internet and must have a modern web browser (such as Firefox 3, Safari 4, or Internet Explorer 8).  Students can also use the open labs on campus to access their online classes. The Central Help Desk is available 24/7 to all faculty, staff, and students. They can be reached on campus at (x4357) and off campus at this toll-free number (877) 241-1756.

The following groups are officially recognized by the Student Activities Office:  Buddhist Fellowship- for more information contact bfc@wpi.edu Christian Bible Fellowship for more information contact cbf-lt@wpi.edu Hillel (Jewish) for more information contact hillel-officers@wpi.edu Muslim Students Association for more information contact msaofficers@wpi.edu Newman Club (Catholic)- for more information contact newman-exec@wpi.edu, or campus liaison Fr. Juan Echavarria Unitarian Universalist Campus Fellowship- for more information contact  uuexec@wpi.edu, or campus liaison Rev. Cheryl Leshay If you are interested in a religion that is not listed above but would like to learn how to connect with a local community of practitioners please contact ReligiousDiversity@wpi.edu.  Interfaith Dialogues Interfaith Dialogues is a group of individuals from the WPI community who meet once a week to discuss topics of life and faith. The group consists of members from a variety of religious backgrounds. If you are interested in learning more contact campus liaisons Rev. Cheryl Leshay and Pastor Andrew H. Borden (for contact information check CRC Chaplains) and read through the Ground Rules for Healthy Inter-Religious Dialogue.  For Fall 2012 Interfaith Dialogues meets each Thursday from 3-5pm in the Campus Center.   Offices & Services Office of Multicultural Affairs About Us News and Events Our Programs Gay, Lesbian, Bisexual & Transgender Religious Diversity Collegiate Religious Center CRC Chaplains Religious Organizations at WPI Religious Services in Worcester Major Religious Holidays OASIS Cultural Center Community Networks Financial Aid Information Request for More Information WPI Diversity Advocacy Board Related Sites Outstanding Women Student Awards The Harris Foundation Disabilty Services Undergraduate Admissions Student Development & Counseling Center Contact Office of Multicultural Affairs Oasis House 20 Schussler Road Worcester, MA 01609-2280 Phone: +1-508-831-5796 Fax: +1-508-831-5818 multiculturalaffairs@wpi.edu Students Parents Alumni Media Corporate Partners Give to WPI Worcester Polytechnic Institute 100 Institute Road, Worcester, MA 01609-2280 Ph: +1-508-831-5000 Contact Us | Directions Resources: Calendar Campus Maps Human Resources Copyright Directories Gordon Library myWPI Newsroom Offices & Services ~ Feedback & Questions ~

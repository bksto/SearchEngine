UCR is one of the top employers in the City of Riverside and the seventh-largest in Inland Southern California. With more than 5,500 staff members — more than 2,300 of whom are career staff employees — UCR depends on the talent and dedication of its staff to keep UCR moving forward. UCR features state-of-the art facilities, laboratories and offices. The community features much lower cost of living than the surrounding Los Angeles, San Diego and Orange counties and features a multitude of support services from professional development opportunities to alternative transportation programs.

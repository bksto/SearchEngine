The Disability Resource Center facilitates access through consultation with faculty and campus departments. Are you or someone you know looking for library, computing or deaf/hard of hearing resources? Are you in need of a personal assistant? Or perhaps you're seeking transportation or housing assistance? Whatever you're looking for, the Disability Resource Center UIC is here to help. For more information, visit the Disability Resource Center website.

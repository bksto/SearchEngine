Welcome to the award-winning UH Teacher Education Program—Tier 1 teacher preparation at the University of Houston! The UH Teacher Education Program is committed to providing you with a successful and rewarding experience as you grow in your teaching skill, knowledge, and talent. This Online Handbook contains the most essential information for pursuing certification in combination with approved undergraduate, post baccalaureate, and graduate teacher preparation programs at the University of Houston. As a Teacher Education candidate, you are responsible for reading and understanding all of the information contained within this Online Handbook. You should schedule a meeting with your education advisor in 160 Farish Hall* to file your official degree or certification plan when you begin the program and then every semester thereafter to have your questions answered and to ensure that you will meet all required benchmarks as you advance through the Teacher Education program. * Education suite in Sugar Land, if applicable Navigating the Teacher Education Online Handbook Throughout this Online Handbook, you will find current application deadlines on the Teacher Education Calendar, updated degree plan templates, student resources, and Teacher Education news and announcements. Please become familiar with the navigation links on the Teacher Education Online Handbook so that you can find the information that you need. We strive to keep information on this handbook as accurate as possible. If you see an error, please send it to the main Teacher Education email address at teached@uh.edu. Teacher candidates working through our Sugar Land program will also want to consult specific details on that program’s website at http://www.sugarland.uh.edu/quest/.

Facts & Figures UCSC opened in 1965 with 650 students. Forty-nine years later, the campus is home to 15,088 undergraduate and 1,455 graduate students. Achievements Achievements by UC Santa Cruz faculty and students have earned national and international recognition for the quality of research and teaching. Administration George Blumenthal is UCSC's 10th chancellor, appointed in July 2006. He joined the campus in 1972 as a professor of astronomy and astrophysics. Banana Slug In a world of beavers, bears, and bobcats, UCSC is home to the mighty Banana Slug — which students voted in 1986 to adopt as the official campus mascot.

Strategic Communications consists of five separate departments: Marketing Media Relations Video Production Services Web Development Creative Design Services Our Goals As a public institution, we strive to communicate fully with campus members and our partners. To create an environment that favors and supports the university through effective communications. Our Objectives To maintain the highest standards of accuracy and professionalism in communication. To present a balanced and complete picture of UCR as a comprehensive research university. To raise awareness and understanding of the university's stature and the value of its activities. To use emerging and established media to communicate. To maintain a highly credible and responsive media relations program.

The solution may be simpler than you think. Many companies have matching gift programs, meaning they match (or even double) their employees' contributions to qualifying charitable organizations. To find out if your company runs a matching gift program, search our online database or ask your company's HR department. Then get a matching gift form from your company, complete it, and mail it to: University of Houston Donor and Alumni Records PO Box 867 Houston, TX 77001-0867 For more information about matching gifts, please contact Ronnie Calhoun at (713) 743-8920.

In the ‘Signature’ section, click the ‘Insert image’ icon above the signature text box and add your image from Drive.   12/7/14 Conversion Support for additional Microsoft Office formats Google is adding conversion support in Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides for 15 new Office file formats. New formats supported include: dot, dotx, dotm*, docm* conversions to Google Docs xlt, xltx, xltm*, xlsm* conversions to Google Sheets pot, potx, potm*, pptm*, pps, ppsx, ppsm* conversions to Google Slides Along with the ability to bring in these new formats, Google has also added better conversion support for charts, SmartArt, and merged table cells in your Office documents.   After the conversion, you can edit, collaborate on, and share your file online.

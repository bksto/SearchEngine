Cal State Fullerton boasts a student body of more than 35,000 students and offers 107 degree programs in eight colleges. More than 220,000 Titans call Cal State Fullerton their alma mater, making our impact substantial both in the region and around the globe. As our institution steadily increases its national profile, I am proud that it continues to be one of the country's most diverse universities – ranked ninth in the nation in terms of baccalaureate degrees awarded to minority students by Diverse Issues in Higher Education, based on data from the U.S. Department of Education. Indeed, ours is a community of high academic standards, stellar student support services, and one that respects and embraces all who come here. It is my hope that our websites serve your information and service needs well, and allow you to learn more about this great university. Mildred García President Bold Leadership for a New Era Cal State Fullerton's new president ushers in a new era for the institution as she begins her presidency, emphasizing continued student-centered learning for a diverse population.   This site is maintained by the Office of the President.

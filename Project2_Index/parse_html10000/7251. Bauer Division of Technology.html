Open from 8:00am to 5:30pm, Monday through Friday, Bauer Division of Technology will attempt to resolve your logged service request within 24 hours. In order to efficiently assist you, we must have a detailed request stating the exact symptoms of the problem or computing needs. This allows our technicians to properly research and diagnose the problem. Our Vision To be recognized nationally as a strategic component of the Bauer College of business by adding value in the area of instructional course delivery and by providing leading edge technology in line with corporate goals and student’s expectations. Our Mission: Superior computing services The mission of the Bauer Division of Technology Department is to provide superior computing services for faculty and staff within the Bauer College of Business.

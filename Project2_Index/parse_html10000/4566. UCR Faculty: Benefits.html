The Benefits of Belonging UC benefits are acknowledged to be among the finest in higher education. As a UC employee, you'll enjoy an outstanding benefits package, including comprehensive health and welfare insurance plans protecting you and your family, an attractive pension plan, generous leave provisions and optional retirement savings opportunities. You will retain your benefits and employment seniority if you transfer anywhere within the UC system. The UCR Human Resources Office helps faculty, staff and their dependents sort through the options and offers workshops on benefits issues, insurance and retirement counseling and other much-needed services. You also have access to the systemwide At Your Service  human resources and benefits website. This site allows you to keep track of your UC Retirement Plan benefits, view your health and insurance enrollments and  find information about your health and insurance benefits. University of California Benefits Include: Paid holidays and vacation days and up to 12 sick days a year with unlimited accruals. A variety of medical plans, as well as UC-paid comprehensive dental plans and vision plans. UC-paid disability and life insurance, plus the opportunity to purchase additional disability, life and accident insurance. Pre-tax Health FSA and DepCare FSA Accounts and employee-paid legal plan. UC Retirement Plan benefits include lifetime monthly retirement income or a lump-sum cashout, retirement income, and survivor and disability income. UC-sponsored Defined Contribution plan, tax-Deferred 403(b) Plan and 457(b) Deferred Compensation Plan. Additional benefits include: Access to credit union Access to auto, home, renters, and pet insurances Childcare services/resources Faculty & Staff Assistance Program Employee discounts to local venues, restaurants, & services Recreational, sports, and fitness centers Transportation services Library privileges For more information on benefits, visit our Human Resources Benefits page.

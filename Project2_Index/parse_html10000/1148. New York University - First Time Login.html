First, make sure you have your NYU Alumni ID. This is a 10 digit number that can be found at the bottom of all NYU alumni emails or on the address label of mail from the Alumni Relations Office. If you do not have this information available, please email alumni.info@nyu.edu with your name, graduation year, and school, or call 212-998-6912 and a member of our staff will assist you. To begin the look-up process, please provide your last name in the box indicated below. Once you've found some potential matches, please use the additional information to verify your name and graduation year. Last Name: Congratulations Class of 2014! If you recently graduated, please note that it takes about two to three months after your graduation for your name to appear in this look-up tool. In the meantime, please take advantage of the great benefits and services available to you now, including the newly launched Alumni Card program.

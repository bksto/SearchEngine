Scholarships are a four-year commitment 1st Year - recipient receives a stipend of $1,500 2nd and 3rd Year - recipient will receive up to $1,500 and will work with Alliance staff learning lifelong skills, participate in district and campus workshops and conferences, and mentor other high school students in district programs. 4th Year - recipient receives a $1,500 stipend to complete their senior year and concentrate on graduating. First Fellowship Recipient Congratulations to first-year freshman, Joselyn Perez, and the very first recipient of the Alliance Fellowship Program!  Joselyn is a high school graduate from the PACE Promise Program through the San Marcos Unified School District.  Joselyn will begin working with the Alliance staff during the Fall 2015 semester.

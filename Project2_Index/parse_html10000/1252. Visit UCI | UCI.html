Transportation & Distribution Services offers information on parking rates and timely updates on sustainable commuting options. Visitor Center The Visitor Center welcomes prospective students and their parents, alumni and all visitors who want to know more about the university. It should be your first stop when you make a trip to the Irvine campus, and it is the starting point for campus tours. Located in the UCI Student Center, across from the Student Center Parking Structure near Campus and West Peltason drives, the Visitor Center is open 8 a.m.–5 p.m. Monday–Friday.

Recent graduates have embarked on careers in business and industry. Many enter graduate school, combining their mathematical skills with additional specialties, such as law, healthcare, engineering, and technology before moving on to successful, rewarding careers. The department offers bachelor of science degrees in two areas: mathematical sciences and actuarial sciences. Students who major in mathematical sciences are able to design their own programs or focus on one of four areas of specialization.

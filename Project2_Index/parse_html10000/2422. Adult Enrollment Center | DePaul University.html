> Admission & Aid > Adult Enrollment Center Adult Enrollment Center Main Page Content We understand your time is precious—that’s why we have an adult enrollment center that centralizes everything you need to enroll at DePaul as an adult student. Whether you have attended college before or are enrolling for the first time, DePaul has a program that will fit your particular needs. Our counselors specialize in serving adult undergraduate students and can assist you with the entire admission process, including: Evaluating your degree options Transferring existing college credit Applying for financial aid Inquiring to see if you qualify for earning credit for life experience Enrolling in programs like employer tuition reimbursement or veteran's assistance Connecting you with the other adult student services you need Admission Information Learn more about the adult admission process, or apply for admission​ as an adult student. Academic Programs We understand that the lives of adult students are complex, and you have distinct educational needs. Our range of programs, tailored to your goals and learning preferences, were developed to meet your specific needs. Adult Student Degree Program Portfolio: ​ Traditional Programs​ ​School for New Learning (SNL) Competenc​e-​based Programs ​School for New Learning (SNL)​​ Accelerated Degree Completion Programs ​Program Highlights Combine liberal studies with majors in more than 150 areas.​ ​Receive credit for college-level learning through life and work experience; use this credit to earn our bachelor's degree in as little as 15 months. ​Go from an associate degree to a bachelor's in 24 months while attending class on campus one night a week. Degree Options​ ​Traditional degree programs ​Bachelor of Arts with an Individualized Concentration (Focus Area) Example Concentrations include: Leadership Track Pre-Law International Business Health Care Studies Environmental Sustainability Organization Development Applied Business Creative Arts​ Bachelor of Arts in Computing Bachelor of Arts in General Business Bachelor of Arts in Early Childhood Education ​Bachelor of Arts in Leadership Studies* Bachelor of Arts in Applied Behavioral Sciences *We offer the B.A. in Leadership Studies at our Loop and Naperville Campuses. Financing Your Education  The staff of DePaul's Office of Financial Aid is skilled in helping adult students like you explore your options and make your education affordable. Find more information on academic scholarships, payment plans, employer reimbursement, and tuition rates. Adult Student Services Find support services designed to meet your needs through the Office of Adult Student Affairs and Academic Advising. Career Opportunities Explore career and internship options, build work experience and find employment through DePaul's nationally recognized Career Center. Events We host a variety of events throughout the year, including designated information sessions designed to help you learn more about our Degree Completion Majors.     Military Personnel If you are a veteran of U.S. military service, you may be eligible for Veteran's Affairs (VA) educational benefits. For more information, visit gibill.va.gov.

About Redlands University of Redlands > About Redlands > City of Redlands & Region City of Redlands & Region The University of Redlands is conveniently located in Redlands, Calif.—a city named for its rich, red soil and famous for its lush citrus groves and temperate climate.  Redlands is located halfway between Los Angeles and Palm Springs in the shadow of Southern California's highest peaks. Redlands, though emerging as a regional leader, boasts small-town charm, a commitment to education at all levels, a community of service to others, environmental preservation and sustainability, historic preservation, and a passion for the arts. Redlands is a short drive from the best that Southern California has to offer: Beaches - 60 minutes San Gorgonio Wilderness Area - 20 minutes Big Bear Lake Ski Area - 60 minutes Bear Mountain Snow Summit Lake Arrowhead Resort - 45 minutes Los Angeles - 60 minutes Palm Springs - 45 minutes Malibu - 90 minutes  San Diego - 2 hours Las Vegas - 4 hours Disneyland - 60 minutes Knott's Berry Farm - 60 minutes Sea World - 90 minutes Universal Studios - 90 minutes To enjoy all Redlands has to offer: A.K.

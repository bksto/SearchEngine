Make a Gift Online Make a Gift by Telephone Mail a Contribution to UH Wire a Gift of Stock or Securities Make a Planned Gift Use the Electronic Funds Transfer Program Additional Questions Make a Gift Online UH benefactors now have a convenient, fast and secure way to give. Make a gift using your credit card by completing this safe and secure online gift form. You may also use this form to make a pledge. Make a Gift by Telephone Please call us at (877) 755-0559 to make a gift using your credit card. Remember, gifts made to the University of Houston are deductible for tax purposes. We will promptly forward you a receipt upon accepting your gift. Mail a Contribution to UH Send a check or money order to the Office of External Relations & Development, 334 Melcher Hall, University of Houston; Houston, Texas 77204-6021. Please be sure to specify if there is a particular program you would like to support and be sure to include your telephone number on your check or letter. Also, you may make a gift by credit card by completing, printing, and mailing the online gift form. Wire a Gift of Stock or Securities UH maintains active trading accounts that make it easy for you to transfer securities as a gift to the university. Please contact Susan F. Williams at (713) 743-8863 for wire transfer instructions, or e-mail at SFWilliams@uh.edu Remember, you can deduct the fair market value of publicly traded securities without paying capital gains tax on the sale of the gifted stock. Make a Planned Gift If you're interested in supporting UH through a planned gift (a bequest, charitable gift annuity, pooled income fund, charitable trust, etc.) or if you'd like to speak to someone about your estate planning, please contact Lynn Mason at (713) 743-4351 or LMason@uh.edu For additional information online, please visit our planned gifts page. Use the Electronic Funds Transfer Program Arrange to have a gift automatically transferred from your bank account to the University of Houston each month. You can call toll-free 1-877-755-0559 to have an authorization form mailed to you. Or, simply complete, print and return the online EFT form with a voided check to begin your monthly contributions.

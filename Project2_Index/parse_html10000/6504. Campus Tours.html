We are here to help you make an informed decision about your college choice. We invite you, your family and friends to visit California State University, Long Beach for a campus tour. We offer three types of tours: family/individual, group and self-guided. Which tour is right for you?   See What Makes The Beach so Hot! Experience our beautiful and dynamic campus with a virtual tour. Be sure to check out Hot Spots @ The Beach. The Hot Spots feature university landmarks, distinctions, noteworthy buildings, sculptures and academic colleges. It's just one more way to show you why CSULB is your University of Choice!   Check out The City of Long Beach A great part of what makes CSULB a wonderful place to live and learn is our location. Explore the City of Long Beach, featuring the historic Queen Mary, the Aquarium of the Pacific's exhibits, the Shoreline Village boardwalk and much more. Discover the diverse and thriving coastal community that is the City of Long Beach.   Directions to The Beach From getting directions and viewing maps to parking on campus, if you are planning a trip to CSULB we encourage you to take a few minutes to review our directions.   California State University, Long Beach 1250 Bellflower Boulevard, Long Beach, California 90840 Web site Credits Feedback

News University of Redlands > News > All News > Gallery: Memory's Gate Gallery: Memory's Gate Memory's Gate This exhibition, by University of Redlands Professor of Art Raúl Acero, includes sculpture and other objects that explore the poetics of memory and time. Opens Feb. 17 in the Peppers Art Gallery, with an opening reception at 4:30 p.m. Feb. 18. Show closes March 21. Gallery hours: 1 – 5 p.m. Tuesday – Friday 2 – 5 p.m. Saturdays and Sundays Gallery is closed on Mondays Closed for Spring Break Feb. 20 through March 2. The Senior Art Exhibition follows, opening March 31 as the annual exhibition of artwork produced by senior art students in their capstone class. You are invited to experience all of the Arts at Redlands, including music, theatre, creative writing and studio art. Sign up for our mailing list to receive notification of all events at Redlands.

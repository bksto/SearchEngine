Then welcome to the University of Houston. Students come to UH from 137 nations, drawn by the University’s outstanding academic programs, exceptional research opportunities, and extraordinary diversity. UH is among the Top 25 institutions with greatest international enrollment in the United States. The UH student body is comprised of more than 39,200 undergraduate and graduate students, serving over 3,000 international students and research scholars Ready to join this dynamic place? International Application Options First-time student? If you are an international student and have never been enrolled in post-secondary study and have completed the equivalent of 12 years of more of elementary and secondary schooling based on your country’s educational system, you should apply as an International Freshman student. International Freshman student. Previous post-secondary coursework/experience? If you are an international student who was or is enrolled in post-secondary study/coursework at a college or university located either overseas or in the U.S. and are now interested in undergraduate admission at the University of Houston, you should apply as an International Transfer student. Completed a four-year undergraduate degree? If you are an international student and have earned the equivalent of a U.S. bachelor’s degree (four years of post-secondary study) and you are interested in a graduate degree, you should apply as an International Graduate student. If you are seeking an additional undergraduate degree or interested in taking additional undergraduate coursework, you should apply as an International Transfer student.

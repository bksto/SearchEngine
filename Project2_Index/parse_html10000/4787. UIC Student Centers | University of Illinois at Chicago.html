Each Student Center features a bookstore; meeting rooms; Student Center Administration offices; dining services; convenience store; TCF Bank; ATMs; barbershop; and service center that handles vending returns and lost and found items. The Student Center on the east side of campus also includes: the Student Leadership Development office; Undergraduate Student Government; student organization offices; ACCC Computer Lab; A. Montgomery Ward Gallery; Wellness Center; bowling, billiards and video games; and Credit Union 1. The Student Center on the west side of campus also includes: the Campus Programs Office; Graduate Student Council; Sport and Fitness Center; photo ID center; art lounge; Craft Shop; and service center (which supplies maps, CTA tokens and more).

T. Bauer College of Business has been recognized for faculty members’ efforts to advance fields of study through research. Faculty members’ interests in business range from efficiency to ethics and have generated significant academic research that can be found in leading business publications. In addition, Bauer faculty work with faculty from other UH colleges to enhance the business perspective in their research areas.

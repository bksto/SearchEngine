This facility is also available to the campus community at a nominal fee. UHS Fitness Express is fully equipped with 16 weight resistant stations and 12 cardiovascular machines. The equipment has been specifically selected to target the major muscle groups to help you achieve your personal fitness goals HOURS Monday thru Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Saturday and Sunday from 2:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. LOCATION  59 University Drive on the East Side of La Cienega Center A brief orientation on the safe use of the equipment is required of all members. New resident orientations are available whenever staff is present in Fitness Express; however, the first two weeks of Fall Quarter require an appointment.

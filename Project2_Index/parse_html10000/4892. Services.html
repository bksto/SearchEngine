The Office of Alumni Relations at California State University, Dominguez Hills works to advance the university, promote the common interests of its students and its alumni, and to serve as a liaison to the university, alumni, current student population, faculty and staff, and the surrounding community. The office is the coordination hub for all programs that build greater alumni awareness, interest, and involvement with the University. Alumni Relations maintains a database of 85,000+ alumni and communicates regularly to keep them informed about their fellow alumni, campus programs and events, and opportunities to stay involved.

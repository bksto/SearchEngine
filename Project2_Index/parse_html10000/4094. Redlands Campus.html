The University of Redlands main campus resides on 160 pristine acres in the historic city of Redlands. Our campus is home to our residential College of Arts and Sciences where you can choose from 40+ majors or study abroad for a semester, and our Johnston Center for Integrative Studies where you can design your own degree. Our Redlands campus is also home to the School of Business—an environment of elevated learning specifically tailored to the needs of busy professionals and working adults, and the School of Education—Southern California’s only accelerated education program with an emphasis in educational justice. See regional locations for adult and professional education programs. In addition to our schools and colleges, you also find our top-rate facilities including the Stauffer Science Center, the "green" high performance Lewis Hall, the new Center for the Arts, the Fletcher Jones Foundation Computer Center, our state-of-the-art Fitness Center, the Thompson Aquatic Center, and the Farquhar Soccer and Recreational Fields.

The Tools for Success UCR prides itself on its support system and the career opportunities we offer our employees. Our Human Resources Department helps our staff members with everything from benefits to education and development to policies and procedures. Our staff members have access to campus recreational activities and can join our Student Recreation Center, an 80,000-square-foot, state-of-the art facility that offers basketball, weight training, racquetball courts and more. They can also take advantage of the services offered at the Schools First Credit Union gain housing and rental assistance, and peruse our campus libraries. These are only a sample of the many resources available to our outstanding staff members. We give our employees the tools they need to make a difference and enjoy their campus experiences and give back to the campus community. Staff Profile Mingji Chu and James Calkins Mingji (Matthew) Chu, a programmer analyst in the College of Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences' Dean's Office, was one of 26 employees recognized for exceptional service to the university and the community at our annual Staff Recognition Awards Ceremony. In 14 months, he developed and finished two E-Business applications that reduced staff workload, saved money and improved student services. He gives back by hosting UCR students and scholars from China in his home and volunteers in the sixth-grade Sunday School class at his church. James "Cam" Calkins, a dishwasher in Dining Services, trains new staff members and students and makes sure safety issues are addressed. He has played the piano at Staff Assembly and chancellor-related events, has taught English in a school in Indonesia and volunteered with the Salvation Army in Malaysia and Hemet.

Become a Member The Front Door to Campus Established in 2007, the Alumni & Visitors Center is the gathering place for UCR alumni, visitors and friends. It is a place to call home, a place to meet new friends and a place to reminisce with old ones. Here you can unwind in front of the fire among the works of alumni authors in our library, or view the latest alumni art exhibit. The center is also home to the offices of Alumni & Constituent Relations and the Alumni Association. Events at the CenterOur 13,865-square-foot facility offers you meeting rooms and a formal board room for your business needs. For more information, please call (951) 827-5471.

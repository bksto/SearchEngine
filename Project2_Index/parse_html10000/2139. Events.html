Volunteering for CSUDH Alumni Relations programs allows you to socialize with other alumni members and enhance your professional network while helping support current students, recruit future Toros and strengthen the university community. Our Alumni Association volunteers help with our major events such as mass mailings and Commencement as well as support our presence at other campus and community events. If you would like to participate, please email alumniprograms@csudh.edu or call (310) 243-2237.

The Trading Lab is not available for reservation. Class Room A 36-seat, fully automated classroom, complete with a call center and PC at each station. To reserve the AIM Center Classroom click here. Board Room A 20-seat executive conference room with video conferencing capability. This boardroom is accessible to graduate Cougar Fund managers, Bauer honors students, as well as business partners of the Bauer College. To reserve the Board Room area for your event click here. Lobby & Ticker area Live electronic ticker and video wall consisting of eight 52" flat plasma monitors lies adjacent to the trading laboratory. The video wall is the first of its kind in North American business schools intended for streaming donor recognition and display of real time business news and financial data.

Writing and ESL Support UC Davis students can get help with writing for ANY course they are taking. Writing Specialists lead workshops and offer appointments. Trained student tutors are available without an appointment on a drop-in basis. Visit the Writing and ESL Support page for more information on how to access these services. Math and Science Offerings UC Davis student can get course-specific help for a variety of math and science classes. Specialists lead workshops, hold office hours and provide assistance in the drop-in tutoring rooms. Trained student tutors lead study sessions as well as provide drop-in tutoring.

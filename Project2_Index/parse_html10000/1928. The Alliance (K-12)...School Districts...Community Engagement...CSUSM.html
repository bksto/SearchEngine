High School Counselors Conference - counselors from partner districts are invited to spend the day learning about the Guaranteed Admission Agreements and how to advise students to meet the criteria of the agreements. District Partner Family Reception Celebrations - high school graduates from partner districts are invited to celebrate their accomplishment and their guaranteed admission to CSUSM at these annual family receptions.

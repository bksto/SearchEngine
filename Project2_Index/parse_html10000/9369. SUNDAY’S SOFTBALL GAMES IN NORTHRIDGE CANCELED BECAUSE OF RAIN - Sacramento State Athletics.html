Social Media Central RSS Feeds Softball Home   |   Roster   |   Schedule/Results   |   Statistics   |   Coaches   |   News   SUNDAY’S SOFTBALL GAMES IN NORTHRIDGE CANCELED BECAUSE OF RAIN Mar 01, 2015 NORTHRIDGE, Calif. -- Due to rain that hit the Northridge area hard overnight, both of Sacramento State's softball games on Sunday at the CSUN/LMU Tournament were canceled because of an unplayable field. The Hornets (5-9) were supposed to play both CSU Bakersfield and Loyola Marymount today, but rain washed that plan away and both games will not be made up. Sacramento State went 2-1 at the tournament, which included victories over Bakersfield and College of Charleston, as well as a loss to tournament host CSUN. Sacramento State returns to action next week when the team plays four times - twice on Friday and twice on Saturday - in San Luis Obispo at the Mustang Classic.

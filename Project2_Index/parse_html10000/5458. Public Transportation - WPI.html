Green Airport in Providence, R.I., Bradley International Airport in Hartford, Conn., and Manchester Airport in Manchester, N.H. Airport Shuttles Worcester Airport Limousine (+1-800-660-0992; info2@wlimo.com) and Knight's Airport Limousine Service (+1-800-822-5456, visit their Web site for rates and reservations) provide transportation to and from Logan Airport and T.F. Green Airport. Buses The Worcester Regional Transit Authority (WRTA) bus system extensively covers the city of Worcester, and the WPI campus lies along two separate routes. For bus schedule information, visit their Web site or call +1-508-791-9782. The city of Worcester is serviced by two bus companies, Greyhound Lines, Inc. and Peter Pan Bus Lines. For fares and schedule information on Greyhound, call +1-800-231-2222. More information on Peter Pan, which only services the northeastern seaboard, is available via a toll-free number, +1-800-343-9999, and an email address, info@peterpanbus.com. Commuter Rail The MBTA Commuter Rail stop in Worcester is about 1.5 miles away from campus, located on Shrewsbury Street. The stop is at the end of the Worcester/Framingham line. For fare information, maps, and schedules, visit the MBTA Commuter Rail Web site or call +1-617-222-3200. Taxi Services Two taxi services provide transportation in the Worcester area: Yellow Cab, +1-508-754-3211, and Red Cab, +1-508-792-9999 & +1-508-756-9000. Trains Amtrak services the city of Worcester from over 500 cities nationwide. The Worcester Amtrak station is located on Shrewsbury Street, about 1.5 miles from the campus.

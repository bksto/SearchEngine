The time and talents you contribute to our programs will help the Alumni Association grow. You'll interact with people from a variety of professions and backgrounds while promoting the university that makes you proud. It's interesting, rewarding and fun! Our volunteer group is comprised of current Beach Students, alumni, Beach parents, staff and faculty emeriti as well as Friends of the University that enjoy donating their time to the Alumni Association and its many programs Other ways to volunteer Our volunteers provide many valuable services and are the lifeblood of the Association. Alumni Association volunteers help with our major events such as Homecoming, Concerts in the Grove and Commencement as well as support our presence at other campus and community events.  They also help us prepare the tens of thousands of pieces of postal mail we send out annually and assist with other clerical and special projects. There are many ways to get involved with the Alumni Association such as: Volunteer at Events Office Support Legislative Advocacy Board of Directors Mentoring Students Hosting Events Guest Speakers University Ambassadors and much more!

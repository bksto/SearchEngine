$6,000   Subscribers to the Material ConneXion receive: •    Access to the world’s largest online library of advanced, innovative and sustainable materials and processes (over 5,000 materials). •    Access to the Materials Research Collaborative on the UH campus with staff support and admittance to the materials library at the Material ConneXion located in New York, Bangkok, Cologne, Daegu and Milan. •    Monthly updates on the 40-50 new materials added to the online database. •    Exclusive invitations to two lectures each year at the UH College of Architecture given by recognized authorities focused on the latest trends in materials markets. •    Academic setting with VIP parking for clients by appointment to build a stronger competitive advantage. •    Better educated and trained employment force. •    Opportunities to interact with students and meet future graduates and employees with specific expertise. All founding partners and subscribers will be recognized for their support through the following: •    Prominent display on the glass entry wall of the library. •    In all printed and emailed information and on the MRC’s website. •    At all lectures and events.

Innovative Spirit The Pennovation Center is the University’s new hub of innovation, research, and entrepreneurialism. Located in the Pennovation Works, a 23-acre site along the Schuylkill River, the facility will house co-working space to support startups and entrepreneurs from the University and the private sector.

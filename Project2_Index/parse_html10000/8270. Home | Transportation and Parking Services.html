Transportation and Parking Services (TAPS) is committed to providing safe and reliable parking and transit services for all students, faculty, staff and visitors. TAPS supports the mission of the University though a collaborative effort that emphasizes integrity, fairness, cooperation, professionalism and respect. TAPS offers a variety of services to meet your transportation, parking and event needs ensuring our customers receive friendly and accommodating customer service from start to finish.

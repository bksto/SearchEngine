and School Events Calendar Student Life / Campus / Student Lounges: “Oases” In recent years, UIC transformed 20+ underutilized areas into comfortable learning centers—or Oases—where students can gather, interact, study or simply lounge while in between classes. In addition to improved lighting and ergonomics, each Oasis features vibrant artwork by UIC Art and Design students. For more information, visit the Oasis website.

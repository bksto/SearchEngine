By becoming a lifetime member of the UCR Alumni Association, you are continuing to build upon your strong relationship with the campus. It is an exciting time to be a Highlander, as our campus is thriving. Don't miss out! Become a Member The Benefits of Joining the UCR Alumni Association UCR Alumni Association members receive the benefits that are not available to the general public. Whether it is reconnecting with old friends, building up your Rolodex or to reminisce about the greatest years of your life, the UC Riverside Alumni Association is for you. Alumni Chapters and Clubs The UCR Alumni Association has chapters throughout the state, including Los Angeles, Orange County, the Inland Empire and the Bay Area. The association sponsors events across the country. Find a chapter near you and see a list of scheduled events. Alumni Association Travel Program Since 1984, the UCR Alumni Association Travel program has provided participants with opportunities to broaden their cultural horizons, foster ties with the university and enhanced camaraderie with fellow alumni while experiencing new. The Alumni Career Network The Alumni Career Network is made up of alumni who are willing to share career information with students and other alumni. Network members may be contacted to set up appointments for in-depth informational interviews. Alumni Career Network listings are located in the Career Center Library. UCR Career Center Resources for Alumni The UCR Career Center provides services designed to support your career and job search needs. Alumni Association members receive a 20% discount on all fees. For information on services for alumni or to schedule appointments with a career counselor call (951) 827-3631. Volunteer Opportunities There are a variety of volunteer opportunities for UCR Alumni. Join an Alumni Association committee, work in the Office of Alumni & Constituent Relations or talk to students about the benefits of UCR. Contact the Alumni Association to learn more.

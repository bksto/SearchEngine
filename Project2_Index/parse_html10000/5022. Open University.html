Fees are $299 per unit. In-person Registration for Spring 2015 begins on Tuesday, January 20, 2015. You may download the Spring 2015 Open University Registration Form on this page. Registration forms will also be available by 7:30 am on Tuesday, January 20, 2015 (the first day of instruction) at the CCPE Student Services registration windows located at the west end of the Foundation building. Students must obtain instructor and department chair permissions to enroll in a class. The last day to enroll for Spring 2015 is Monday, February 16, 2015. Students enrolling through Open University will receive Special Sessions Credit. Students may apply up to 24 units of Special Sessions Credit toward a baccalaureate degree. At the option of the appropriate college and department, up to 9 units of Special Sessions Credit may be applied toward a graduate degree.

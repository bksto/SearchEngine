Come back to campus to enjoy class reunions, all-school activities, and fun for the whole family. General Information  Friday, May 15, 2015 Location       5:00 p.m. or later Reunion Class Warm-ups for ’55 ,’60 ,’65 ,’70 ,’75, ’90, ’00, ’05 & ’10 Various Locations   Break the ice and shoot the breeze with old friends on campus or at a favored Redlands location.(various prices)         Saturday, May 16, 2015   9:30 - 11:30 a.m. Bulldogs in Service University Hall   Join the Office of Community Service Learning, alumni, and friends for coffee and service. Stop Hunger Now’s meal packaging program is a fun, hands-on project that is suitable for all ages. These meal packages are shipped around the world to support school feeding programs and crisis relief. Stop by and be a part of the assembly team! (complimentary)         12:00 - 3:00 p.m.  Reunion Lunches for Silver Bulldogs, ’55, ’60, ’65, ’70, ’75, ’84/’85, ’90 ,’95, ’00, ’05 & ’10 Various Locations   The people you love and remember will be waiting for you to arrive at your class reunion lunch. Your class has a special reunion location, chosen just for you, and a program that will help you build lots of great new memories. Reserve your spot now for this time of fun and friendship. (various prices)         12:00 - 2:00 p.m. Lunch in the Commons for All Bulldogs Irvine Commons   Not attending a reunion lunch? Join us in the Irvine Commons and see how today’s students eat. It’s a casual dining experience with a multitude of options available- this is not your parent’s dining hall! Look for our flagged tables to join other alumni and guests. (no host)         3:00 - 6:00 p.m. Wang Tang Watering Hole for All Bulldogs   Willis Center/    After a fun-filled day, nothing tastes better than a cool drink shared with friends. Stop by and hang out, relax and catch up with old and new friends.  (complimentary appetizers, no host bar) Patio       6:00 - 8:00 p.m.  Orkey Porkey Dinner & Toast to the Classes for All Attendees Gallery Lawn   A campus-wide celebration for all reunion classes as well as non-reunion attendees. This is a chance to celebrate with those special people who made your time at the University of Redlands so memorable. Come represent your class and dance to your decade’s music, provided by the University of Redlands’ own KDAWG Radio. We’ll welcome and embrace our newest Bulldog alumni, the Class of 2015, with a special toast celebrating their graduation and entrance into our ranks. ($50)         8:00 p.m - 12:00 a.m.  Joozy Woozy Late Night Lounge for All Bulldogs Alumni House     Keep the night hopping as the Alumni House takes on a speakeasy sparkle.This is the spot for cold drinks and hot music as you relax with a craft cocktail, smoke a cigar and mingle to the sultry sounds of Renee Rojanaro ’00, ’05 as she leads  her band, After Dark. It’s the bee’s knees!(complimentary appetizers, no host bar)         Sunday, May 17, 2015   9:30 - 11:00 a.m. Reunion Brunches for ’55, ’70, ’75, ’84/85, ’90, ’95, ’00, ’05 & ’10 Irvine Commons   If you’re leaving on a jet plane, or just shuffling off to Bakersfield, a casual breakfast at the new and improved Irvine Commons will get you rolling in the right direction. Fill up before you go and have a last laugh with old friends. See you next time! (no host)         9:30 - 11:00 a.m. Reunion Brunch for ’60  Alumni House   Join your classmates for one last event before you depart campus. Come up to the Alumni House to enjoy the view, eat a delicious brunch, and bid farewell to friends. ($25)         9:30 - 11:00 a.m. Reunion Brunch for ’65 Miraflores   In honor of your landmark reunion year, the University is providing a complimentary brunch at Miraflores, the President’s Residence. This is the perfect way to end your 50th reunion weekend.

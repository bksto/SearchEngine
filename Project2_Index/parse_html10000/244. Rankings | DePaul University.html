Here's a sampling. DePaul University The largest Catholic university, and the 12th largest private, not-for-profit university in the nation. (2013) Ranked No. 3 for best college cities in the U.S. by The Princeton Review in The Best 379 Colleges. (2014) U.S.News & World Report ranked DePaul No. 121 among almost 1,800 national universities. (2013) Ranked in the top 10 of schools producing the largest percentage of interns by U.S.News & World Report. (2012) The Princeton Review ranked DePaul students among the happiest in the United States in its “Best Colleges” publications four times since 1999.​​​ U.S.News & World Report included DePaul among universities with “hot” college majors for its computer game design program. (2012) Diversity DePaul received the Higher Education Excellence in Diversity award from INSIGHT into Diversity magazine.​ (2014) Diverse Issues in Higher Education recognized DePaul as being among the top 100 institutions that grant degrees to minority students in 40 disciplines. (2011) Ranked 19th in Diversity MBA Magazine’s 2012 Best Places for Diverse Managers to Work national survey. (2012) Hispanic Outlook cited DePaul as one of the top 100 institutions awarding bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees to Hispanics. (2011) Community Service and Sustainability The Princeton Review's Guide to 322 Green Colleges included DePaul for the third consecutive year. (2012) DePaul was admitted to the President’s Higher Education Community Service Honor Roll for its strong institutional commitment to service and campus-community partnerships. (2013) Named among the nation’s top 25 service-learning programs by U.S.News & World Report. (2011) Business  The Princeton Review ranked DePaul’s undergraduate entrepreneurship major 15th and graduate entrepreneurship program 20th nationally among more than 2,000 schools surveyed for its Best Schools for Entrepreneurs. (2014) Ranked No. 3 nationally for undergraduate business program being "a good value" among private universities by BusinessWeek. (2010) Arts The School of Cinematic Arts is ranked in the top 20 of film schools in the nation by The Hollywood Reporter. (2014) Ranked among the top 25 drama schools in the world by The Hollywood Reporter. (2012) Animation Career Review ranked the College of Computing and Digital Media among the top 100 schools in animation. (2012) Technology Ranked among the top 15 graduate schools to study video game design in the country by The Princeton Review. (2014) Communication The DePaulia student newspaper won 15 awards from the Illinois College Press Association, including first place for General Excellence for a Weekly Big-School Newspaper. (2014) The Public Relations and Advertising program was named one of five national finalists for 'PR Education Program of the Year' by the PR WeekAwards. (2013) Student-run Radio DePaul was named the nation's 'Best Streaming College Radio Station' by the Intercollegiate Broadcasting System.

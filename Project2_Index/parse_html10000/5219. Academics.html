UC Santa Cruz offers an innovative, interdisciplinary education with a focus on undergraduate learning. Combining the advantages of a small college setting with the resources of a major research university, UCSC's faculty and researchers often partner with undergraduate students in their labs and research activities, and students co-publish papers with their professors.

The staff is responsible for creating publications, flyers, posters, reports, advertising, promotional campaigns, internal and external communication. This office is also responsible for all the college media contacts, event organizations, and public relations services. The Marketing and Public Relations Department is dedicated to producing quality print and Web services for the college. The department staff is responsible for the monitoring, design, execution and consulting of a wide variety of the college's print and web publications, such as catalogs, brochures, and newsletters, recruitment materials, as well as promotional give-away-items. The department maintains the Chaffey College image by making sure that all printed collateral preserves the college's quality image and identity and is aligned with the college mission.

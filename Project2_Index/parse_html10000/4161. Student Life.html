The Office of Student Affairs & Academic Support Services serves to educate, engage, and empower each student at New York University College of Dentistry to realize their full academic and professional potential. By collaborating with students, faculty, and staff, the Office supports and enhances the educational experience and the entire student journey, both inside and outside the classroom.

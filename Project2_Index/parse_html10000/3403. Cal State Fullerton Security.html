CSUF Home Page Login Security Q: The lock, which appears on my browser whenever I visit a secure page, does not show up on the CSUF home page. Is it safe to enter my "Log In" information on this page? A: Yes. It is safe to enter your "Login" information on the CSUF home page. Your Username and Password are not transmitted until you click on the "Login" button. Upon doing so, a secure session is established between your browser and our systems. Your information is then encrypted using a 128-bit encryption algorithm (the strongest encryption method commercially available) and sent to our systems for authentication.

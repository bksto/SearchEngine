The Architecture Computer Lab offers a variety of programs on the windows platform allowing students to create drawings, BIM models, parametric models, and solid models. There are over 40 computers, an 11x17 scanner, a large format scanner, 2 printers, and 5 plotters to allow students to conceptualize and realize their creative designs.

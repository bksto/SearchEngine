Benefits of our scholarly community include class sizes capped at 20 students, priority class registration, additional academic advising, an Honors Lounge, peer mentoring, student-faculty dinners, newsletters, an Honors Student Government, cultural outings, service activities, and many other experiences that enrich the Honors community while extending the Honors experience beyond the classroom. To learn more, visit the Honors Program website. Honors Program Admission Information If you are an already-admitted student who demonstrates strong academic motivation and would like to be challenged by a rigorous academic curriculum at the university level, you are encouraged to apply to the University Honors Program. The Honors Admission Committee individually reviews each Honors application, taking into consideration all components of the student’s academic profile, particularly the content and quality of the essay responses. To apply for the University Honors Program please complete the online application form​. The Honors Program has a limited number of spaces, and we make admission decisions on applications as they arrive. Priority consideration will be given to those students who submit an application on or before Monday, February 2, 2015 (Priority Consideration Deadline).

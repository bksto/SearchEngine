Academics University of Redlands > Academics > School of Education > Student Resources > 2015 Commencement 2015 Commencement The University of Redlands is hosting the 2015 School of Education Commencement events on April 15-17, 2015, at our Redlands campus. All eligible students are highly encouraged to participate in these events. Our Commencement is a ceremonious event that allows the University to formally honor students and recognize their recent academic accomplishments in the presence of families, friends, peers, staff, and faculty. Please note that all events during Commencement, whether indoors or out, including the Greek Theatre are non-smoking events. 2015 Commencement Information Handout 2015 Application to Participate in School of Education Commencement  Purchase tickets for the April 15th Commencement dinner Cap, gown, and tassel can be purchased at the University bookstore or online (shipping extra). Gowns are sold by height. Master's candidates will also need to purchase a light blue hood. Graduation announcements are also available for purchase online.

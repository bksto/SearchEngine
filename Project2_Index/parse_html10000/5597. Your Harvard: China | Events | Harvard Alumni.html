The evening will also feature an interactive discussion with distinguished faculty and alumni exploring innovations that will shape the global research agenda for the next decade—from advanced visualizations around buildings and cities to bioimaging and nanoscale technology. Speakers include: Ali Malkawi, Professor of Architectural Technology, Harvard Graduate School of Design; Founding Director, Harvard Center for Green Buildings and Cities George Yeo MBA '85, Chairman, Kerry Logistics Xiaowei Zhuang, David B. Arnold Jr. Professor of Science, Faculty of Arts and Sciences  Doors open at 6:00 p.m. Business attire is requested, and a touch of crimson is encouraged. Spouses and guests are welcome.

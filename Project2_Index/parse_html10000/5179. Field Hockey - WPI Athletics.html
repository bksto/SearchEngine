Senior Nicole Holmes (Plymouth North) was one of 37 WPI students to receive NEWMAC Academic All-Conference consideration for the fall sports season. Former opponents on Thanksgiving, sophomore Jason Lamb (Plymouth South) and junior Vinny Tavernelli (Plymouth North) are now teammates on the Engineers football team. Freshman Brendan Harty (Plymouth South) is also on the squad. [read more] Puishys and Cervelloni Named to All-NEWMAC Field Hockey Team November 11, 2014 Boston, MA --- Juniors Lauren Puishys and Abby Cervelloni were placed on the NEWMAC Field Hockey All-Conference Second Team following a vote by the league’s head coaches following the regular season. The duo never left the field, racking up just over 1332 minutes each in 18 contests. [read more] 37 Fall Student-Athletes Land on NEWMAC Academic All-Conference Teams November 6, 2014 Boston, MA --- WPI saw 37 student-athletes across six fall sports collect NEWMAC All-Academic honors, as announced by the conference office. Nine Engineers made it a perfect 3-for-3 while 10 more received their second citation and three teams boasted eight honorees. [read more] Field Hockey Edged by MIT 1-0 in NEWMAC Tournament Quarterfinal Box Score November 4, 2014 Cambridge, MA --- A goal by senior Caitlin Mackey in the 46th minute was all MIT needed as it held on to defeat WPI in a quarterfinal game of the NEWMAC Field Hockey Championship Tournament on Tuesday night. With the win, No. 3 seed MIT (12-4) secured its sixth straight trip to the semifinals, extending the longest active streak of semifinal appearances within the conference. Meanwhile, No. 6 WPI, which clinched its first playoff berth since 2011, closed the season with an overall record of 11-7.

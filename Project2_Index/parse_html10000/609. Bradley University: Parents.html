Everything from the academic calendar to student activities and more can be found here to help you and more importantly your student to be a success here at Bradley. As a Bradley parent, guardian, spouse, or another family member of a student you're likely to have questions from time to time, about the University, our campus, curriculum, services and policies, or about your student. The links at left and below will take you to targeted areas within our Web site that are most likely to answer those questions. The University has numerous departments that are designed to provide vital services to our students. If you don't find the information you're looking for here, please feel free to contact the appropriate department listed in our A to Z Index or use our "where do you go for help" to send your question to us. Bradley recognizes that families play an important role in assisting students and in supporting them in their university experience and personal development. We know that a university experience marks a major transition point in peoples' lives, and it is our hope that we can help support that transition for both our students and their families.

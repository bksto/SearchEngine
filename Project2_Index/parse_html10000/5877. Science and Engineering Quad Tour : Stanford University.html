ABOUT THE TOUR Highlights This tour covers the Science and Engineering Quad, including: Hewlett and Packard Buildings Huang Engineering Center Y2E2 Building Duration 1 hour Cost Free Schedule Dates and times for this tour generally vary from academic quarter to quarter. The tour is generally offered 2-3 times per week. The tour may be limited or closed during holiday or academic break periods. Please also note that dates and times may vary depending on guide availability. If the SEQ tour is not available on the day of your visit or you would like to explore the School of Engineering on your own, you can download a self-guided tour: Jen-Hsun Huang Engineering Center Self-Guided Tour For additional information see: Stanford Engineering at a Glance View Schedule for details and to make reservations. Reservations The Science and Engineering Quad Tour requires reservations, and sessions fill quickly. Thus we recommend that you make your on-line reservation well in advance. The tour is not open to large groups and is limited to four people per party. Prospective Students: Planning a Spring Visit? We strongly encourage prospective students planning a visit to do so either before March 14 or after April 7 in order to maximize the value of the visit. These are our busiest weeks of the year, and they coincide with Stanford's final exams and our own Spring Break — large crowds are thus paired with limited offerings and greatly reduced tour guide staffing. To truly get a sense of what Stanford is like, come during an academic quarter in order to sit in on classes, enjoy smaller tour groups, and take part in special interest tours and programs.

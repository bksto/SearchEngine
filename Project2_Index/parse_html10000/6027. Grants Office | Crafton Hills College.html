For students seeking financial aid including grants and scholarships, go to the Financial Aid Office. The goals of the Grants Office are to: 1. Link Crafton Hills College priorities to external funding sources; 2. Train faculty and staff on grant development and management; and 3. Help project directors and coordinators manage their grants to ensure compliance with federal, state and private funder requirements. Grant Searching: Crafton Hills College subscribes to Grant Station, which offers grant-writing tutorials and a database of available grants that is updated daily. Take a look: www.grantstation.com.

Eller MBA Programs Master of Accounting Master of Science Accounting Master of Finance Master of Management Information Systems Online Master of MIS Doctoral Programs   Rogers College of Law Founded in 1915, the University of Arizona James E. Rogers College of Law is the oldest law school in the state of Arizona and one of the premier public law schools in the United States.  Several programs in the Rogers College of Law are within the Graduate College.   College of Medicine, TUCSON Faculty members at the College of Medicine explore every aspect of health and disease, from the molecular mechanisms of gene action to population studies of disease prevention. Their work has contributed to improved understanding of the cause of diseases and to better treatments and prevention of disorders. The College of Medicine has a few degrees in the Graduate College.   College of Medicine, Phoenix As a full, four-year program based in downtown Phoenix, the UA College of Medicine – Phoenix focuses on training exemplary physicians, scientists and leaders. This independently accredited college boasts an innovative, technologically advanced curriculum, and a wide variety of clinical partners. The Professional Studies and Health Sciences Certificate is in the Graduate College.   College of Pharmacy As the only public college of pharmacy in the state, we educate future pharmacists, train those who want to specialize, and offer continuing education to practicing pharmacists. Learn to discover new drugs, cure diseases, evaluate therapies, determine treatment outcomes, and assess healthcare policy.   Zuckerman College of Public Health Study, research and get involved with the community at Arizona's first and only accredited college of public health.

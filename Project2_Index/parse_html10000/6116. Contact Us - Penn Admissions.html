We are eager to connect applicants, parents, and counselors with the information they need to navigate the admissions process. We are here to help. If you are an applicant, up-to-date information can be found on the Applicant Homepage. The fastest way for us to respond is by e-mail or phone. Your questions can be submitted using the inquiry email menu below or by calling us. Office of Admissions University of Pennsylvania Office of Admissions 1 College Hall, Room 1 Philadelphia, PA 19104-6376 Email: info@admissions.upenn.edu Phone: (215) 898-7507 Fax: (215) 898-9670 While we are unable to accommodate appointments or interview requests with specific admissions staff, an admissions officer is on-duty and available to speak with you over the phone or in person at our office. Contact us by region if you have more specific questions that you believe need to be directed to an admissions officer. Priority will be given to questions with answers not found on our website.

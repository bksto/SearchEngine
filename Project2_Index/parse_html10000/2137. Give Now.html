All gifts, no matter what size, make a huge difference and will be used to support scholarship funds, academic programs and the student experience. Here are ways you can make a donation right now: Online Giving Use your Visa or Mastercard to make a gift online with our secure form. You can also set up recurring payment plans. By Phone Call (310) 243-2182 Office hours: Monday–Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. (PST) Please have the following information available when calling: Class year of donor, if applicable Amount of gift Whether you want to set up a recurrent payment Where you want your gift designated Credit card number and expiration date You will receive a receipt acknowledging the amount of your gift. By Mail Please make your check payable to CSUDH Foundation and mail it to: CSU Dominguez Hills Office of Development Welch Hall 425A 1000 East Victoria Street Carson, CA 90747 Please include the following information with your check: Class year of donor, if applicable Where you want your gift designated You will receive a receipt acknowledging the amount of your gift in the mail within 10 working days of the date of your donation.

The advantages of requesting this leave are: No readmission application is required if all of the steps to request leave status are completed prior to study abroad departure; Registration pass times will be established in GOLD to allow registration prior to returning to UCSB, and; State and federal financial aid may be available for students who have established financial aid eligibility for the UCSB campus. Choosing to request leave status in association with study abroad involves preparation in advance of departure. The following links provide valuable information and access to forms.

The Department of Financial Services is committed to supporting the academic mission of the University by providing financial services to meet the needs of the entire Cal State Fullerton community. Our mission is to: provide efficient, quality, customer-oriented service. provide prudent financial guidance and management of the University's fiscal resources. ensure that all expenditures, reconciliation, accounting, and reporting functions comply with laws and policies. develop technology tools and training programs which facilitate approved business practices and fiduciary responsibilities.

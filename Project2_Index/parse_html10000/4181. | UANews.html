The Festival brings authors from a variety of subject areas and diverse genres. It's your chance to meet your favorite authors, discover new works, and hear from up-and-coming writers. The Festival's Science City gives attendees an opportunity to engage in hands-on activities in five science "neighborhoods." Brought to you by the University of Arizona's College of Science and BIO5 Institute there are over 100 hands-on activities, exciting demonstrations, informative talks, author panels, book signings, and fascinating presentations for all ages. The Tucson Festival of Books is located on the University of Arizona Mall along East University Boulevard, stretching from Old Main to east of Cherry Avenue, and in several nearby buildings.

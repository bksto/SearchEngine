No two students learn exactly the same way. SBVC's specialized programs are here to help. Beyond the typical classroom experience are unique programs whose aim is to help student reach their career and educational goals.   From high school students looking to concurrently take college classes to the award-winning Valley-Bound Commitment program that offers a free first year of college to local high school graduates, SBVC has a variety of specialized programs available to support student success.

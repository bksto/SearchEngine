We phone alumni, parents and friends of Bradley and update their contact information, tell them what’s happening on campus, and secure gifts for the University. The Bradley Fund Phone a Thon is one large family! The student callers are led by the Bradley Fund Intern team. The BFund calling room has been described as the best job on campus! We emphasize team work and goal setting. It’s great to be able to reach alumni and talk about their lives and their experiences at Bradley. You are always welcome to drop in and see how things are going in the Bradley Fund Phone room!   Bradley Fund Interns Hannah, Cody, Daniala, Deanna Team Dragonite!

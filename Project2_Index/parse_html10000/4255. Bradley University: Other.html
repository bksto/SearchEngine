Consulting, management and construction oversight services are also conducted for several communications systems on and off campus, including tower management, and cooperation with CES for cell phone providers, cable TV projects, emergency telecommunications for police, fire and other FCC controlled services. Center for Testing The Center for Testing is a central campus location that is used for educational and career testing for Bradley students, business and industry professionals and members of the community. Communication and Engineering Services Communication and Engineering Services (CES) provides system design, installation and maintenance for Bradley University’s telecommunications, Digital Compressed Video and networking systems. Computing Services Bradley’s Computing Services Department, which includes the Technology HelpDesk is responsible for overseeing the University’s technological functions such as its wireless Internet, e-mail services and MyBU. Information Resources and Technology Information Resources and Technology creates Bradley’s technological environment. IRT is comprised of computing services, broadcast engineering, communication engineering services and Instructional Technology and Media Services departments. Instructional Technology and Media Services Instructional Technology & Media Services offers assistance to the University community through six service areas: Audio Visual Services, Audio/Video Production, Duplicating, Graphics, Mediated Classrooms and Videoconferencing. Student Financial Services Financial Assistance, Students Fees/Billing and Student loans comprise Bradley's Student Financial Services (SFS)---a one-stop experience for billing and bill payments, financial aid and scholarships, and student and parent borrowing. University Police Utilizing community-oriented policing, the Bradley University Police Department works to enhance quality of life, create a safe living environment and promote a positive learning experience for students, faculty, staff and community members on and off campus.

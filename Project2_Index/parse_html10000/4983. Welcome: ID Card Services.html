With your Student/ Faculty/ Staff ID card, you can access an array of services/privileges all over campus, including the use of: Library services, Beach Club debit, Meal Plans, Computer lab access, copiers, and more. Who Needs It? Everyone. The CSULB ID card is an all-purpose card that is required for all students, faculty, and staff. Your ID Card is your official form of identification while a member of the CSULB community. How do I get an ID Card? Go to the ID Card Services Office located in the University Bookstore near the textbook aisles Present a a government issued ID such as a driver's license, passport, or a state ID Proof of registration Fill out an application and agreement form Questions and comments? Contact IDCard@csulb.edu. Location The ID Card Office is located in the University Bookstore near the Supplies Department.

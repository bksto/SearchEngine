And with the cost of higher education today, it also stands as one of the largest. So it’s understandable that prospective students and their families will have concerns and questions about how to best pay for college. WPI offers a variety of options that can help lower the cost of college to varying degrees. These include: Financial aid packages Merit-based scholarships On-campus employment Loan programs and payment plans for parents To determine the steps needed to apply for financial aid at WPI please select your enrollment status from the list below or from the list under Applying for Financial Aid in the menu on the left.

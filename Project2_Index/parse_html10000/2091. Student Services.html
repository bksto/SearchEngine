Career Center Find internship opportunities, conduct job searches, receive career counseling, attend hiring events, build your resumé, practice your interview skills and connect with employers who actively hire CSUDH graduates. The Career Center at CSUDH is here to help you reach your professional goals. Student Health Student Health and Psychological Services are available to CSUDH students seeking basic healthcare from medical professionals as well as psychological counseling and resources to address personal issues and goals. Getting Involved CSUDH offers a dynamic campus community with countless opportunities to get involved, make connections and enjoy your university experience to the fullest. Here you’ll find information on CSUDH’s Student Government Body, clubs and organizations, Greek life and the Office of Student Life.   Veteran Services CSUDH offers a veteran-friendly campus with a Veteran Student Program office dedicated to helping veterans and active duty service members transition to civilian life, access their education benefits and connect with helpful resources. Disabled Student Services Disabled Student Services (DSS) is focused on making sure students with disabilities have full access to CSUDH’s educational, culture, social and physical facilities and programs. Services include admissions assistance, priority registration, on-campus housing, special accommodations and resource referrals. Students with disabilities have a responsibility to initiative contact to request services and arrange accommodations. Know Your Rights Familiarize yourself with your rights and responsibilities as a CSUDH student, including important academic policies and procedures, student conduct, anti-hazing policies and alcohol and substance abuse policies.

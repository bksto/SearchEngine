Feel free to download a print friendly campus map with parking and other important information. From the East (Chicago, Bloomington/Normal, St. Louis) At the I-74/I-474 split, stay to the right and continue on I-74 West. After entering the Peoria Metro area, take the University Street exit (91) and stay in the left lane. Turn left on to University Street and continue south for approximately four blocks. After going through the intersection of University and Main Streets, stay in the right lane and enter Bradley's campus. From the West (Galesburg, Quad Cities) Just outside of Peoria, at the I-74/I-474 split, continue on I-74 East. After entering the Peoria Metro area, take the University Street exit (91) and turn right heading south on University Street for approximately four blocks. After going through the intersection of University and Main Streets, stay in the right lane and enter Bradley's campus.

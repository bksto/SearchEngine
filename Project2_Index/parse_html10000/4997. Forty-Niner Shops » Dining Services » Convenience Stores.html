Located throughout campus, our four convenient stores offer a variety of snacks, drinks, school supplies, scantrons, and grab n’ go meals. Be sure to spend some time eating or studying on our open air patio.  Inside, you will find a variety of food options that range from sandwiches and salads to soups and chili.  We also have many hot foods such as pizza slices, burritos, hot pockets, nachos, hot dogs, hamburgers and other hot sandwiches.  When you are done with your meal, you may want to treat yourself to something frozen from our selection of ice cream, milkshakes or fruit smoothies. You will also find that we carry blue books and scantrons along with other school items that you can pick up on the way to class.  Finally we do offer a limited selection of health items such as aspirin or band aids to handle that last minute emergency. Bookstore Convenience Store The Beach Bookstore Convenience Store is located immediately next to the University Bookstore and offers a variety of products you may find helpful in the classroom or just what you were craving.  Curb your sweet tooth with a bag of delicious bulk candy of your choice or enjoy a healthy packaged sandwich. Corner Market Convenience Store Located on the west side of the University Student Union upper deck overlooking the Beach pool, this store offers both your traditional grab n’ go items as well as a limited line of CSULB logo wear.  If you’re on your way to a campus event or want to sport Beach gear before class, this is the place to grab a sweater and a snack and you’re on your way! The Outpost Convenience Store The Outpost Convenience store was added to the 49er Shops with the renovation of the popular Outpost Grill.  This convenience store offers quick grab n’ go options and a beautifully renovated patio for your to enjoy.  No need to run up campus for last minute school supplies – stop by the Outpost Convenient Store instead.  You can find us on the east side of campus near the Vivian Engineer Center.

Technology also plays an important role on our campus. The new James L. Welch Hall boasts of five mediated-instruction classrooms, nine computer-based laboratories, two workstation rooms, and the 250-seat Claudia Hampton Lecture Hall. The University Library opened the campus' first-ever Internet cafe, a laid back alternative to the many computer labs. Our campus locations include smart classrooms, labs, conference rooms, and more. Faculty and staff can reserve a location for their next meeting, conference, or training session.

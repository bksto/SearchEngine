This on-demand book maker creates a library-quality paperback. Check out our video demo on our YouTube channel. What can I print? Self-publishing: A book or other original work that you wrote Between 40-800 pages in black & white (book cover in color) Minimum size is 4.5" × 5.5"; max is 7" × 10"; most common is 6" × 9" On-demand books: Search and print more than 7 million on-demand book titles: Search for on-demand books: How much does self-publishing cost? $5 one-time setup, $2.50 for a book cover, plus: Number of Pages Cost Per Page 40-100 7 cents 101-150 6 cents 151-500 5 cents 501-800 4 cents We can also bind a previously printed book for just $4. Customization options: $5 per file for file conversion and $25 per hour for consultation (cover and/or page formatting). You can register your book with an ISBN and place a barcode on the back cover for $35. Standard rates for shipping may apply. All sales are final. How do I use the self-publishing service? Format your file following the submission requirements. Fill out the self-publishing form. Make an appointment with us at (520) 621-6411 or LBRY-askedc@email.arizona.edu.

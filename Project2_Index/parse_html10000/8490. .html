About Redlands University of Redlands > About Redlands > Office of the President > President's Cabinet > Vice President, Enrollment Vice President, Enrollment Kevin Dyerly '00 '04 returns to the University of Redlands to assume the role of the first vice president for enrollment. He came from Whitman College where he served as director of admissions. As director, Kevin was responsible for all phases of Whitman’s admissions and recruitment efforts including oversight of admission operations and the professional staff. Prior to joining the admission organization at Whitman College in 2005, Kevin worked for the University of Redlands as associate dean of admissions of the College of Arts and Sciences. He received his bachelor’s degree in business administration in 2000 and his MBA in 2004 from the University of Redlands. He serves on the executive board of the Pacific Northwest Association of College Admission Counselors, and currently holds the position of president. Office of the Vice President for Enrollment The vice president for enrollment serves as the University’s chief enrollment officer responsible for the strategy and management of all aspects of enrollment at the institution, including recruitment, admissions and yield. The vice president provides executive leadership in the planning, development and administration of University-wide strategic enrollment initiatives that are aggressive, innovative and consistent with the values, goals and mission of the University. The vice president for enrollment serves as a member of the President’s Cabinet, reporting to the President.

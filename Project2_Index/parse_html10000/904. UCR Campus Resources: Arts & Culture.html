The Arts are Alive at UCR Whether you're a spectator or a participant, the arts at UC Riverside will engage your senses, inspire your creativity, ignite your intellect, and spark your imagination. From our live student performances in music, theatre and dance, to thought-provoking art exhibitions at ARTSblock, to top-name entertainers at our UC Riverside Presents series, UCR's creative spirit and energetic diversity have earned us a reputation across the region as a thriving arts environment.

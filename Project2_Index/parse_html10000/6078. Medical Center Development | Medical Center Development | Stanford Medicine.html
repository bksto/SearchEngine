Learn More Show Front Bend the curve on health-care cost Show Back Bend the curve The Clinical Excellence Research Center is finding new ways to deliver better health care at lower costs. Philanthropic funding will help support research and training to propel the science of health care value improvement, nationally and globally. Learn More Show Front Transform Cancer Care Show Back Transform Transforming Cancer Care is an effort to completely re-imagine the delivery of cancer care and shape the future of cancer science with the help of philanthropy. The effort will leverage disruptive innovation to deliver a new, transformative model of care. Learn More Show Front Harness the power of big data Show Back Harness the power The Stanford Biomedical Data Science Initiative will find ways of using big data to improve global health. The initiative will develop a blueprint the whole world can use to share knowledge, uncover new discoveries, and harness the power of large scale computing and data analysis to shape health care in the 21st century. Learn More Show Front Create an environment of innovation Show Back Create an environment The Biomedical Innovation Building will be a state-of-the-art facility that fully supports the talent and vision of Stanford Medicine's research innovators. The space will house leaders from genetics, cardiovascular medicine, and related fields who will collaborate on a synergistic mix of research and treatment programs. Show Front Advancing Excellence In addition to the initiatives above, the Campaign for Stanford Medicine is making investments in key programs that support teaching, research, and patient care across Stanford University School of Medicine and Stanford Health Care.

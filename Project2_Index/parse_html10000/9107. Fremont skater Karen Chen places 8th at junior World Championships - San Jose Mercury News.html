Chen, 15, was 12th after the short program but improved to score 157.30 points, which still was 28 points from reaching the podium. Chen also finished eighth in the free skate. Russian star Evgenia Medvedeva, 15, won the gold medal by almost seven points over teammate Serafima Sakhanovich, 14. Japan's top junior Wakaba Higuchi, 14, got the bronze while another top Russian, Maria Sotskova, jumped from 10th to fifth overall. Kevin Shum of Piedmont finished 16th out of 24 skaters in the men's short program. Shum scored 61.61 points, just short of his personal best.

Daire, Ph.D., Professor & Associate Dean for Research Dr. Daire guides the overall operations of the college’s research office and is responsible for advancing the research culture in the COE while serving as a catalyst for the ongoing cutting-edge research that advances the educational, health, and quality of life experiences of local, state, national, and international communities. Specifically, he will develop and oversee (a) faculty development initiatives; (b) seed research, college structural and operational support initiatives; and (c) local, national, and international collaborations and partnerships to support expansion of research efforts. Contact: apdaire@uh.edu or 713-743-5443. Lori Armstrong, MBA, CRA, Research Liaison Officer Lori Armstrong is the Authorized Signing Official on behalf of the University for pre-award research activities in the College of Education. She is the “Liaison” between COE and other colleges on campus, other universities, and external funding agencies. Lori assists faculty in searching for research funding opportunities and coordinates the proposal development and submission process. She provides guidance to faculty and staff on “tools” (RAMP, RD2K, PIVOT, Cayuse, etc.) as well as interpretation and implementation of University and Federal policies and regulations. In addition to specific pre-award activities, Lori oversees the day-to-day activities within the Office of Research. Contact: laarmstrong@uh.edu or 713-743-9838. Lena Mitchell, Research Administrator Lena Mitchell is responsible for the financial management of all funded research projects. She coordinates the financial implementation of awards, expense monitoring, budget administration, and reporting requirements. Contact: lmitchell@central.uh.edu or 713-743-5011. Lee Hofweber, Office Assistant II Lee Hofweber provides general support to the COE Office of Research. In his role, Lee assists with post-award financial transactions, coordination of regulatory documents (COI, Effort, etc.), will oversee the Office’s support of undergraduate research initiatives (PURS, SURF, and development of portal connecting URAs with faculty), coordinating COE faculty research awards, and the general office administration of the COE Office of Research. Contact: lmhofweber@uh.edu or 713-743-7241. Vikrant Lakhanpal, Graduate Assistant Vikrant Lakhanpal assists in the coordination of COE OOR projects and initiatives, preparing reports, coordinating trainings and workshops, and other office administrative support. Contact: vlakhanp@central.uh.edu.

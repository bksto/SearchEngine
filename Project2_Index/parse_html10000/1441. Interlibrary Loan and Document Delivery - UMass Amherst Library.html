Interlibrary Loan  Is the item unavailable at the UMass Amherst Libraries? Get it from other collections! Current and retired UMass Amherst faculty and staff, and current students may use Interlibrary Loan services to obtain a loan of books and other materials from a library beyond the Five Colleges or to obtain copies of articles and other materials from any library at no charge. Library Express Are the UMass Amherst Libraries too far away? Are you too busy to come to the Library? We deliver! Current UMass Amherst faculty, students, and staff may request delivery of articles or books from the UMass Amherst library collections via Library Express.

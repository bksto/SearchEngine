Workstation Program In this section Get Started Requesting Accounts Get Help Related Links Accounts & Passwords Your Cal Poly account and password act as a key to many online services offered by the University such as Email, Calendar, WiFi, PolyLearn, and administrative tools. Get Started Applicants Visit the Admissions Access Help site to activate your account. New Students, Faculty, or Staff Call or visit the Service Desk at (805) 756-7000 to request a temporary password. Requesting Accounts Applicants, Students, Faculty, and Staff These accounts are automatically created for all people officially associated with the University. Affiliates These accounts are used to request a new account for an individual who is not officially associated with the University. These accounts must be paid for by the sponsoring Cal Poly organization. To request an account, enter a Technical Service Request. Department/Service Accounts These accounts are used to request an organizational account for University departments, units, or services. To request an account, enter a Technical Service Request. Get Help If you are new to Cal Poly, call or visit the Service Desk: Location: Building 46 Phone: (805) 756-7000 Forgot your password? Click the Forgot Password link on the MyCalPoly Portal and provide answers to your security questions. Need to change your password? Visit the MyCalPoly Portal and click the Personal Info tab. Need help requesting an account? Visit the MyCalPoly Portal and click the Technical Service Request single-click link.

Recruiting at UC Riverside brings with it all the advantages of the prestigious University of California system, as well as our own unique traditions and heritage. Our students have unparalleled access to cutting-edge research with world-class faculty, providing them with invaluable training and experience for today’s jobs. UC Riverside students are prepared to fit the needs of your company today. We offer the largest Business Administration undergraduate degree program of any UC in Southern California, and our students excel in high demand fields such as engineering, nano-technology, bio-engineering, computer science, chemistry and biological sciences.

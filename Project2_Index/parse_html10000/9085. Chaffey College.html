Join us at Chaffey College where you will experience a tradition of teaching excellence and extensive support services. Chaffey College offers small classes, excellent instructors and learning facilities, and free tutoring to assist international students in successfully completing their college courses. International students benefit greatly from attending Chaffey College because faculty and staff demonstrate a commitment to the academic and personal potential of each student and genuine interest in helping students succeed. As a Chaffey College student, you can expect to gain a clear advantage in the fulfillment of your persona and professional goals. A Chaffey education offers you a successful and meaningful career path for jobs of the future. A Chaffey experience offers you lifelong friendships and learning in a safe environment in beautiful Southern California.

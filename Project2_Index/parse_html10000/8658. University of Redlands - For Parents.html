So whether you’re just around the corner or clear across the country, we want to help you stay connected to your child and the Bulldog family. Local parents are encouraged to join “Town & Gown,” a membership and service organization dedicated to strengthening ties between the university and the region. Annual dues are reasonably priced at just $45. To join or for more information click here. Regional Chapters are another terrific way to stay engaged. New groups are popping up across the country, so if you have an interest in joining an existing club or starting a new one contact us at Bulldog Regional Chapters. For additional events you are sure to enjoy go to our event page. College life can be a challenge for parents and students alike. Our staff is well trained to help make the adjustment to college a successful one for everyone involved. So, please do not hesitate to call on any one of us. Important resource links are included here. For additional assistance, call the University Relations Office at 909-748-8840.

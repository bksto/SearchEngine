We also offer a wide range of online classes for students who prefer to learn at their own pace – when and where it is most convenient. And, our Open University program allows individuals to take regular Cal Poly Pomona courses without university admission. Local, regional and international employers rely on the CEU to provide customized training programs to meet the immediate needs of today’s workforce and tomorrow’s leaders. And, our Study Abroad program allows students to travel the world while earning credits toward their degree. No matter what your educational need, the College of the Extended University is Your Avenue to Success.

Thanks to the support of alumni, friends and members of the campus community, the Campaign for Cal Poly Pomona exceeded its goal of raising $150 million. Learn more about the campaign. You can make a difference. Cal Poly Pomona was founded on a legacy of giving. Former cereal magnate W.K. Kellogg donated his winter ranch in Pomona for educational use. His land eventually became the core of the Cal Poly Pomona campus, paving the way for generations of people to benefit from higher education. Today, the university enjoys a strong foundation of supporters who are essential in building excellence. We thank you for your interest in making a lasting difference at the university. Through gifts large and small, we can touch the lives of current and future students, promote research, support programs and help the greater community.

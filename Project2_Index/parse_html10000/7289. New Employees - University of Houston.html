Welcome to our New Hire class of February 9, 2015! We are happy that you have joined the UH family.  Whether you are joining us as a new faculty, staff or student employee, know that you have joined a great organization with great people who want to make a difference both in the student’s university experience and in future innovation initiatives. As you begin your journey as an employee at UH there are several points of information that you need to know and deadlines that require action from you.  The information below will cover new hire mandatory training requirements, benefits sign-up deadlines, required direct deposit set up and the Affordable Care Act notification.  Please make sure that you read the information completely and take action on those items that affect you. NEW HIRE MANDATORY TRAINING NEW HIRE ORIENTATION PRESENTATION NEW HIRE ORIENTATION HR SERVICE CENTER  W-4 As a new hire your tax status is automatically set up as Single with 0 allowances by IRS regulations. This is the HIGHEST taxing amount for withholding tax.  You are responsible for logging into PASS (People’s Advantage Self Service) and setting up your W4 withholding status.

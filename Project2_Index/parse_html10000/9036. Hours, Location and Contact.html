Nearest Parking Lots: Parking lot 25 West Entry Parking Structure The Activities and Recreation Center parking lot has day permits for $9, which can be purchased from machines in the lots (cash or credit cards accepted) and coin-metered spaces. Weeknight Parking: Free before 7 a.m. and after 10 p.m. Visitor passes can be purchased for $9 for the time in between. Weekend Parking: Free in all campus lots unless there is a special event. Retirees Park Free The UC Davis campus encourages retired staff and faculty to remain active within the campus community and interact with colleagues and students. Former UC Davis faculty and staff, identified by the campus Benefits Office as officially retired, are eligible for a Retiree (RT) Permit 30 days following their retirement date. Retirees may visit the TAPS office to request an RT permit, and should be prepared to present their annuitant card and driver’s license as identification. For more info, visit the TAPS website. Try the EasyPark Personal Parking Meter from TAPS The EasyPark Personal Parking Meter is a great alternative for those who do not park for very long periods of time, or do not drive to campus on a daily basis. Please check out the TAPS website for more information and availability. For any further questions concerning parking, please contact Transportation and Parking Services.

Penn’s world-class faculty facilitates research, exploration and service learning as tools for positive change. You'll have a home in one (or two) of Penn's four undergraduate schools, but you can take courses, pursue minors, conduct research, or even pursue a second degree. Penn undergraduates across all four schools fulfill their writing requirement through Critical Writing Seminars, small classes offered by the Critical Writing Program. Undergraduate Schools Students get an exceptional liberal arts and sciences foundation alongside a world-class education in nursing, engineering, and business. Interdisciplinary Programs With a menu of 4,200 courses, 14 inter-school minors, and 7 innovative dual-degree programs, Penn students cross traditional academic boundaries with ease. Our Faculty Leading voices in their fields, our professors come to Penn for the opportunity to teach and research with some of the world’s brightest undergraduates. Undergraduate Research and Learning $700+ million in research funding each year means countless opportunities for Penn undergraduates. Hubs for Innovation and Cultural Learning Penn’s learning hubs are physical and intellectual spaces for student-directed innovation and creativity. After Penn Our graduates’ lives reflect the values of their alma mater: purposeful, service oriented, and engaged.

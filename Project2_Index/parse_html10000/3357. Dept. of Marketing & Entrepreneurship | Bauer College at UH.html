Selling: Bauer is #1 for Selling. Bauer's Program for Excellence in Selling has the deepest curriculum, largest enrollment and highest corporate support of any university program in selling and sales management, and our Sales Excellence Institute is a hub for sales research and education. Entrepreneurship: Entrepreneur magazine and The Princeton Review rank Bauer's undergraduate Entrepreneurship program among the top three in the nation since 2007, and Bauer students have won more awards than any other school in national undergraduate business plan competitions in recent years.

Now You Can Help Someone Else Do the Same Since its inception in 1976, the UCR Alumni Association Scholarship Program has distributed more than $2.2 million in scholarship support, providing funds for freshmen, continuing students, re-entry students and student-athletes. Your gift provides for a better future for our students by making available additional scholarships for students who wouldn't otherwise be able to attend UCR. Help support your commitment to academic excellence by making a donation to the UCR Alumni Association Scholarship Program.

At UIC, graduate students can expect unsurpassed research opportunities across a wide spectrum of disciplines. With $335 million dollars worth of research expenditures, UIC is consistently in the top 50 US institutions for research expenditures. In all sources of research funding, UIC is third among Illinois universities.

Hours of Operation: Our office is open Monday - Thursday from 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM and Friday from 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Location: The UAC is located in Welch Hall A-220. Appointments and Walk-ins: Students can schedule 30 minute appointments Monday - Friday from 8:30 AM to 1:30 PM. The UAC does not offer same day appointments in the morning. Walk-in advising sessions are available from 2:00 PM to close. The final walk-in session will be taken approximately 30 to 45 minutes before closing, depending on advisor availability and the number of students waiting. To schedule an appointment, please call (310) 243-3538 or visit Welch Hall A-220 during regular business hours to speak with a front desk representative. To meet with an advisor during appointments or walk-ins, students must provide valid photo identification, preferably the student's CSU Dominguez Hills campus ID card. To schedule an appointment, students must provide their nine-digit CSUDH student ID number. Late Arrival Policy: Students with appointments are given a five (5) minute grace period. After five minutes, student may need to reschedule the appointment. Additional Contact Information: Click here to visit our Contact page.

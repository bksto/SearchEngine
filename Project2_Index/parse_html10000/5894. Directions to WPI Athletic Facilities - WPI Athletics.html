Directions to the Donahue Rowing Center GPS Address: 237 North Quinsigamond Avenue Shrewsbury From the WPI Parking Garage on Salisbury Street in Worcester Massachusetts, take a right onto Salisbury Street and continue onto Lincoln Street. Merge onto I-290 E via the ramp to Marlboro/Interstate 190 and take exit 22 for Main Street toward Shrewsbury. Turn left onto Main Street, than turn left onto North Quinsigamond Avenue. The Donahue Rowing Center will be on the right.
